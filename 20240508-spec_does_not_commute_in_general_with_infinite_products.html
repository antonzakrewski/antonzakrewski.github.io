spec does not commute in general with infinite products

1. Proposition

Let \(R_i\) be rings and \(\prod R_i\) the product. Then in general

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Spec}(\prod R_i) \not\simeq \coprod \mathrm{Spec}(R_i) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • prime ideal in an infinite product of rings
  • isomorphisms under functors
  • underlying-set functor
