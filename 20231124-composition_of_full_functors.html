composition of full functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{E}\) be categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{G}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{E}\) be full functors.

Then the composition \(\mathcal{G} \circ \mathcal{F}\) is also full
