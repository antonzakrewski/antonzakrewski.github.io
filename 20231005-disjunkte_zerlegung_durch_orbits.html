g-set as coproduct of orbits

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a set, \(G\) a group and \(\triangle: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a group action. Then

\begin{align*} X \cong \coprod \mathcal{O}_x \end{align*}

as G-Sets

2. Proof

Let

\begin{align*} \varphi: X \rightarrow& \coprod \mathcal{O}_x \\ x \mapsto x \end{align*}

be the set theoretic map.

By Menge der Ã„quivalenzklassen als Partition and equivalence relation of an orbit this is a Bijection. Since the category of G-Sets is a balanced category and bijective maps are bimorphisms it suffices to show, that \(\varphi\) is a G-equivariant map

This follows from

\begin{align*} \varphi(g \cdot x) =& g \cdot x \\ =& g \cdot \varphi(x) \end{align*}
