Tor functor commutes with localization

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(S \subseteq R\) a multiplicatively closed subset. Then for a module \(A\) and the Tor Functor it holds that

\begin{align*} S^{-1} \mathrm{Tor}_n^R(A,-) \cong \mathrm{Tor}_n^{S^{-1}R}(S^{-1}A, S^{-1}(-)) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • localization and tensor functor commute
  • natural isomorphism between right exact functors and isomorphism of left derived functors
