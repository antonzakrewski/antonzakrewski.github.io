graded noetherian ring and nonzero part as finitely generated module

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring.

TFAE:

  1. \(R\) is noetherian
  2. \(R_0\) is noetherian and \(R_{+}\) is a finitely generated \(R_0\)-module

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

  • first component of a noetherian graded ring as noetherian ring

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
