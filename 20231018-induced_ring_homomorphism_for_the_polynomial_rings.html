induced ring homomorphism for the polynomial rings

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be rings and \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) a ring-homomorphism. Then the induced ring homomorphism is defined as

\begin{align*} \varphi^X: A[X] \rightarrow& B[X] \\ a X^i \mapsto& \varphi(a) X^i \end{align*}
