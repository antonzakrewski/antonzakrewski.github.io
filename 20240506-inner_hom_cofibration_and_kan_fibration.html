inner hom, cofibration and kan fibration

1. Proposition

Let \(\iota: A \rightarrow B\) be a cofibration of simplicial sets, \(p: X \rightarrow Y\) a (left/right ???) kan fibration. Then for the inner hom of simplicial Sets the induced map

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}(B,X) \rightarrow \mathcal{F}(A,X) \times_{\mathcal{F}(A,Y)} \mathcal{F}(B,Y) \end{align*}
