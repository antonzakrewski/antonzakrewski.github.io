augmented tensor singular chain complex n-functor

1. Definition

Given category Top and an abelian group \(M\), the augmented singular chain complex tensor n-functor is defined as functor to the category of augmented chain complexes

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Sing}^{\mathrm{augmented}}(- \times -;M): \mathrm{Top}^n \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ch_*}^M(\mathcal{A}) \\ (X_i) \mapsto& \bigotimes_{i=1}^n \mathrm{Sing}^{\mathrm{augmented}}(X_i;M) \\ (f_i: X_i \rightarrow Y_i) \mapsto& \bigotimes_{i=1}^n \mathrm{Sing}(f_i): \bigotimes_{i = 1}^n \mathrm{Sing}^{\mathrm{augmented}}(X_i;M) \rightarrow \bigotimes_{i=1}^n \mathrm{Sing}^{\mathrm{augmented}}(Y_i;M) \end{align*}

as functor composition of

  1. the iterative tensor product of chain complexes functor using augmented chain complexes as full subcategory of normal chain complexes
  2. augmented singular chain complex functor
