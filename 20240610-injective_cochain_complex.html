injective chain complex

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes Suppose \(I_*\) is a chain complex.

TFAE:

  1. \(I_*\) is an injective object in \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\)
  2. \(I_*\) is a split exact chain complex of injectives

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
