precomposition with homotopy invariant functor as homotopy invariant functor

1. Proposition

Given category Top (resp. category Top*) a categories \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) and functors

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}: \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ \mathcal{G}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow& \mathcal{D} \\ \end{align*}

where \(\mathcal{F}\) is homotopy invariant.

Then the functor composition

\begin{align*} \mathcal{G} \circ \mathcal{F}: \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow \mathcal{D} \end{align*}

is also homotopy invariant

2. Proof

suppose \(f,g\) are homotopic maps. then by assumption

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}(f) = \mathcal{F}(g) \end{align*}

hence it follows, that

\begin{align*} \mathcal{GF}(f) =& \mathcal{G}(\mathcal{F}(f)) \\ =& \mathcal{G}(\mathcal{F}(g)) \\ =& \mathcal{GF}(g) \end{align*}
