1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(\gamma: [0,1] \rightarrow X\) a path from \(x\) to \(x'\) for \(x,x' \in X\). Then \(\gamma\) induces a natural pointed homotopy equivalence

\begin{align*} \Omega_x(X) \rightarrow \Omega_{x'}(X) \end{align*}
