restriction of an open map to an open subset as open map

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) an open map. Then for \(O \subseteq X\) open, the restriction

\begin{align*} f_{\vert O}: O \rightarrow Y \end{align*}

is also an open map
