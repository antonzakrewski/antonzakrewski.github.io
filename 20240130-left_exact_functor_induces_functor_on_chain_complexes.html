left exact functor induces functor on chain complexes

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be additive categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) be a left exact functor. Then \(\mathcal{F}\) induces a functor on the categories of chain complexes

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_*: \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{B}) \end{align*}
