transitive group action on the fibres for a covering like group action

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a topological group, \(X\) a topological space and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a topological group action Suppose \(G\) on \(X\) is covering-like, then the group action on the fibres is transitive
