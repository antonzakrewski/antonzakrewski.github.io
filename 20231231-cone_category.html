cone category

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(J: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) a diagram. Then the category of cones is defined as category containing

  1. objects \(M \in \mathcal{C}\) with morphisms \(\mu_j\)
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} & M \arrow[]{rd}{\mu_i} \arrow[swap]{ld}{\mu_j}& \\ J \arrow[]{rr}{} & & I \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
  1. a morphism $f: (M, ϕ) → (M', ϕ') $ such that
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} & M \arrow[]{d}{f} \arrow[swap, bend right]{ldd}{\varphi_i} \arrow[bend left]{rdd}{\varphi_j} & \\ & \arrow[swap]{ld}{\varphi'_i} M' \arrow[]{rd}{\varphi'_j} & \\ J \arrow[]{rr}{} & & I \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

commutes
