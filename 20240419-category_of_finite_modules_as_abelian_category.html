category of finite modules as abelian category

1. Proposition

The category of finite R-modules is an abelian category.

2. Proof

2.1. enrichment

full subcategory of an ab-enriched category as ab-enriched category

2.2. finitely bicomplete

follows as

  • finite biproducts are finite again
  • kernel and cokernel are finite again

2.3. isomorphism between image and coimage

is inherited by fullness
