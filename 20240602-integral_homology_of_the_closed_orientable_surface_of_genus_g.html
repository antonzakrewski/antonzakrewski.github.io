integral homology of the closed orientable surface of genus g

1. Proposition

Let \(\sigma_g\) be the closed orientable surface of genus \(g\) Then the singular homology is given by

\begin{align*} H_n(\Sigma_g) =& \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n = 0 \\ \mathbb{Z}^{2g} & \mbox{if } n = 1 \\ \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n = 2 \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}
