stalk of a presheaf preserves products

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) be presheaves. Then for the product of presheaves \(\mathcal{F} \times \mathcal{G}\), the stalk at \(x\) preserves products, i.e.

\begin{align*} (\mathcal{F} \times \mathcal{G})_x \simeq \mathcal{F}_x \times \mathcal{G}_x \end{align*}
