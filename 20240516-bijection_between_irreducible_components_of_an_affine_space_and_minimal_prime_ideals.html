bijection between irreducible components of an affine space and minimal prime ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space. Then there exists a natural Bijection between irreducible components and minimal prime ideal

\begin{align*} \{\mathrm{Irred component}\} \simeq& \{minimal prime ideal\} \\ T \mapsto& \mathcal{I}(T) \mathcal{Z}(\mathfrak{a}) \mapsfrom \mathfrak{a} \end{align*}
