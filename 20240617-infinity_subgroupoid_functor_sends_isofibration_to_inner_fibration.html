infinity subgroupoid functor sends isofibration to inner fibration

1. Proposition

Let \(f: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be an <inner fibration> / isofibration> between infinity categories Then the induced morphism

\begin{align*} f^{\simeq}: \mathcal{C}^{\simeq} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}^{\simeq} \end{align*}

under the maximal infinity-subgroupoid functor and the subcategory inclusion is an inner fibration
