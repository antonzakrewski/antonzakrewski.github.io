covariant ext of a ring over a group ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(G\) a group and \(R[G]\) the group ring and \(\mathrm{Ext}_{R[G]}^n(R,-)\) the Ext functor where \(R\) is seen as module over \(R[G]\) (cf. ring as module over a group ring) Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_{R[G]}^{n}(R,-) = 0 \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • covariant ext for a fixed projective module
  • ring as projective module for a group ring
