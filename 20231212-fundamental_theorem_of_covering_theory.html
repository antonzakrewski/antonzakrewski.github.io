1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a locally path connected, semi-locally simply connected topological space.

Then the functor functor from the category of coverings

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Cov}(B) \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(B), \mathrm{Set}) \end{align*}

is an adjoint equivalence

Furthermore, the functor is essentially surjective if \(B\) is semi-locally simply connected space
