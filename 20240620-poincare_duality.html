poincare duality

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional manifold and \(\sigma_{X} \in H_{n}^{\mathrm{lf}}(X;R)\) be an R-Orientation Then

\begin{align*} - \cap \sigma_{X}: H_c^{p}(X;M) \rightarrow H_{n-p}(X;M) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism for all \(p\)

  • cap product

2. Proof

Note that by poincare duality for Rn this claim holds for a chart \(U \cong \mathbb{R}^n\)

Now given two charts \(U,V\), we want to show that this claim holds for \(U \cup V\). Then by poincare duality for an open cover it remains to show that this holds for \(U \cap V\). This follows by poincare duality lemma for an open subset.

Since a manifold coverable by countably many charts we may choose \(U_i \cong \mathbb{R}^n\) and write

\begin{align*} O_n \coloneqq_{i \leq n} \bigcup U_i \end{align*}

Then the claim holds for \(O_n\) as shown above. Hence the statement follows for \(M = \bigcup O_n\)
