left simplicial set of a morphism

1. Definition

Let \(p: S \rightarrow X\) be a morphism of simplicial sets. Then the left simplicial set associated to \(p\) is defined as

\begin{align*} X_{/p}: \Delta \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ [n] \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{S/\mathrm{sSet}}(\Delta^n \star S, X) \\ (f: [n] \rightarrow [m]) \mapsto& ((f \star S)^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{S/\mathrm{sSet}}(\Delta^m \star S, X) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{S/\mathrm{sSet}}(\Delta^n \star S, X)) \end{align*}

where \(\mathrm{sSet} \downarrow X\) is the slice category, \(- \star S\) is the slice join functor and \(f^{ \rightarrow}: \Delta^n \rightarrow \Delta^m\) is a functor given by postcomposition

\begin{align*} f^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet}}(-,[n]) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet}}(-,[m]) \end{align*}
