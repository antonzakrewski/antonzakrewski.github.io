fully faithful embedding of graded modules to chain complexes

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(R = R_1 \oplus \bigoplus 0\) the trivial gradation of \(R\) and \(\mathrm{G-Mod}_R\) the category of graded modules. Then there exists a fully faithful functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ch_*}: \mathrm{G-Mod}_R \rightarrow \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod}) (M_n) \mapsto& (... \rightarrow M_{n+1} \rightarrow M_n \rightarrow ...) f: (M_n) \rightarrow (N_n) \mapsto& f_n \end{align*}

where the differential is given by the zero morphism

2. Proof

note that by definition of a graded module homomorphism the morphism \(f_n\) have welldefined codomains. Furthermore the differential as zero morphism ensures commutativitiy
