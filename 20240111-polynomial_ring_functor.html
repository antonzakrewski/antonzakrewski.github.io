polynomial ring functor

1. Definition

Given category Ring and a set \(S\), there exists a functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Polynom}(S): \mathrm{Ring} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ring} \\ R \mapsto& R[X_s \vert s \in S] \\ (\varphi: R \rightarrow S) \mapsto& (\varphi^S: R[X_s] \rightarrow S[X_s]) \end{align*}

where

\begin{align*} \varphi^S: R[X_s] \rightarrow& S[X_s] \\ \alpha \mapsto& \varphi(\alpha) x_s \mapsto& x_s \end{align*}
