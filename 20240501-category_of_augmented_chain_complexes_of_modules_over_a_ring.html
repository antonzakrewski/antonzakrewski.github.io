category of augmented chain complexes of modules

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(M\) an \(A\)-module Then the category of augmented chain complexes of modules over \(M\) is defined as category of augmented chain complexes with augmented chain complexes of modules over the ring as objects
