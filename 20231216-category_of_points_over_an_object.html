category of points over an object

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(I \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\).

Then the category of points over \(I\) is the category containing

  1. pairs of morphisms \((p: X \rightarrow I, s: I \rightarrow X)\), such that \(s\) is a section of \(p\) as objects, i.e. \(s \circ p = \mathrm{id}_{X}\)
