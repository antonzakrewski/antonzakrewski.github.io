bijection between algebraic subsets and radical ideals of the coordinate ring

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be an algebraically closed field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(V \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) an algebraic set Then there exists an order reversing Bijection

\begin{align*} \{W \vert W \subseteq V \mathrm{ algebraic subset}\} \cong \{\mathcal{J} \vert \mathcal{J} \subseteq A(V) \mathrm{ radical ideal}\} \end{align*}
