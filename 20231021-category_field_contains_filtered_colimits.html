category field contains filtered colimits

1. Proposition

the Category Field contains filtered colimits

2. Proof

Let \(\mathcal{J}: J \rightarrow \mathrm{Field}\) be a (nonempty) filtered diagram. Let the (supposed) colimit be

\begin{align*} \mathrm{cl} \coloneqq \prod_{j \in J} F_j/\sim \end{align*}

where \(\sim\) is the same equivalence relation for the direct limit of rings

2.1. welldefined

Since there exists a \(j \in J\), we conclude that \([0_j],[1_j] \in \mathrm{cl}\). Let \(a \in F_j, b \in F_l\), then there exist an upper bond \(F_k\) and thus

\begin{align*} [a] + [b] =& [\iota_{j,k}(a)] + [\iota_{l,k}(b)] \\ =& [\iota_{j,k}(a) + \iota_{l,k}(b)] \end{align*}

and thus there exists an inverse if \([a] + [b] \neq 0\)

2.2. universal property

similar to filtered colimit of rings
