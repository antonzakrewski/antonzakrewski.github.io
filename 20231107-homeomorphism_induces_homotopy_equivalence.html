homeomorphism induces homotopy equivalence

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a homeomorphism. Then \(f\) is a Homotopy equivalence

2. Proof

follows from the constant homotopy for

\begin{align*} \mathrm{id}_{g} \cong& g \circ f \\ \mathrm{id}_{Y} \cong& f \circ g \end{align*}
