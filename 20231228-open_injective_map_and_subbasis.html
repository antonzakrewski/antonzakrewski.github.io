open injective map and subbasis

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a set-theoretic map. Suppose \(\mathcal{S}\) is a Subbasis

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is an open map
  2. for \(U \in \mathcal{S}\), the image \(f[U] \subseteq Y\) is open

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

special case

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from

  1. image and union
  2. injective image and intersection

where the intersections and unions commutes, hence finite intersections and arbitrary unions commute.
