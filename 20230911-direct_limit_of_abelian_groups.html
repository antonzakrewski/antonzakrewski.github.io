direct limit of abelian groups

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{Grp}\) be the category of abelian groups and \(F: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be a filtered diagram. Then the direct limit of \(F\) is

\begin{align*} G \coloneqq (\coprod_{i \in J} G_i)/~ \end{align*}

where

\begin{align*} (g,i) \sim (h,j) :\Leftrightarrow \exists k \in J f_{ik}(g) = f_j{jk}(h) \end{align*}

and multiplication is defined

\begin{align*} [(g,i)][(h,j)] = [f_{ik}(g)f_{jk}(h)] \end{align*}
