cap product and cup product

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(\alpha \in H^p(X;R), \beta \in H^{q}(X;R)\) and \(s \in H^n(X;R)\) be elements of the singular homology and singular cohomology then the cap product and cup product result in

\begin{align*} (\alpha \cup \beta) \cap x = \alpha \cap (\beta \cap x) \end{align*}
