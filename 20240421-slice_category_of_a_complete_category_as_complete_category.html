slice category of a complete category as complete category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a (sufficiently) complete category, \(X \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) an object Then the slice category \(\mathcal{C} / X\) is also complete.

2. Proof

follows dually to coslice category of a cocomplete category as cocomplete category
