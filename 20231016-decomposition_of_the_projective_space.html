decomposition of the projective Space

1. Proposition / heuristic

Given a field \(F\)

\begin{align*} \mathbb{P}^n = \bigsqcup_{i = 0}^n \mathbb{A}^n \end{align*}

where \(\mathbb{A}^n\) is the affine space over \(F\)

2. Proof

follows inductively

\begin{align*} \mathbb{P}^n =& \{[\alpha_0,...,\alpha_n] \vert \alpha_n = 0\} \sqcup \{[\alpha_0,...,\alpha_n] \vert \alpha_n \neq 0\} \\ \cong& \mathbb{A}^{n} \sqcup \mathbb{P}^{n-1} \end{align*}

where there is the welldefined map

\begin{align*} [\alpha_0,...,\alpha_n] \rightarrow& (\frac{\alpha_1}{\alpha_0},...,\frac{\alpha_n}{\alpha_0}) \end{align*}

for \(\alpha_0 \neq 0\)
