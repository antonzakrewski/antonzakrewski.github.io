1. Proposition

Let \(\Sigma_g\) be a orientable surface of genus \(g\), which is <triangulated by vertices, edges and triangles. Then it holds, that

\begin{align*} 2 - 2 g = \vert \text{vertices} \vert - \vert \text{edges} \vert + \vert \text{triangles} \vert \end{align*}
