1. Rechnung

1.1. Ziel

Berechnung eines Vektor mit rechtem Winkel zu 2 gegebenen Vektoren

\begin{align*} \vec{a} \circ \vec{c} = 0 \\ \vec{b} \circ \vec{c} = 0 \end{align*}

1.2. Folgerung

\begin{align*} 0 =& xa_1 + ya_2 + za_3 \\ 0 =& xb_1 + yb_2 + zb_3 \\ xa_1 =& -(ya_2 + za_3) && \vert \cdot b_2 \\ xa_1b_2 =& b_2 (ya_2 + za_3) \\ -xb_1 =& yb_2 + zb_3 && \vert \cdot a_2 \\ -xb_1a_2 =& a_2 (yb_2 + zb_3) \\ x (a_1b_2 - b_1a_2) =& z (a_2b_3 - a_3b_2) \\ z \coloneqq& (a_1b_2 - b_1a_2) \\ \Rightarrow x =& a_2b_3 -a_3b_2 \\ \end{align*}

1.3. Analog

\begin{align*} 0 =& xa_1 + ya_2 + za3 \\ 0 =& xb_1 + yb_2 + zb3 \\ ya_2 =& -(xa_1 + za_3) \\ -yb_2 =& xb_1 + zb_3 \\ yb_1a_2 =& - b_1 (xa_1 + za_3) \\ -ya_1b_2 =& a_1 (xb_1 + zb_3) \\ y (b_1a_2 - a_1b_2) =& z (a_1b_3 - a_3b_1) \\ z =& (a_1b_2 - b_1a_2) \\ y =& a_3b_1 - a_1b_3 \end{align*}
