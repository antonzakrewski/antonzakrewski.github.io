zero functor coeffacable

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(0: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) the zero functor. Then \(0\) is coeffacable

2. Proof

follows from \(\mathrm{id}: A \rightarrow A\)
