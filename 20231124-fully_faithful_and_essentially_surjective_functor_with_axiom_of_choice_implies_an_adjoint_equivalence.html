1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a fully faithful essentially surjective functor Then assuming the Axiom of choice, \(\mathcal{F}\) induces an adjoint equivalence

2. Proof
