RMod admits enough injectives

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod Then \(\mathrm{RMod}\) admits enough injectives

2. Proof

  • hom into rational circle group as injective module
  • co-extension for the rational circle group as cogenerator
  • category RMod as complete category
  • injective cogenerator with products implies enough injectives
