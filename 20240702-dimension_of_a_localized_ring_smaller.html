dimension of a localized ring smaller

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(S \subseteq A\) a multiplicatively closed subset Then the dimension

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dim}(A) \geq \mathrm{dim}(S^{-1}A) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • correspondence theorem for prime ideals and localization
