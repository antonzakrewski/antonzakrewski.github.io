dihedral group

1. Definition

Let \(n \in \mathbb{N}\), then the \(n\)-th dihedral group is defined as

\begin{align*} \langle \{r,s\} \vert r^n, s^2, rsrs \rangle \end{align*}

see:

  • presentation of a group

Note that

\begin{align*} rsrs =& 1 \\ rsrs^{-1} =& 1 \\ rsr =& s \\ rs =& sr^{-1} \end{align*}
