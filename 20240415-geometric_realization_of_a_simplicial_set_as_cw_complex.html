geometric realization of a simplicial set as cw complex

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a simplicial set. Then the geometric realization of \(X\) canonically is a CW-Complex

2. Proof

follows from

  • n+1 skeleton as pushout
  • simplicial set as colimit over skeleta
