minimal prime ideal over an ideal

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{a}\) an ideal. Then a prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}\) is said to be minimal over \(\mathfrak{a}\), if \(\overline{\mathfrak{p}} \subseteq A/\mathfrak{a}\) is a minimal prime ideal
