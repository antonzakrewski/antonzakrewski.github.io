locally finite homology and orientability

1. Propositionb

Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional manifold, \(H_n^{\mathrm{lf}}(X)\) the locally finite homology

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is orientable
  2. the factorization \(\rho^{\mathrm{lf}}\) is a natural isomorphism
  3. \(H_n^{\mathrm{lf}}(X) \cong \mathbb{Z}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

it suffices to show that \(\rho^{\mathrm{lf}}\) is objectwise an iso (cf. natural isomorphism and isomorphism on the objects)

2.1.1. surjective

Choose an R-Orientation orientation \(\sigma_{X} = (\sigma_{X,K}) \in H_n^{\mathrm{lf}}(X)\). Then the projection map to generators, hence it is surjective

2.1.2. injective

section determined by stalks

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

special case

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
