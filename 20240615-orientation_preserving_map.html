orientation preserving map

1. Definition

Let \((M,\sigma),(N,\tau)\) be \(n\)-dimensional manifolds with orientations \(\sigma, \tau\) and \(f: M \rightarrow N\) a continuous map Then \(f\) is said to preserve the orinetation if for each \(m \in M\) and the induced map on local singular homology

\begin{align*} p: H_n(M,M \setminus \{m\}) \mapsto H_n(N, N \setminus \{f(m)\}) \end{align*}

is is compatible with the elements

\begin{align*} \sigma_x(1) \mapsto \tau_{p(x)}(1) \end{align*}
