maximal ideal over a field in general not a point

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a (non algebraically closed) field, \(K[X_1,...,X_n]\) the polynomial ring and \(\mathfrak{m}\) a maximal ideal. Then in general

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{m} \neq (X_i - \alpha_i \vert i \in \{0,...,n\}) \end{align*}

for suitable \(\alpha_i\)
