fibration of points

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(\mathrm{Pt}(\mathcal{C})\) be the category of points. Then the fibration of points is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \Pi: \mathrm{Pt}(\mathcal{C}) \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ (p: X \leftrightarrow I: s) \mapsto& I \\ ((u,v): (p: X \leftrightarrow I: s) \leftrightarrow (q: Y \leftrightarrow J: t)) \mapsto& (v: I \rightarrow J) \\ \end{align*}

where \(s: I \rightarrow X\) is the domain of the section
