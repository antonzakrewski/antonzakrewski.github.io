relative singular homology commutes with coproducts

1. Proposition

Let \(X_i,A_i\) be pairs of spaces and

\begin{align*} H_n(X_i, A_i) \end{align*}

the relative singular homology functor

Then it holds, that

\begin{align*} H_n( \coprod (X_i, A_i)) \simeq \oplus H_n(X_i, A_i) \end{align*}

\begin{align*} \mathrm{coker}(C_n(\coprod A_i) \rightarrow C_n(\coprod X_i)) \simeq& \mathrm{coker}(\bigoplus C_n(A_i) \rightarrow C_n(X_i)) \\ \simeq& \bigoplus \mathrm{coker}(C_n(A_i) \rightarrow C_n(X_i)) \end{align*}
