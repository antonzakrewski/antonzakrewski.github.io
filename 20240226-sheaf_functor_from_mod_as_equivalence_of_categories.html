sheaf functor from Mod as equivalence of categories

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(X\) the affine scheme

\begin{align*} \tilde{}: \mathrm{AMod} \rightarrow \mathcal{O}_X-\mathrm{Mod} M \mapsto& \tilde{M} \end{align*}

the sheaf functor from Mod

Then \(\tilde{}\) is an equivalence of categories

2. Proof

using adjoint equivalence of categories and fully faithful and essentially surjective functor and sheaf functor from Mod as fully faithful functor it remains to show, that \(\tilde{}\) is essentially surjective

This follows from quasi coherent sheaf of modules and affine part as associated sheaf
