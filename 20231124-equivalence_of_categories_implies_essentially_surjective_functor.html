equivalence of categories implies essentially surjective functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be an equivalence Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is essentially surjective
