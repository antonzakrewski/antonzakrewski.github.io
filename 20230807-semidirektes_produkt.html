semidirect product

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(G,H\) be groups, \(\varphi: H \rightarrow \mathrm{Aut}(G)\) a homomorphism between \(G\) and the automorphism group. Then the semidirect product \(G \rtimes_{\varphi} H\) is defined as group \(G \times H\) with the operation

\begin{align*} (g_1,h_1) * (g_2,h_2) =& (g_1 \left(\varphi(h_1)\right)(g_2), h_1 \cdot h_2) \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. neutral element

Let \(e \in G,H\), then

\begin{align*} (g_1,h_1) * (e,e) =& (g_1 \varphi(h_1)(e), h_1 \cdot e) \\ =& (g_1 \cdot e, h_i) \\ =& (g_1,h_1) \\ (e,e) * (g_1,h_1) =& (e \circ \varphi(e)(g_1), e \cdot h_1) \\ =& (e \circ \mathrm{id}(g_1), h_1) \\ =& (g_1,h_1) \end{align*}

2.2. inverse element

Let \((g_1,h_1) \in G \rtimes_{\varphi} H\). By assumption, \(\varphi(h_i)\) is an automorphism, so there exists an \(g_2 \in G\) such that \(g_1^{-1} = \varphi(h_1)(g_2)\). Therefore

\begin{align*} (g_1, h_1) * (g_2, h_1^{-1}) = (g_1 \cdot \varphi(h_1)(g_2), h_1 \cdot h_1^{-1}) \\ =& (g_1 \cdot g_1^{-1}, h_1 \cdot h_1^{-1}) \\ =& (e,e) \end{align*}

Furthermore, \(\varphi(h_1^{-1}) = \varphi(h_1)^{-1}\) and hence

\begin{align*} g_1^{-1} =& \varphi(h_1)(g_2) && \vert \varphi(h_1)^{-1} \\ \varphi(h_1)^{-1}(g_1^{-1}) =& g_2 && \vert ()^{-1} \\ \varphi(h_1)^{-1}(g_1) =& g_2^{-1} \end{align*}

Thus

\begin{align*} (g_2,h_1^{-1}) * (g_1,h_1) =& (g_2 \varphi(h_1^{-1})(g_1), h_1^{-1} h_1) \\ =& (g_2 \cdot g_2^{-1}, h_1^{-1} h_1 ) \\ =& (e,e) \end{align*}

2.3. associative

For \((g_1,h_1), (g_2,h_2), (g_3,h_3)\) it holds

\begin{align*} ((g_1, h_1) * (g_2,h_2)) * (g_3, h_3) =& (g_1 (\varphi(h_1))(g_2), h_1 \cdot h_2) * (g_3, h_3) \\ =& ( \left( g_1 \varphi(h_1)(g_2) \right) \cdot \varphi(h_1 \cdot h_2)(g_3), \left(h_1 \cdot h_2 \right) \cdot h_3) \\ =& ( g_1 \varphi(h_1)(g_2) \cdot \varphi(h_1)(g_3) \cdot \varphi(h_2)(g_3), h_1 \cdot h_2 \cdot h_3) \\ =& (g_1 \varphi(h_1)(g_2 \varphi(h_2)(g_3)), h_1 \cdot h_2 \cdot h_3) \\ =& (g_1,h_1) * (g_2 \varphi(h_2)(g_3), h_2 \cdot h_3) \\ =& (g_1,h_1) * ((g_2,h_2) * (g_3, h_3)) \end{align*}
