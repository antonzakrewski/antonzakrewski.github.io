module as filtered colimit of finitely generated submodules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module Then \(M\) is a filtered colimit of finitely generated modules

2. Proof

Consider the diagram with inclusions as morphisms and finitely generated submodules as objects
