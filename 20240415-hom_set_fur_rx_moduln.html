hom set für RX moduln

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(M,N\) \(R\)-modules and \(A_M \in \mathrm{End}_{R}(M), A_n \in \mathrm{End}_{R}(N)\). Then for \(M,N\) as Modul als Modul über dem Polynomring Then the hom-set is given by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{R[X]}(M,N) = \{f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{R}(M,N) \vert f \cdot A_m = A_n \cdot f\} \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. \(\subseteq\)

Note that \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{R[X]}(M,N)\) is also a module homomorphism over \(R\). Furthermore it holds that

\begin{align*} f(A_m \cdot m) =& f(X \cdot m) \\ =& X \cdot f(m) \\ =& A_n \cdot f(m) \end{align*}

2.2. \(\supseteq\)

Let \(f \in \{f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{R}(M,N) \vert f \cdot A_m = A_n \cdot f\}\) then it remains to show that \(f\) is an \(R[X]\)-module homomorphism. For \(r \cdot X\) we get

\begin{align*} f(r \cdot X \cdot m) =& f(r \cdot A_m \cdot m) \\ =& r \cdot f(A_m \cdot m) \\ =& r \cdot A_n \cdot f(m) \\ =& r \cdot X \cdot f(m) \end{align*}

and furthermore

\begin{align*} f((r_1 X^i + r_2 X^j) \cdot m) =& f(r_1 X^i \cdot m + r_2 X^j \cdot m) \\ =& f(r_1 A_m^i \cdot m + r_2 A_m^j \cdot m) \\ =& f(r_1 A_m^i \cdot m) + f(r_2 A_m^j \cdot m) \\ =& r_1 \cdot X^i \cdot f(m) + r_2 \cdot X^j \cdot f(m) \\ \end{align*}
