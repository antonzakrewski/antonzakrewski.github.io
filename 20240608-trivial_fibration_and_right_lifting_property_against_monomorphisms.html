trivial fibration and right lifting property against monomorphisms

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{sSet}\) be the category sSet and \(f: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a morphism of simplicial sets

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is a trivial fibration
  2. \(f\) has the right lifting property against monomorphisms

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of:

  • set with left lifting property as saturated set
  • monomorphisms as saturated closure of boundary inclusion

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
