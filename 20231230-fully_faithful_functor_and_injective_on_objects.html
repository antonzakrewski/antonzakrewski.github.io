fully faithful functor and injective on objects

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a fully faithful functor. Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is already sufficiently injective on objects, i.e. for

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}(X) = \mathcal{F}(X') \Rightarrow X \cong X' \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • full conservative functor and isomorphic objects
  • fully faithful functor as conservative functor
