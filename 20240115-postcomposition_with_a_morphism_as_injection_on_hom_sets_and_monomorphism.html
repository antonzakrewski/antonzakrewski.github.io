postcomposition with a morphism as injection on hom sets and monomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a locally small category, \(A,B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) and \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,A), \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,B)\) be the contravariant hom-functor and \(f: A \rightarrow B\) a morphism TFAE:

  1. each components of the natural transformation \(f^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,A) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-,B)\) is injective
  2. \(f\) is a monomorphism
