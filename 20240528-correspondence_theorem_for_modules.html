correspondence theorem for quotient modules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(N\) a submodule Then there exists a natural correspondence

\begin{align*} \{submodules A \supseteq N\} \cong \{submodules \overline{A} \subseteq M/N\} \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from

  1. quotient module
  2. preimage of a submodule as submodule

and correspondence theorem for groups / correspondence theorem for subgroups and quotient rings
