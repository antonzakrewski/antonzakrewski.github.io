right exact functor preserves zero morphisms

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be finitely cocomplete, pointed categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a right exact functor Then \(\mathcal{F}\) preserves the zero morphism

2. Proof

dual to left exact functor preserves zero morphisms
