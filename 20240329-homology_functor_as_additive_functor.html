homology functor as additive functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Then the homology functor

\begin{align*} H_n: \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathcal{A} \end{align*}

is an additive functor

2. Proof

follows from

  • additive functor and biproduct
  • colimits commute with colimits
  • limits commute with limits
