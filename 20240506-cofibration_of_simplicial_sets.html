cofibration of simplicial sets

1. Definition

Let \(\iota: A \rightarrow X\) be a morphism of simplicial sets \(A,X\). Then \(\iota\) is said to be a cofibration, if the boxtensor has the left lifting property against kan fibrations \(X \rightarrow \Delta^0\)

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \times \Delta^1 \coprod_{A \times \{0\}} B \times \{0\} \arrow[]{d}{\iota \boxtimes ( \{0\} \hookrightarrow \Delta^1)} \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[]{d}{} & X \arrow[]{d}{}\\ B \times \Delta^1 \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[dashed]{ru}{}& \Delta^0 \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
