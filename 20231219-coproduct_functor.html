coproduct functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, where \(\mathcal{D}\) admits (enough) coproducts and \(\mathcal{F}_i\) be functors. Then the coproduct functor is

\begin{align*} \coprod \mathcal{F}_i: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow& \mathcal{D} \\ A \rightarrow \coprod \mathcal{F}_i(A) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • colimit of functors for a cocomplete codomain
