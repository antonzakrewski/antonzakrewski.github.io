universal cover as nonempty cover

1. Proposition

Let \(B,E\) be topological spaces and

\begin{align*} p: E \rightarrow B \end{align*}

a universal cover Then \(p\) is a nonempty covering.

2. Proof

Note that by Yoneda by natural transformations from Hom-Sets to a presheaf, a natural transformation is given by \(b \in \mathrm{Fib}_b(p: E \rightarrow B) = \mathrm{Fib}_b^p\).
