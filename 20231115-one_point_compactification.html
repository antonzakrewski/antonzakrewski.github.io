one point compactification

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space. Then the one point compactification of \(X\) is defined as strong compactification \(\iota: X \rightarrow X^+\), such that

\begin{align*} \vert X^+ \setminus \iota[X] \vert < 1 \end{align*}
