euler characteristic of a closed connected oriented 4n+2 manifold

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a closed connected \(4n+2\)-dimensional manifold. Then the euler characteristic is even.

2. Proof

using the fp betti number duality of an even dimensional orientable compact manifold (f2 betti number duality of an even dimensional compact manifold) it suffices to show that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dim}(H_n(X, \mathbb{F}_p)) \equiv 0 \mod{2} \end{align*}

As \(\mathrm{dim}(H_{n-p}) = \mathrm{dim}(H_{n+1})\)

Suppose \(\mathrm{dim}(H_(X, \mathbb{F}_p))\) is odd, then using the intersection form skew symmetric for odd n we conclude that

\begin{align*} I(x,x) = - I(x,x) \end{align*}

showing \(I(x,x) = 0\) for all \(x \in H_n(X)\) since \(\mathrm{char}(\mathbb{F}_p) \neq 2\) for \(p \neq 2\)
