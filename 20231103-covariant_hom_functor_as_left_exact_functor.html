covariant hom-functor as left-exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a finitely complete category. Then the covariant hom functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,-): \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set} \end{align*}

is a covariant left exact functor

2. Proof

follows from:

  • Set as complete category
  • left exact functor
  • covariant left exact functor and exact sequence
  • covariant hom-functor preserves small limits
