existence of a minimal prime Ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathfrak{p}\) a prime ideal. Then there exists a minimal prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}_m \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\), such that for \(\mathfrak{q} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\) with \(\mathfrak{q}\) as prime ideal, it holds

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{q} = \mathfrak{p} \end{align*}
