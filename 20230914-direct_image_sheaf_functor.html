direct image sheaf functor

1. Definition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a continuous map. Suppose \(\mathcal{C}\) is a complete category and \(\mathrm{Sh}(X), \mathrm{Sh}(Y)\) the category of sheaves.

The direct image image sheaf functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \iota_*: \mathrm{Sh}(Y) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Sh}(X) \\ \mathcal{F} \mapsto& \iota^{ \leftarrow} \circ \mathcal{F} \end{align*}

where \(\iota^{ \leftarrow}\) is the contravariant functor from Category of Top to Cat on open Subsets
