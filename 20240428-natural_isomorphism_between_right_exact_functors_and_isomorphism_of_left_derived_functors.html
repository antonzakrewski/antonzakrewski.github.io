natural isomorphism between right exact functors and isomorphism of left derived functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) right exact functors and \(\eta: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) a natural isomorphism. Then the left derived functors \(\mathcal{LF}_n, \mathcal{GF}_n\) are uniquely isomorphic (cf. morphism of delta functors)

2. Proof

follows from

  • natural transformation between right exact functors and morphism of left derived functors
  • universal property unique up to unique isomorphism
