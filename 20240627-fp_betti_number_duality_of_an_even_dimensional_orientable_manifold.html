f2 betti number duality of an even dimensional compact manifold

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a compact \(2n\)-dimensional manifold Then the betti number for f2 is symmetric, i.e.

\begin{align*} \mathrm{betti}_{n-k}(M,\mathbb{F}_2) = \mathrm{betti}_{n+k}(M, \mathbb{F}_2) \end{align*}

2. Proof

using:

  • existence of a unique F2 orientation
  • poincare duality for a closed manifold / poincare duality for a compact manifold with boundary

There exists an isomorphism

\begin{align*} H^{n-k}(M, \mathbb{F}_2) \simeq H_{n+k}(M, \mathbb{F}_2) \end{align*}

Using the universal coefficient theorem for singular homology and Dimension eines Dualraums we conclude that

\begin{align*} H^{n-k}(M) \cong 0 \oplus \mathrm{Hom}(H_{n-k}(M),\mathbb{F}_2) \cong H_{n-k}(M) \end{align*}
