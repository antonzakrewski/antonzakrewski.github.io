relative homology of a manifold

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological manifold of dimension \(n > 0\) and \(x \in X\). Then the singular homology

\begin{align*} H_k(X, X \setminus \{*\}, M) = \begin{cases} M & \mbox{if } k = n \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let \(U\) be a coordinate chart with \(U \simeq \mathbb{R}^n\) Then excision of homology gives an isomorphism

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} H_k(X,X \setminus \{x\}) & \arrow[]{l}{\simeq} H_k(U,U \setminus \{x\}) \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & H_k(\mathbb{R}^n, \mathbb{R}^n \setminus \{0\}) \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
