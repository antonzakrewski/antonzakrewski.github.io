coslice category of a complete category as complete category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a complete category, \(X \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) an object and \(X \ \mathcal{C}\) the coslice category Then \(X \ \mathcal{C}\) is also complete
