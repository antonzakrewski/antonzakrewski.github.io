join of represented simplicial sets

1. Proposition

Let \(n,m \in \mathbb{N}\), then we get for the represented simplicial sets \(\Delta^n,\Delta^m\) and the join \(\star\)

\begin{align*} \Delta^n \star \Delta^m \simeq \Delta^{n + 1 + m} \end{align*}

2. Proof

using represented simplicial set as nerve and nerve commutes with join, we get

\begin{align*} \Delta^n \star \Delta^m \simeq& N([n]) \star N([m]) \\ \simeq& N([n] \star [m]) \end{align*}

then it follows from join of finite linear ordered sets, that

\begin{align*} [n] \star [m] \simeq [n + m + 1] \end{align*}

and hence we conclude

\begin{align*} N([n] \star [m]) \simeq& N([n + m + 1]) \\ \simeq& \Delta^{n+m+1} \end{align*}
