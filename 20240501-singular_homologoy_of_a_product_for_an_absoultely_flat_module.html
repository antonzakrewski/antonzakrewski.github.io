singular homologoy of a product for an absolutely flat, hereditary Ring

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{Top}\) be the category Top, \(R\) an absolutely flat, hereditary ring. Then the map out of the tensor of homology to the homology of the tensor

\begin{align*} H_n(- \times -;R) \simeq& H_n(-;R) \otimes_R H_n(-;R) \end{align*}

is a natural isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of

  • tor for an absolutely flat ring
  • kunneth formula for singular homology
  • sandwhiched by zero object and isomorphism
