limit functor preserves monomorphisms

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(I,J: \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be diagrams, such that their limits exist, and \(\eta: I \rightarrow J\) a natural transformation containing only monomorphisms Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{lim}(\eta) \end{align*}

is also a monomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • limits commute with limits
  • functor preserving pullbacks preserves monomorphisms
  • pullback as limit
