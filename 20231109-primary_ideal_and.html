primary ideal and similar definition

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{q}\) an ideal.

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{q}\) is primary
  2. \(\forall x, y \in A : x y \in \mathfrak{q} \Rightarrow x \in \mathfrak{q} \lor y \sqrt{\mathfrak{q}}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

weaker condition

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from the symmetry: suppose \(x \cdot y \in \mathfrak{q}\), w.l.o.g. \(x,y \not\in \mathfrak{q}\), then

\begin{align*} x \not\in \mathfrak{q} \Rightarrow& y \in \sqrt{\mathfrak{q}} \\ y \not\in \mathfrak{q} \Rightarrow& x \in \sqrt{\mathfrak{q}} \\ \end{align*}
