weak homotopy equivalence

1. Definition

Let \((X,x_0), (Y,y_0)\) be based spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a based map.

Then \(f\) is said to be a weak homotopy equivalence, if for all \(n \geq 0\) and the homotopy group functor \(\pi_n: \mathrm{Top}_* \rightarrow \mathrm{Grp}\)

\begin{align*} \pi_n(f): \pi_n(X,x_0) \rightarrow \pi_n(Y,y_0) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism in category Group resp. category set for \(\pi_0\)
