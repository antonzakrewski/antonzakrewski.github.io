length of a coresolution

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, \(M \rightarrow I_*\) a coresolution Then the length is defined as length of the resolution \(I_*^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow M^{\mathrm{op}}\) in the opposite category
