affine covering of a projective space

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathbb{P}K^n\) the projective space equipped with the zariski topology on the projective space. Then the affine covering is an open covering defined as

\begin{align*} U_i \coloneqq \{ [\alpha_0,...,\alpha_n] \subseteq \mathbb{P}K^n \vert \alpha_i \neq 0\} \subseteq \mathbb{P}K^n \end{align*}

2. Proof

\(\mathbb{P}K^n \setminus U_i = V(x_i)\)
