birational varieties

1. Definition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field. Two affine varieties \(V,W\) are said to be biration, if they are isomorphic in category of affine varieties and dominant maps, \(\mathrm{AVar}\)

2. Explicite

if there exists dominant birational maps such that

\begin{align*} f \circ g =& \mathrm{id} \\ g \circ f =& \mathrm{id} \end{align*}

on their domains
