semi locally simply connected space and open covering of semi locally simply connected spaces

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space.

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is a semi-locally simply connected space
  2. there exists an open cover of \(X\), where each \(O_i\) is semi locally simply connected

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

taking \(X\) as cover suffices

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Let \(x \in X\), then by assumption there exists an \(O_i\) with \(x \in O_i\). Then choose an open neighbourhood \(V \subesteq V_i\), such that every loop inside \(V_i\) is nullhomotopic in \(O_i\)

Then \(V_i\) suffices for \(X\), as taking the homotopy \(H: [0,1]^2 \rightarrow O_i \hookrightarrow X\) does the job.

Note that \(V \subseteq X\) is an open neighbour (cf. by openness in a subspace)
