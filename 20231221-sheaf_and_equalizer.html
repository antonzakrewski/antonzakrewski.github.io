sheaf on a complete category

1. Definition

Let be a category containing (sufficiently many) products, a topological space and

a presheaf

Then is said to be a sheaf, if for an open cover , the products and the morphisms

form an equalizer, where the morphisms are defined by the universal property using

monomorphism
