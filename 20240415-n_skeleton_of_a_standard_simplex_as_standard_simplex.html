n skeleton of a n standard simplex as standard simplex

1. Proposition

Let \(\Delta^n\) be the represented simplicial set, \(\mathrm{sk}_n\) the n skeleton functor. Then it holds

\begin{align*} \mathrm{sk}_n(\Delta^n) \simeq \Delta^n \end{align*}
