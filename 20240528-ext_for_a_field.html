ext over a field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, then for \(n \geq 1\) the Ext functor is given by the zero functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_n^K(-,-) = 0 \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows \(\mathrm{Hom}_{K}(-,-)\) is exact, since

  • vector space as projective module
  • additive functor preserves split exact sequences
  • projective module and split exact sequence
