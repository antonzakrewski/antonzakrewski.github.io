classification of 0-ring algebras

1. Proposition

Let \(0\) be the zero ring and \(S\) an 0-Algebra. Then \(S \cong 0\)

2. Proof

corollary of zero as unit and zero ring, as by definition there exists a ring-homomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: 0 \rightarrow S \end{align*}

with

\begin{align*} 1_S =& \varphi(1_0) \\ =& \varphi(0_0) \\ =& 0_S \end{align*}
