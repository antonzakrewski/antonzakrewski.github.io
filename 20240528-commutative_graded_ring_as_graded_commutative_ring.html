commutative graded ring as graded-commutative ring for a stretch

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring. Then there exists an isomorphism of categories between

  1. the category of commutative graded Algebras
  2. the full subcategory containing commutative graded R-Algebra of the category of graded-commutative Algebras
