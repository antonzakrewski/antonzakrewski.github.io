natural isomorphism and preserved colimit

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be functors and \(\eta: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) a natural isomorphism. Let \(I: \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be a diagram TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}\) preserves \(I\) as colimit
  2. \(\mathcal{G}\) preserves \(I\) as colimit

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

followf from the symmetry of the argument
