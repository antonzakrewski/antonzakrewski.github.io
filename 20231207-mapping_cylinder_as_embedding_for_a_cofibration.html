mapping cylinder as embedding for a cofibration

1. Proposition

Let \((X,A)\) be topological spaces, such that \(\iota: A \rightarrow X\) is a cofibration. Then the map

\begin{align*} i: \mathrm{Cyl}(i) \rightarrow& X \times [0,1] \\ \end{align*}

is an topological embedding

2. Proof

\begin{align*} q: A \times [0,1] \cup X \times \{0\} \rightarrow \mathrm{Cyl}(i) \end{align*}

\begin{align*} g: \mathrm{Cyl}(i) \rightarrow& X \times [0,1] \\ q(x,0) \mapsto& (x,0) \\ q(a,t) \mapsto& (\iota(a), t) \end{align*}
