composition of based mapping spaces as continuous map

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0), (Y,y_0), (Z,z_0)d\) be based spaces, where \(Y\) is locally compact and \(\mathrm{Map}_*(X,Y), \mathrm{Map}_*(Y,Z), \mathrm{Map}_*(X,Z)\) Based Mapping space with the compact open topology. Then composition

\begin{align*} \sigma: \mathrm{Map}_*(Y,Z) \times \mathrm{Map}_*(X,Y) \rightarrow \mathrm{Map}_*(X,Z) \end{align*}

is a continuous map

2. Proof

2.1. welldefined

follows from

\begin{align*} f \circ g(*) =& f(*) \\ =& * \end{align*}

for the base point \(*\)

2.2. continuous

follows from continuous composition for locally compact spaces on the compact open topology and restriction of a continuous map as continuous map / continuous map and restriction to the image
