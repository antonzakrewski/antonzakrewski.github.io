1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a locally and globally path-connected topological space. Let \(b \in B\) and consider the fundamental group \(\pi(B,b)\) as G-Set over itself and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fib}_b: \end{align*}

the topological fibre Set functor

TFAE:

  1. \(\pi(B,b)\) is in the image of the functor
  2. there exists a simply connected covering \(\pi: \tilde{B} \rightarrow B\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
