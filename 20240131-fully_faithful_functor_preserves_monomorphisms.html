fully faithful, essentially surjective functor preserves monomorphisms

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a fully faithful functor Then \(\mathcal{F}\) preserves monomorphisms

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • monomorphism and pullback
  • fully faithful and essentially surjective functor with Axiom of Choice implies an adjoint equivalence
  • right adjoint preserves pullbacks
