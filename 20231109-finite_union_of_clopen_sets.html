finite union of clopen sets

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(C_1,...,C_n\) be clopen. Then the union

\begin{align*} \bigcap_{i=1}^n C_i \end{align*}

is also clopen

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • finite union of closed sets
  • Openness of a union of open sets
