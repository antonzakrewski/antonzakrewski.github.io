group structure on the higher homotopy group

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space, then there exists the group structures

\begin{align*} g \star_i g: I^n \rightarrow X (t_1,...,t_n) \mapsto& \begin{cases} f(t_1,...,2t_i,...,t_n) & \mbox{if } t_i \in [0,\frac{1}{2}] \\ g(t_1,...,2t_i - 1,...,t_n - 1) & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

note that by Eckmann-Hilton Argument they all induce the same group structure
