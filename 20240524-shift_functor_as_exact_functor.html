shift functor as exact functor

1. Proposition

Let be an abelian category and be the category of chain complexes Then the shift functor of chain complexes is an exact functor

2. Proof

corollary of exact chain complex where an short exact sequence

is exact if and only if it is degreewise exact and applying the shift preserves this degreewise exactness
