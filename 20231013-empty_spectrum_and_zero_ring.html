empty spectrum and zero ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring.

TFAE:

  1. \(A = 0\) is the zero ring
  2. \(\mathrm{Spec}(A) = \emptyset\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

by assumption, \(0 \in \mathfrak{a}\) for an ideal \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq A\). But \((0)\) is not a strict ideal

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

  1. maximal ideal and non-unit
  2. maximal ideal as prime ideal
  3. zero as unit and zero ring

follows from

as zero is a unit by 1,2 and by 3) we have the zero ring
