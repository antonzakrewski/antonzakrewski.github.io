contravariant ext functor for RMod dualizes coproducts

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(M\) an \(R\)-module Then the Ext functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_{R}^{n}(-,M) \end{align*}

dualizes coproducts to products

2. Proof

  • category RMod opp as AB4 category
  • contravariant ext functor dualizes coproducts
