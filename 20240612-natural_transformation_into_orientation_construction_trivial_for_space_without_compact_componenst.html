natural transformation into orientation construction trivial for space without compact components

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional manifold without compact component. Then the morphism (given by natural transformation into orientation construction )

\begin{align*} \rho_x: H_n(X;M) \rightarrow H_n(X, X \setminus \{x\};M) \end{align*}

is trivial for arbitrayr \(x \in X\)
