injective resolution and enough injectives

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category. TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{A}\) admits enough injectives
  2. each object \(A\) in \(\mathcal{A}\) admits an injective coresolution

2. Proof

follows dually from projective resolution and enough projectives
