sheafification and stalks

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{V}\) a category and \(\mathcal{F}\) a V-valued presheaf. Then for the sheafification \(\mathcal{F}^+\) and the stalks at \(x \in X\), it holds that

\begin{align*} (\mathcal{F}^+)_x \cong \mathcal{F}_x \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • sheafification as left adjoint
  • left adjoint preserves colimits
