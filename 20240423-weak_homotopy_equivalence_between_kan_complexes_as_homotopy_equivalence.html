weak homotopy equivalence between Kan complexes as homotopy equivalence

1. Proposition

Let \(K,L\) be Kan complexes and \(f: K \rightarrow L\) a morphism of simplicial sets.

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is a weak equivalence between simplicial sets
  2. \(f\) is a simplicial homotopy equivalence

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of:

where

\begin{align*} f^{*}: \pi_0(L,K) \rightarrow \pi_0(K,K) \end{align*}

by equivalence relation on hom for a kan complex there exists a morphism \(g: L \rightarrow K\) such that

\begin{align*} g \circ f \simeq \mathrm{id}_{K} \end{align*}

since \(f \circ g \circ f \simeq f\). by right cancellation

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

corollary of simplicial homotopy equivalence as weak equivalance
