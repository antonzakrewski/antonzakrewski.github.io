product of the directed poset of compacts surjects to union

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a hausdorff topological space, \(U,V \subseteq X\) subsets. Then for the product of directed poset of compact subspaces \(\mathcal{K}_{-}\) the map

\begin{align*} \cap: \mathcal{K}_{U} \times \mathcal{K}_V \rightarrow \mathcal{K}_{U \cap V} \\ (K_1,K_2) \mapsto K_1 \cap K_2 \end{align*}

is surjective.

2. Proof

Let \(K \in \mathcal{K}_{U \cap V}\) be compact. Then \((K,K)\) suffices
