cosingular chain functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Given \(\mathrm{TopPairs}\) as category of topological pairs, an abelian group \(M\) and Category Ab , then the cosingular chain functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} C_{\mathrm{sing}}(-,-;M): \mathrm{TopPairs}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{Ab}) \\ (X,A) \mapsto& \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A), M[0]) \\ f: (X,A) \rightarrow (Y,B) \mapsto& (f^{ \rightarrow}: \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C^{\mathrm{sing}}(Y,B), M[0]) \rightarrow \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A), M[0])) \end{align*}
