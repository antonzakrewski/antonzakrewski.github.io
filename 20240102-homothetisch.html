homothetisch

1. Definition

Let \(V\) be a real vector space and \(M,M' \subseteq V\) sets. Then \(M \sim M'\) are said to be <>, if there exists an \(t \in V\) and \(\lambda \in (0,\infty) (or \lambda \in [0,\infty)\)) such that

\begin{align*} M = \lambda M' + t \end{align*}
