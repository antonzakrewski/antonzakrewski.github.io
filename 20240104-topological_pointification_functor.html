topological pointification functor

1. Definition

given category Top and category Top*, the pointification functor is defined as covariant functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Point}: \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top}_* \\ X \mapsto& X \coprod \{*\} \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f': X \coprod \{*\} \rightarrow Y \coprod \{*\}) \end{align*}

where \(\coprod\) is the topological sum resp categorical coproduct and \(f'\) \begin{align*} f': X \coprod \{\} →& Y \coprod \{\}
x \mapsto& f(x)

2. \mapsto& *

\end{align*}

3. Proof

3.1. welldefined morphisms

follows from glueing lemma for the open cover \(X,*\), where \(f\) is continuous by assumption and \(c: * \rightarrow *\) as constant map

3.2. functorality

follows from: compute or:

  • functorality and postcomposition with a faithful functor
  • pointification functor in set
  • forgetful functor from Top to Set preserves colimits
