category of biconcrete categories

1. Proposition

The category of coconcrete categories is defined as category containing

  1. small coconcrete category as objects
  2. functors \(\mathcal{G}_1, \mathcal{G}_2\) making
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{L} \arrow[]{d}{\pi} \arrow[]{r}{\mathcal{G}_1} & \mathcal{L}' \arrow[]{d}{\pi'}\\ \mathcal{C} \arrow[]{r}{\mathcal{G}_2} & \mathcal{C}' \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
