field of functions on a projective variety

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathbb{P}_K^n\) the projective space and \(Y \subseteq \mathbb{P}_K^n\) a projective variety. Then the projective field of functions is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathcal{O}(Y) \coloneqq \{\frac{g}{h} \vert g,h \in K[X_0,...,X_n] \text{ homogen}, \mathrm{deg}(g) = \mathrm{deg}(h)\}/\sim \end{align*}

where \(\sim\) is the equivalence relation generated by

\begin{align*} \frac{g}{h} \sim \frac{g'}{h'} \Leftrightarrow h'g = hg' \mod I(Y) \end{align*}

for the vanishing ideal \(I(Y)\)
