postcomposition with a module homomorphism as module homomorphism for a commutative ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M,N,L\) \(R\)-modules and \(f: N \rightarrow L\) a module-homomorphism Then postcomposition is a module homomorphism

\begin{align*} f^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,N) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,L) \end{align*}

see:

  • Menge der Modulhomomorphismen über einem kommutativen Ring als Modul

2. Proof

Let \(f_1,f_2 \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,N)\) and \(r \in R\). Then by element chasing for \(n \in N\), we get

\begin{align*} f^{ \leftarrow}( f_1 + r \cdot f_2)(n) =& (f \circ (f_1 + r f_2))(n) \\ =& f((f_1 + r f_2)(n)) \\ =& f((f_1(n) + (r f_2)(n)) \\ =& f(f_1(n)) + f(r \cdot f_2(n)) \\ =& f(f_1(n)) + (r \cdot f(f_2(n))) \\ =& (f^{ \leftarrow}(f_1)(n)) + r \cdot (f^{ \leftarrow}(f_2))(n) \end{align*}
