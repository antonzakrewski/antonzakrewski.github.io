subspace of a noetherian topology as noetherian

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a Noetherian topological space and \(U \subseteq X\). Then \(U\) equipped with the subspace topology is also Noetherian.

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • noetherian space as hereditarily compact space
  • associativity of subspace topology
