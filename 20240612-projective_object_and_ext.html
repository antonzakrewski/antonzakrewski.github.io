projective object and ext

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(P \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{A})\) an object.

TFAE:

  1. \(I\) is exact
  2. \(\mathrm{Ext}_{\mathcal{A}}^{n}(P,-)\) is the zero functor
  3. \(\mathrm{Ext}_{\mathcal{A}}^{1}(P,-)\) is the zero functor

2. Proof

follows from:

  • right derived functor of an exact functor
  • covariant ab-hom functor as exact functor and projective object
