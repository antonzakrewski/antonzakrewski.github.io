unit group functor of an algebra as representable functor

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathrm{A-Alg}\) the category of A-Algebra and \(\mathrm{Ab}\) the Category Ab. then the unit group functor of an algebra (after applying the forgetful functor from Ab to Set) is a covariant representable functor

2. Proof

Consider

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{Hom}_{A-Alg}(A[X,X^{-1}], B) \simeq B^{\times} \\ \varphi \mapsto& \varphi(X) \\ (X \mapsto a) \mapsfrom& a \end{align*}
