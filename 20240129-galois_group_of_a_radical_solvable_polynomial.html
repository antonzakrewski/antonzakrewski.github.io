galois group of a radical solvable polynomial as solvable group

1. Proposition

Sei \(K\) ein Körper mit characteristik \(\mathrm{char}(K) = 0\) und \(f \in K[X]\) ein durch ein radikal lösbares Polynom. Dann ist die Galoisgruppe

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Gal}(f) \end{align*}

eine auflösbaren gruppe

2. Proof

By assumption, there exists a radical extension \(M/K\) such that \(M\) is a radical extension. By existence of galois extension for a radical extension in characteristic 0 there exists a galois field extension \(L/K\).

By galois group of a radical extension as solvable group, \(\mathrm{Gal}(L/K)\) is a solvable group.

Furthermore, by Galoiskorrespondenz, \(\mathrm{Gal}(M/K) \subseteq \mathrm{Gal}(L/K)\) is a subgroup

Thus by subgroup of a solvable group as solvable group
