product of the directed poset of compacts surjects to intersection

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a regular hausdorff topological space, \(U_i \subseteq X\) be open subsets. Then for the product of directed poset of compact subspaces \(\mathcal{K}_{-}\) the map

\begin{align*} \cap: \mathcal{K}_{U_i} \times \mathcal{K}_V \rightarrow \mathcal{K}_{\bigcup U_i} \\ (K_i) \mapsto& \bigcup K_i \end{align*}

is surjective.
