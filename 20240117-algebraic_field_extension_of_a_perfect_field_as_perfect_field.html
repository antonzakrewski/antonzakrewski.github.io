algebraic field extension of a perfect field as perfect field

1. Proposition

Let \(L/K\) be an algebraic field extension for a perfect field \(K\). Then \(L\) is also a perfect field.

2. Proof

Let \(E/L\) be an algebraic field extension, then so is \(E/K\) (cf. algebraic field extension and intermediate field extension). by assumption, \(E/K\) is a separable field extension.

Thus by Sandwhichsatz über separablen Körpererweiterungen, \(E/L\) is also separable and hence \(L\) is perfect
