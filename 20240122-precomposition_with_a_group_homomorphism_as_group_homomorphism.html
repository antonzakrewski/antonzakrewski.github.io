precomposition with a group homomorphism as group homomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(G,H,N\) be abelian groups and \(\varphi: G \rightarrow H\) a group-homomorphism. Then precomposition is a group-homomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Ab}}(H,N) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Ab}}(G,N) \end{align*}

see: Menge der abelschen Gruppenhomomorphismen als abelsche Gruppe

2. Proof

Let \(f_1,f_2 \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Ab}}(H,N)\), then by element chasing for \(g \in G\), we get

\begin{align*} \varphi^{ \rightarrow}(f_1 + f_2)(g) =& (f_1 + f_2)(\varphi(g)) \\ =& f_1(\varphi(g)) + f_2(\varphi(g)) \\ =& \varphi^{ \rightarrow}(f_1)(g) + \varphi^{ \rightarrow}(f_2)(g) \end{align*}

hence

\begin{align*} \varphi^{ \rightarrow}(f_1 + f_2) =& \varphi^{ \rightarrow}(f_1) + \varphi^{ \leftarrow}(f_2) \end{align*}
