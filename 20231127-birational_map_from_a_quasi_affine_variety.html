rational map from a quasi-affine variety

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be algebraically closed field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(V,W \subseteq \mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) be an affine variety. Let \(U \rightarrowtail V\) be a simple quasi-affine variety.

A rational map \(f: U \rightarrow W\) is defined as rational function / rational map

\begin{align*} f: V \rightarrow W \end{align*}

with \(U \subseteq \mathrm{dom}(f)\) (see: domain of a rational functiowa )
