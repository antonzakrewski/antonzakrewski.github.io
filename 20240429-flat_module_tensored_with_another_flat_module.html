flat module tensored with another flat module

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(M,N\) flat modules and

\begin{align*} M \otimes_A N \end{align*}

the tensor product

Then \(M \otimes_A N\) is also flat

2. Proof

follows from associativity of a tensor product,

\begin{align*} ((M \otimes_A N) \otimes_A -) \simeq (M \otimes_A (N \otimes_A -)) \end{align*}

where both $(M ⊗A -), \((N \otimes_A -)\) are exact functors, therefore also the functor composition (cf. composition of exact functors as exact functor)
