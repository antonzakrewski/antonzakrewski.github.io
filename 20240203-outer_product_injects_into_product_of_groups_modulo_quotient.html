outer product isomorphic to product of groups modulo quotient

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(N,H \subseteq G\) be subgroups Then the map

\begin{align*} \varphi: N \oplus H \rightarrow& (NH)/ \langle n h n^{-1} h^{-1} \vert (n,h) \in N \times H\rangle \\ (n,h) \mapsto& n \cdot h \end{align*}

is a group-isomorphism
