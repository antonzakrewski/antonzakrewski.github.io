filtered colimits commute with finite limits in RMod

1. Proposition

Given category RMod, filtered colimits commute with finite limits

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • filtered colimits commute with finite limits in Ab
  • forgetful functor from RMod to Ab as right adjoint / forgetful functor from RMod to Ab as left adjoint
  • right adjoint preserves limits / left adjoint preserves colimits
  • forgetful functor from RMod to Ab as conservative functor
  • conservative functor preserving limits and colimits and commuting
