separable element and p-th power

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field with positive characteristic \(p > 0\), \(L/K\) an algebraic field extension and \(\alpha \in L\) TFAE:

  1. \(\alpha\) is a separable element
  2. \(K(\alpha^p) = K(\alpha)\) as simple field extensions

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Note that \(K(\alpha^p) \subseteq K(\alpha)\). By Sandwhichsatz über separablen Körpererweiterungen, it follows, that

\begin{align*} K(\alpha)/K(\alpha^p) \end{align*}

is a separable field extension.

Furthermore the minimal polynomial \(f_{\alpha} \in K(\alpha^p)[X]\) divides

\begin{align*} X^p - \alpha^p \in K(\alpha^p)[X] \end{align*}

Then by frobenius endomorphism, we get

\begin{align*} X^p - \alpha^p =& (X - \alpha)^p \in K(\alpha)[X] \end{align*}

since \(f_{\alpha}\) is separable, we conclude from

\begin{align*} f_{\alpha} \mid (X - \alpha)^p \end{align*}

that \(f_{\alpha} = X - \alpha\) and hence by Grad einer einfachen algebraischen Körpererweiterung und Grad des Minimalpolynom it follows, that

\begin{align*} [K(\alpha) : K(\alpha^p)] = 1 \end{align*}

and therefore \(K(\alpha) = K(\alpha^p)\)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Beweis durch Kontraposition

Suppose \(\alpha\) is not separable, then for the minimalpolynomial \(f\) of \(\alpha\), by irreducible polynomial in positive characteristic as evaluation of a seperable polynomial there exists a separable, irreducible polynomial \(g_i \in K[X]\) such that

\begin{align*} g(X^{p^n}) = f \end{align*}

then \(g(X^n)\) is the minimal polynomial of \(\alpha^p\) and hence by Grad der algebraischen Körpererweiterung und Grad des Minimalpolynom and Grad von Polynomen unter Verknüpfung, it follows, that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{deg}(f) =& p^n \cdot \mathrm{deg}(g) \\ \mathrm{deg}(g(X^n)) =& n \cdot \mathrm{deg}(g) \end{align*}

and thus \(K(\alpha^p) \subsetneq K(\alpha)\)
