artinian ring as noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be an Artinian ring. Then \(A\) is also noetherian.

2. Proof

using nilradical as jacobson radical in a jacobson ring and artinian ring as jacobson ring we conclude that

\begin{align*} \mathcal{N}_A = \bigcap_{i=1}^n \mathfrak{m}_i \end{align*}

for finitely many maximal ideals \(\mathfrak{m}_i\) (cf. Artinian ring and finite amount of maximal ideals)

Note that the nilradical in an artinian ring is a nilpotent ideal it follows that

\begin{align*} (\prod_{i=1}^n \mathfrak{m}_i)^k \subseteq (\bigcap_{i=1}^n \mathfrak{m}_i)^{k} = \mathcal{N}_A^k = 0 \end{align*}

Then this follows from finite product of maximal ideals as zero product and noetherian / artinian ring
