cellular map

1. Definition

Let $(X, \mathrm{SK}n(X)), (Y,\mathrm{SK}n(Y)) $ be CW-Complexes. A cellular map \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) is a continuous map such that

\begin{align*} f[\mathrm{SK}_n(X)] \subseteq \mathrm{SK}_n(Y) \end{align*}
