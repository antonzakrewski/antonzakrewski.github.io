nerve functor

1. Definition

Given Category Cat, the nerve functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} N: \mathrm{Cat} \rightarrow& \mathrm{sSet} \\ \mathcal{C} \mapsto& ([n] \mapsto& \mathrm{Fun}([n], \mathcal{C}) \\ (\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}) \mapsto& (\mathcal{F}^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Fun}(-, \mathcal{C}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(-, \mathcal{D})) \end{align*}

see:

  • nerve of a category
  • where \([n]\) is the partially ordered set as category

(n-composable morphisms)
