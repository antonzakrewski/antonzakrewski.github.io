singular cohomology algebra functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, then the singular cohomology algebra functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Top}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{grCAlg}_R \end{align*}

where \(\mathrm{grCAlg}_R\) is defined as category of graded-commutative Algebras

2. Proof

follows from the singular cohomology group functor and the natural structure

  1. singular cohomology as graded-commutative Algebra
  2. singular cohomology induces morphism on graded-commutative Algebras
