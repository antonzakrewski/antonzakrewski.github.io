arrow category as functor category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(\mathrm{Arr}(\mathcal{C})\) the arrow category. Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Arr}(\mathcal{C}) \cong \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta^1, \mathcal{C}) \end{align*}

where \(\mathrm{Fun}(\Delta^1, \mathcal{C})\) is the functor category of the interval category
