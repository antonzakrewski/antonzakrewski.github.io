Maclaurinsche Reihe: Cosinus

1. Satz

\begin{align*} \cos(x) =& 1 - \frac{1}{2!} x^2 + \frac{1}{4!}x^4 - \frac{1}{6!}x^6 + \frac{1}{8!}x^8 ... \end{align*}

2. Herleitung

  • Ableitung Sinus/Cosinus
\begin{align*} \cos(x) =& 1 + \frac{0}{1!}x + \frac{-1}{2!}x^2 + \frac{0}{3!}x^3 + \frac{1}{4!}x^4 ... \\ =& 1 - \frac{1}{2!} x^2 + \frac{1}{4!}x^4 - \frac{1}{6!}x^6 + \frac{1}{8!}x^8 ... \end{align*}
