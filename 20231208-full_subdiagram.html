full subdiagram

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(I: \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be a diagram and \(J: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) a subdiagram.

Then \(J\) is said to be full, if the factorization \(\iota: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{I}\) is full
