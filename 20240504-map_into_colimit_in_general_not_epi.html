coprojection into colimit in general not epi

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(I: \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) a diagram and \(\mathrm{colim}(I)\) the colimit. Then in general there does not exist an epic coprojection

2. Proof

consider the category Top and a (pseudo-) CW-Complex The \(0\) skeleton consists of \(\mathbb{N}\) many points.

Then let the \(n\)-skeleton glue an n-sphere at the \(n\)-th point.

Then clearly no coprojection is a surjection.
