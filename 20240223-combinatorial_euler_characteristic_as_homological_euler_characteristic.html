combinatorial euler characteristic as homological euler characteristic

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a finite cw-complex, then the homological euler characteristic is the combinatorial euler characteristic of a cw-complex.

\begin{align*} \chi_{\mathrm{comb}}(X) = \chi_{\mathrm{hom}}(X) \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from euler characteristic of a finite cw complex and field as coefficients, as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{rk}(H_n(X)) =& \mathrm{dim}_{\mathbb{Q}}(H_n(X; \mathbb{Q})) \\ =& \mathrm{dim}_(\mathbb{Q})(H_n^{\mathrm{cell}}(X;\mathbb{Q})) \\ =& \end{align*}

(cf. finitely generated abelian group and dimension as Q Vectorspace)
