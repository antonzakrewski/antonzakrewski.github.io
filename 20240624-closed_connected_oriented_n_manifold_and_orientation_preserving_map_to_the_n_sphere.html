closed connected oriented n-manifold and map to the n-sphere

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a closed connected \(n\)-dimensional manifold and \(\sigma_X \in H_n(X)\) an orientation Then there exists a continuous map

\begin{align*} f: X \rightarrow S^n \end{align*}

such that

\begin{align*} f_*(\sigma_X}) \in H_n(S^n) \end{align*}

is a generator of \(H_n(S^n)\)
