homotopy between morphisms of simplicial sets

1. Definition

Let \(X,Y\) be simplicial sets, \(A \subseteq X\) a sub simplicial set and \(f_0,f_1: X \rightarrow Y\) natural transformations such that

\begin{align*} (f_0)_{\vert A} = (f_1)_{\vert A} \end{align*}

A homotopy from \(f_0\) to \(f_1\) relative to \(A\) is defined as map

\begin{align*} h: X \times \Delta^1 \rightarrow Y \end{align*}

such that

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \times \{0\} \arrow[]{r}{} & A \times \Delta^1 \arrow[]{r}{} & X \times \Delta^1 \arrow[]{r}{}& Y \\ A \arrow[]{u}{\simeq} \arrow[swap]{rrru}{(f_0)_{\vert A}} & & & \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} X \times \{0\} \arrow[]{d}{\simeq}\arrow[]{r}{\iota} & X \times \Delta \arrow[]{r}{H}& Y \\ X \arrow[bend right]{rru}{f_0} & & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} X \times \{1\} \arrow[]{d}{\simeq}\arrow[]{r}{\iota} & X \times \Delta \arrow[]{r}{H}& Y \\ X \arrow[bend right]{rru}{f_1} & & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

commute
