biproduct functor as preserves limits and colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an additive category and \(\mathrm{Fun}(I^{\delta},\mathcal{A})\) the functor category for a finite discrete category \(I^{\delta}\) Then the biproduct functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(I^{\delta}, \mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathcal{A} \end{align*}

is exact

2. Proof

follows from:

  • limits commute with limits
  • colimits commute with colimits
