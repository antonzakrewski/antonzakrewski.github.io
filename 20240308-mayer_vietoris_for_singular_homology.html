mayer vietoris for singular homology

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(X_1,X_2 \subseteq X\) subspaces such that \(\mathrm{int}(X_1) \cup \mathrm{int}(X_2)\). Then there exists an exact sequence of relative singular homology

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... \arrow[]{r}{} & H_{n}(X_1 \cap X_2) \arrow[]{r}{(\iota_1,\iota_2)} & H_n(X_1) \otimes H_n(X_2) \arrow[]{r}{\iota_1' - \iota_2'} & H_n(X) \arrow[]{r}{} & H_{n-1}(X_1 \cap X_2) \arrow[]{r}{\Delta_n} & ... \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

where \(\partial: H_n(X) \rightarrow H_{n-1}(A \cap B)\) is given by

\begin{align*} \partial: H_n(X) \rightarrow H_n(X,X_2) \simeq H_n(X_1,X_1 \cap X_2) \rightarrow H_{n-1}(X_1 \cap X_2) \end{align*}
