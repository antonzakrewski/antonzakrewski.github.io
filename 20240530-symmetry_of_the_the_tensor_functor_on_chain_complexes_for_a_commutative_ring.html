symmetry of the the tensor functor on chain complexes for a commutative ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) the category of chain complexes. Then there exists a natural isomorphism

\begin{align*} -_1 \otimes_R -_2 \cong -_2 \otimes_R -_1 \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • commutativity of a coproduct
  • commutativity of the tensor product for a commutative ring
