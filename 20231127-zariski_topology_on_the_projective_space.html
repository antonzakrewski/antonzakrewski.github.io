zariski topology on the projective space

1. Definition

Let \(F\) be a field and \(\mathbb{P}_K^n\) the projective space. Then the zariski topology on the \(\mathbb{P}_K^n\) is defined with the closed set.

\begin{align*} \mathcal{T}_{\mathbb{P}_K^n} = \{ V(J) \} \end{align*}

for homogeneous ideals \(J \subseteq K[X_0,...,X_n]\).

2. Proof

  1. empty set as projective algebraic set projective space as projective algebraic set
  2. finite union of projective algebraic sets
  3. intersection of projective algebraic sets as projective algebraic set
