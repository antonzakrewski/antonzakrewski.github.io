localization at a maximal ideal and isomorphism on filtration

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be an Integral domain, \(\mathfrak{m} \subseteq A\) a maximal ideal, \(A_{\mathfrak{m}}\) the localization at \(\mathfrak{m}\) with maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{n}\) Then the map

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathfrak{m}^r/\mathfrak{m}^n \rightarrow \mathfrak{n}^r/\mathfrak{n}^n \end{align*}

is an ring-isomorphism
