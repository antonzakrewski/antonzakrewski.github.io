finite product of noetherian rings as noetherian ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A_i\) be noetherian rings for \(i \in \{1,...,n\}\). Then the finite direct product

\begin{align*} \prod A_i \end{align*}

is also noetherian

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • finite product of noetherian modules as noetherian module
  • noetherian ring as noetherian module
