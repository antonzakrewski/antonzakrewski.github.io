glueing of compatible families of R-orientations for an open cover

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a manifold, \(U_{\alpha}\) an open cover of \(X\), \(R\) a ring and \((s_{\alpha})_{\alpha}\) a compatible family of orientations. Then there exists a unique R-Orientation \(\sigma_{X}\) such that

\begin{align*} \sigma_{X,x} = \rho_{x}(s_{\alpha}) \end{align*}

for all \(\alpha\) with \(x \in U_{\alpha}\)

2. Proof

\begin{align*} \rho_{x}(s_{\alpha}) = \sigma_{X,x} \end{align*}

for all \(\alpha\) with \(x \in U_{\alpha}\)

2.1. induction basis

If \(K = K_1\) is contained in a single chart \(U_{\alpha}\) excision of homology for

\begin{align*} (U_{\alpha}, K \setminus (X \setminus U_{\alpha})) \hookrightarrow (X,K) \end{align*}

\begin{align*} H_k(X, X \setminus K;R) \simeq& H_k(U_{\alpha}, X \setminus K;R) \\ \simeq& H_k( \mathbb{R}^n, \mathbb{R}^n \setminus \varphi_{\alpha}(K);R) \end{align*}
