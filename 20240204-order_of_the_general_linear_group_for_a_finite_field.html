order of the general linear group for a finite field

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{F}_q\) be a finite field and \(\mathrm{GL}_n(\mathbb{F}_q)\) the general linear group. Then the group order is

\begin{align*} \vert \mathrm{GL}_n(\mathbb{F}_q) \vert =& \prod_{i=0}^{n-1} q^n - q^{i} \\ =& \prod_{i=0}^{n-1} q^{i} ( q^{n-i} - 1) \end{align*}
