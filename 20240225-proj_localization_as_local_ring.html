proj localization as local ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a N-graded ring and \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Proj}(R)\) a non irrelevant, homogeneous ideal in Proj Then the proj localization at \(\mathfrak{p}\) is a local ring

2. Proof

follows from the definition as localization at a prime ideal as local ring
