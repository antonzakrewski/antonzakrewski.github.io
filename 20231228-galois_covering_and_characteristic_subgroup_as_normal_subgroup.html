galois covering and characteristic subgroup as normal subgroup

1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a locally and globally path-connected topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map.

TFAE:

  1. \(p\) is a galois covering
  2. for any \(e \in E\), the characteristic subgroup \(p_*(\pi_1(E,e)) \rightarrowtail \pi_1(B,p(e))\) is a normal subgroup

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
