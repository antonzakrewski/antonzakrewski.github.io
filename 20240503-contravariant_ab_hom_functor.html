contravariant ab-hom functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an Ab-enriched category and \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{A})\) an object

Then the Ab-hom functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{A}}^{\mathrm{Ab}}(-,A): \mathcal{A}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Ab} \\ B \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{A}}^{\mathrm{Ab}}(B,A) \\ f \mapsto& (f^{ \rightarrow}) \end{align*}
