reflective subcategory of a bicomplete category as bicomplete category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a bicomplete category and \(\mathcal{D}\) a reflective subcategory. Then \(\mathcal{D}\) is also bicomplete

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • reflective subcategory of a cocomplete category as cocomplete category
  • reflective subcategory inherits limits
