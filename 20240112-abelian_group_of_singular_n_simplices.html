abelian group of singular n-simplices

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space and \(n \geq 0\). Then the abelian group of singular \(n\)-simplices is defined as free abelian group of \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Top}}(\Delta^n_{\mathrm{Top}}, X))\), i.e.

\begin{align*} C^{\mathrm{sing}_{n}}(X) =& \mathrm{FreeAb}(\Delta_\mathrm{Top}^n,X) \\ =& \mathbb{Z}(\Delta_\mathrm{Top}^n,X) \end{align*}

By convention, we define

\begin{align*} C_{-1}^{\mathrm{sing}} = 0 \end{align*}

as zero group
