polynomial of degree 2 over F5 splits in F25

1. Proposition

Let \(f \in \mathbb{F}_5[X]\) be a polynomial with degree \(\mathrm{deg}(f) = 2\). Then \(f\) splits over \(\mathbb{F}_{25}\)

2. Proof

Suppose

\begin{align*} f = \alpha_2 X^2 + \alpha_1 X + \alpha_0 \end{align*}
