thinning an exact sequence to a chain complex

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... \arrow[]{r}{} & A_{n+1} \arrow[]{r}{d_{n+1}} & A_{n} \arrow[]{r}{d_n} & A_{n-1} \arrow[]{r}{d_{n-1}} & ... \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

a long exact sequence

Then for a sequence $… < ln+1 < ln < ln-1 < …$, the sequence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... \arrow[]{r}{} & A_{l_{n+1}} \arrow[]{r}{d_{l_{n+1}}} & A_{l_n} \arrow[]{r}{d_{l_n}} & A_{l_{n-1}} \arrow[]{r}{d_{l_{n-1}}} & ... \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

is a chain complex
