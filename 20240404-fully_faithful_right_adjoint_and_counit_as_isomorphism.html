fully faithful right adjoint and counit as isomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{R}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a right adjoint

  1. \(\mathcal{R}\) is fully faithful
  2. the counit is an isomorphism

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
