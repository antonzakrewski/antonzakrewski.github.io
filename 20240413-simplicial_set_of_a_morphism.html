simplicial set of a morphism

1. Definition

Let \(p: S \rightarrow X\) be a morphism of simplicial sets. Then the simplicial set associated to \(p\) is defined as

\begin{align*} X_{p/}: \Delta \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ [n] \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet} \downarrow X}(S \star \Delta^n, X) (f: [n] \rightarrow [m]) \mapsto& ((S \star f)^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet} \downarrow X}(S \star \Delta^m, X) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet} \downarrow X}(S \star \Delta^n, X)) \end{align*}

where \(\mathrm{sSet} \downarrow X\) is the slice category, \(S \star -\) is the slice join functor.
