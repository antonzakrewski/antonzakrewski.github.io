strong monoidal structure on a functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be monoidal category, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a functor.

Then a monoidal structure on \(\mathcal{F}\) contains

  1. unit constraint
  2. multiplication isomorphism of a monoidal functor
  3. left unit identity / right unit identity
  4. satisfying the hexagon identity
