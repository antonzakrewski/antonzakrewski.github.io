Hahn-Mazurkiewicz Theorem

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a Hausdorff topological space.

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is a) compact b) pathconnected c) secondcountable d) locally path connected
  2. there exists a continuous surjection \(\pi: [0,1] \rightarrow X\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollarys :)

  • continuous image of compact space as compact space
  • stetiges Bild eines wegzusammenhängenden Raumes als wegzusammenhängender Raum
  • konstruktion büer basis :)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
