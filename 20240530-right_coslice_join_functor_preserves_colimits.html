right coslice join functor preserves colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{sSet}\) be the category sSet, \(S\) a simplicial set and

\begin{align*} S \star -: \mathrm{sSet} \rightarrow S / \mathrm{sSet} \end{align*}

the right coslice join functor

Then \(S \star -\) preserves colimits

2. Proof

follows analogously to left coslice join functor preserves colimits
