morphism of locally ringed spaces

1. Definition

Let \((X,\mathcal{O}_X), (Y, \mathcal{O}_Y)\) be locally ringed spaces A morphism \(f: (X,\mathcal{O}_X) \rightarrow (Y,\mathcal{O}_Y)\) of locally ringed spaces is a morphism of ringed spaces, where the induced map on the stalks is a local ring homomorphism
