free monoid functor as left adjoint

1. Proposition

the free monoid functor is a left adjoint to the underlying-set functor, i.e.

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Mon}}(\mathrm{Mon}(X), M) \cong \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Set}}(X, \mathrm{Set}(M)) \end{align*}
