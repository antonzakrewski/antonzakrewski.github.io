alexander-whitney transformation

1. Definition

Given category Top, \(C^{\mathrm{sing}}(-)\) the augmented singular chain complex functor. Then the

the alexander-whitney transformation is given by a natural transformation

\begin{align*} \mathrm{AW}: C^{\mathrm{sing}}(-, -) \Rightarrow C^{\mathrm{sing}}(\pi_1(-)) \otimes C^{\mathrm{sing}}(\pi_2(-)) \end{align*}

corresponding to \(1\) by the equivalence class of chain maps from product to tensored augmented singular chain complexes

Note that this is only defined up to chain homotopy
