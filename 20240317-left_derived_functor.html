left derived functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) an additive functor. Then a left derived functor is a universal delta functor \(\{L \mathcal{F}_n\}_n\) with a natural isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_0 \cong \mathcal{F} \end{align*}
