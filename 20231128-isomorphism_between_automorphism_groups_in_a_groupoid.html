isomorphism between automorphism groups in a groupoid

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a groupoid. Then for \(A,B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) and an isomorphism \(f: A \rightarrow B\), there exists the natural isomorphism between the automorphism group

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Aut}_{\mathcal{G}}(A) \rightarrow \mathrm{Aut}_{\mathcal{G}}(B) g \mapsto& f \circ g \circ f^{-1} \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from hom-functor and functor preserving isomorphisms
