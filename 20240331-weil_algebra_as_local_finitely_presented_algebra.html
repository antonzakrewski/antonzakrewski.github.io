weil-algebra as local, finitely presented algebra with nilpotent maximal ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(W\) be an K-Algebra. TFAE:

  1. \(W\) is a Weil-Algebra
  2. \(W\) is a local, finitely presented Algebra, such that the maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{m}\) is nilpotent ideal
