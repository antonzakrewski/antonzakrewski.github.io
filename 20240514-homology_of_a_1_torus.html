homology of a 1-torus

1. Proposition

the singular homology of a 1-torus is given by

\begin{align*} H_k(T^1) =& \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } k \in \{0,1\} \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}
