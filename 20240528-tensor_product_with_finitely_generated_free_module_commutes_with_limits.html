tensor product with finitely generated free module commutes with limits

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod Then the functor

\begin{align*} - \otimes_R R^n: \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow \mathrm{Ab} \end{align*}

preserves limits

2. Proof

using

  1. product and equalizer imply existence of a limit
  2. equalizer as kernel in an ab-enriched category

2.1. kernel

follows from \(R^n\) as free module and hence flat module

2.2. products

follows from

\begin{align*} - \otimes_R R^n =& - \otimes_R \oplus_{i=1}^n R \\ \simeq& \oplus_{i=1}^n (- \otimes_R R) \\ \simeq& \oplus_{i=1}^n - \end{align*}

where the biproduct functor as preserves limits and colimits
