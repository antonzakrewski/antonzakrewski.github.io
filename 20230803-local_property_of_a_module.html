local property of a module

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod Let \(\Pi\) be a property of a ring \(M\) Then \(\Pi\) is said to be a local property

TFAE:

  1. \(M\) has the property \(\Pi\)
  2. for each prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}\), the localization at a prime ideal \(M_{\mathfrak{p}}\) has the property \(\Pi\)
