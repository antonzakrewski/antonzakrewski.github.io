dimension and open covering

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(U_i\) an open covering of \(X\). Then for the dimension, it holds, that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dim}_{X} = \sup \{\mathrm{dim}(U_i) \} \end{align*}
