characteristic subgroup

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(B\) be a topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map. Then for \(e \in E\), the characteric subgroup is defined as (sub-)group

\begin{align*} p_*(\pi_1(E,e)) \rightarrowtail \pi_1(B,p(e)) \end{align*}
