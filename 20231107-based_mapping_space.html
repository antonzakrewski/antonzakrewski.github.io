Based Mapping space

1. Definition

Let \((X,x_0), (Y,y_0)\) be based spaces. Then the based mapping space is defined as subspace topology on

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_{*}((X,x_0), (Y,y_0)) \subseteq \mathrm{Map}(X,Y) \end{align*}

where

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_{*}((X,x_0), (Y,y_0)) \coloneqq \{f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Top}_*}((X,x_0),(Y,y_0))\} \end{align*}
