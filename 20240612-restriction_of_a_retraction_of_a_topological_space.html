restriction of a retraction of a topological space

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces, \(r: X \rightarrow Y\) a retraction. Suppose \(U \subseteq Y, U \supseteq r[X]\) is a subspace, then

\begin{align*} r: X \rightarrow U \end{align*}

is also a retraction

2. Proof

follows from

  • continuous map and restriction to the image
  • Einschränkung einer stetigen Abbildung als stetige Abbildung
