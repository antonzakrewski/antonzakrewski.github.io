left slice functor from twisted arrows and left fibration

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{sSet}\) be the category sSet,

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{d}{i} & Y \\ X \arrow[]{r}{\varphi} & X \arrow[]{u}{f} \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

be morphisms such that

  1. \(i\) is a monomorphism
  2. \(f\) is an inner and left fibration

Then the induced map for the slice and pushout

\begin{align*} X_{/ \varphi} \rightarrow X_{/ \varphi i} \times_{Y_{A f \varphi i}} X_{/ f \varphi} \end{align*}

is a left fibration

2. Proof

Given a lifting problem

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \Lambda_j^n \arrow[]{d}{} \arrow[]{r}{} & X_{\varphi/} \arrow[]{d}{} \\ \Delta^n \arrow[dashed]{ru}{} \arrow[]{r}{}& X_{\varphi/} \times_{Y_{f \varphi \iota} \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

we may reduce it to

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} B \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[bend left]{rr}{} & \Delta^n \star A \coprod_{\Lambda_j^n \star A} \Lambda_j^n \star B \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[]{d}{}& X \arrow[]{d}{}\\ & \Delta^n \star B \arrow[dashed]{ru}{} \arrow[]{r}{} & Y \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

(cf. bijection between lifting problems of join slice adjunction)

Since \(\iota: A \rightarrow B\) is by assumption a monomorphism (as is \(\Lambda_j^n \rightarrow \Delta^n\)) and the left horn inclusion as right anodyne map

Therefore we are done induced map for the slice and pushout as right anodyne
