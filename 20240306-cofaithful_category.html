coconcrete category

1. Definition

A coconcrete category is a triple \((\mathcal{C}, (\mathcal{L}, \mathcal{F}), \pi)\) where

  1. \((\mathcal{L}, \mathcal{F}: \mathcal{L} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set})\) is a concrete category
  2. \(\pi: \mathcal{L} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) is an full functor surjective on objects.
