half exact functor preserving epimorphisms as right exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a half exact functor.

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}\) preserves epimorphisms
  2. \(\mathcal{F}\) is right exact functor

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
