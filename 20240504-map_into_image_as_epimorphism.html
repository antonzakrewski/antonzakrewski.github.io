map into coimage as epimorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a pre-abelian category, \(f: A \rightarrow B\) a morphism and \(\mathrm{coim}(f)\) the coimage. Then the canonical morphism

\begin{align*} A \rightarrow \mathrm{coim}(f) \end{align*}

is an epimorphism
