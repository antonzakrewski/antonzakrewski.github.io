tor for a field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field. Then the Tor Functor is naturally isomorphic to the zero functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Tor}_n^R(-,-) \simeq 0 \end{align*}

for \(n \geq 1\)

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • field as absolutely flat ring
  • tor for an absolutely flat ring
