tensor product of noetherian modules as noetherian module

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(M,N\) Noetherian modules over \(A\). Then the tensor product

\begin{align*} M \otimes_A N \end{align*}

is also a noetherian module

2. Proof

follows from the

  • finite sum of noetherian modules as noetherian module
  • image of a noetherian module as noetherian module

and the sum of modules surjects onto tensor product

\begin{align*} \varphi: M \oplus N \twoheadrightarrow M \otimes_A N \end{align*}
