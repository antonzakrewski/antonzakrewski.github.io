left derived functor from an Ab4 opp category commuting with products commutes with products

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, where \(\mathcal{A}\) is Ab4 opp and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a right exact functor which preserves coproducts Then there exists isomorphism of delta functors for the left derived functor

\begin{align*} \mathcal{LF}_n(\prod -) \cong \prod \mathcal{LF}_n(-) \end{align*}

2. Proof

Here \(\coprod \mathcal{F}(-)\) is right exact as \(\coprod -\) is by assumption exact (cf. composition of right exact functors as right exact functor) Hence this follows from natural transformation between right exact functors and morphism of left derived functors
