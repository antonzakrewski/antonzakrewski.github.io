suspension of a space coverable by two open pointed contractible sets

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(\Sigma X\) the suspension. Then there exists an open cover by pointed contractible spaces.

2. Proof

Let

\begin{align*} [0,1] \times X / \sim \end{align*}

be the quotient.

Then using the topology of a colimit we may take

\begin{align*} \left(\frac{1}{3}\right),1] \times X, [0,\frac{2}{3}) \times X \end{align*}

as open cover, which are precisely the Cones

Then this follows by cone as contractible space
