multiplicativity of the local degree

1. Proposition

Let \(f,g: S^n \rightarrow S^n\) be a continuous map on the n-spheres, such that there exists a point \(x \in S^n\) and neighbourhoods \(U\) of \(x\), \(V\) of \(f(x)\) and \(W\) of \(g(f(x))\), where

\begin{align*} f_{\vert U}: U \rightarrow& V \\ g_{\vert V}: V \rightarrow& W \end{align*}

are local homeomorphisms

Then it holds for the local degree, that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{deg}_x(g \circ f) = \mathrm{deg}_{f(x)}(g) \cdot \mathrm{deg}_{x}(f) \end{align*}
