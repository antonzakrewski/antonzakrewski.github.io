pre-abelian valued functor category as pre-abelian category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a small category and \(\mathcal{A}\) an pre-abelian category Then the functor category

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{A}) \end{align*}

is also pre-abelian
