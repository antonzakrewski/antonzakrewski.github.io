compact subset of a cw-complex as subset of a finite subcomplex

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \emptyset, \mathrm{SK}_n(X))\) be a CW-Complex and \(A \subseteq X\) a compact subset. Then \(A\) is contained in a finite CW subcomplex

2. Proof

idea:

  • get an open cover
