Deck as quotient of a normalizer

1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a locally and globally path-connected topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a connected covering map. Then for any \(e \in E\), there exists an group-isomorphism for the quotient group

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Deck}(p) \simeq \left( N_{\pi_1(B,p(e))}(\pi_1(E,e)) \right) / \pi_1(E,e) \end{align*}

where \(N_{\pi_1(B,p(e))}(\pi_1(E,e))\) is the Normalizer. deck group

2. Proof

corollary of

  • automorphism group of a quotient and normalizer
