categorification of a ring

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring. Then the categorifaciton of \(R\) is defined as Ab-enriched category \(BR\) with one object \(*\) and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{BR}(*,*) = R \end{align*}

where

  1. the addition is given by the enrichment
  2. the multiplication is composition

Furthermore the neutral elements are given by

\begin{align*} 1_R =& \mathrm{id}_{*} \\ 0_R =& 0 \end{align*}
