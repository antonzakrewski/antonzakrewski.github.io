signature of a bilinear form admitting a lagrangian

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field with \(\mathrm{char}(K) \neq 2\) and \(V\) be a finite dimensional vector space. Let \(\varphi: V \otimes_K V \rightarrow K\) be a non degenerate symmetric bilinear form on \(V\). Suppose \(\varphi\) admits a lagrangian.

Then the signature of \(\varphi\) is zero
