finite coproduct in the category of propositions as disjunction

1. Proposition

Given the category of propositions \(\mathrm{Prop}\) and Propositions \(A_1,...,A_n\), the categorical coproduct is the disjunction

\begin{align*} A_1 \lor ... \lor A_n \end{align*}
