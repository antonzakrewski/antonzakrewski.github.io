inclusion from an open set as open map

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(U \subseteq X\) a subspace.

TFAE:

  1. \(U\) is open
  2. the inclusion \(\iota: U \rightarrow X\) is open

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

follows from openness in a subspace

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from \(U\) as open space inside \(U\), hence \(\iota[U] = U \subseteq X\) is open
