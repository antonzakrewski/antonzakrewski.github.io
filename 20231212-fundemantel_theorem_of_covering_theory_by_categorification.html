functor from category of coverings to fundamentalgroup-sets as fully faithful

1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a locally pathconnected topological space. Then the functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fib}_{B}: \mathrm{Cov}(B) \rightarrow& \pi_1(B,b)-\mathrm{Set} \cong \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(b), \mathrm{Set}) \\ (p: E \rightarrow B) \mapsto& (\mathrm{Fib}^p: \tau_{\leq 1}(B) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set}) \\ (f: (p: E \rightarrow B) \rightarrow (p': E' \rightarrow B) \rightarrow& (f_{b}: \mathrm{Fib}^p \rightarrow \mathrm{Fib}^{p'}) \end{align*}

is fully faithful, where

  1. \(\mathrm{Fib}_b\) is the monodromy functor
  2. and \(f_{b}\) is the induced natural transformation

2. Proof
