smooth projective quadric as rational variety

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field with characteristic \(\mathrm{char}(F) \neq 2\). Suppose \(Q \subseteq \mathbb{P}^n\) is a smooth projective variety of dimension \(n-1\), then \(Q\) is a rational variety with

\begin{align*} Q \cong \mathbb{P}^{n-1} \end{align*}
