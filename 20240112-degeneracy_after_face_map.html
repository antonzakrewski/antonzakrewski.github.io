simplicial identities of degeneracy after face map

1. Proposition

Given the simplex category, the face map \(\delta\) and the degeneracy map \(\sigma\), it holds, that

\begin{align*} \sigma_j \delta_i = \begin{cases} \delta_i \sigma_{j-1} & \mbox{if } i < j \\ \mathrm{id} & \mbox{if } i = j, j + 1 \\ \delta_{i-1} \sigma_j & \mbox{if } j + 1 < i \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}
