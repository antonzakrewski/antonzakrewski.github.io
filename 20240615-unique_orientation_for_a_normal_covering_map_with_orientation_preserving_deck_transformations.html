unique orientation for a normal covering map with orientation preserving deck transformations

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional manifold and \(\sigma\) an orientation of \(Y\). Suppose \(p: Y \rightarrow M\) is a normal covering map, such that each deck transformation is orientation preserving.
