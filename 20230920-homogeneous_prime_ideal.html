homogeneous prime ideal

1. Definition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a graded ring and \(\mathfrak{a}\) a homogeneous ideal Then \(\mathfrak{a}\) is said to be a homogeneous prime ideal, if for for homogeneous elements \(a,b \in R\) with \(a \cdot b \in \mathfrak{a}\) it follows that \(a \in R\) or \(b \in R\)
