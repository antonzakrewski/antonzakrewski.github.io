category of short exact sequences

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category Then the category of short exact sequences is defined as full subcategory of \(\mathrm{Fun}([2],\mathcal{A})\) containing short exact sequences as objects, where \([2] = \{0,1,2\}\) is partially ordered set as category
