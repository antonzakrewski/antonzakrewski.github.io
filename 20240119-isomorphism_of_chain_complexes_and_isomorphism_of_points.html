isomorphism of chain complexes and isomorphism of points

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an additive category, \(\mathrm{Ch}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes and \(f\) a morphism.

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is an isomorphism
  2. \(f_n: M_n \rightarrow M'_n\) is an isomorphism for \(n \in \mathbb{Z}\)

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • category of chain complexes as functor category
  • natural isomorphism and isomorphism on the objects
