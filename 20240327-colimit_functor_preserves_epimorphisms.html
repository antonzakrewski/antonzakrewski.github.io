colimit functor preserves epimorphisms

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(I,J: \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be diagrams, such that their colimits exist, and \(\eta: I \rightarrow J\) a natural transformation containing only epimorphisms. Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{colim}(\eta) \end{align*}

is also an epimorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • colimits commute with colimits
  • functor preserving pushouts preserves epimorphisms
  • pushout as colimit
