homotopy group

1. Definition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space.

Then the \(n\)-th homotopy group for \(n \in \mathbb{N}^+\) is defined as

\begin{align*} \pi_n(X,x) \coloneqq \pi_0(\mathrm{Map}_*((S^n,1),(X,x)) \end{align*}

where \(\pi_0\) is the path component and \(\mathrm{Map}((S^n, 1), (X,x))\) is the Based Mapping space with the compact open topology
