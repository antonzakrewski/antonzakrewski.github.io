inclusion functor from groupoid to cat as left adjoint

1. Proposition

Given the category of groupoids \(\mathrm{Grpd}\) and Category Cat \(\mathrm{Cat}\), the inclusion

\begin{align*} \iota: \mathcal{Grpd} \rightarrow \mathrm{Cat} \end{align*}

admits a right adjoint, namely the functor from cat to grouoid

2. Proof

let \(\mathcal{G}\) be a groupoid and \(\mathcal{C}\) a small category. Then there exists

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Grpd}}(\mathcal{G}, \mathrm{Iso}(\mathcal{C})) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Cat}}(\iota(\mathcal{G}), \mathcal{C}) \\ \mathcal{F} \mapsto& \mathcal{F} \circ \iota_{\mathcal{C}' \hookrightarrow \mathcal{C}} \\ \varphi^{-1}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Cat}}(\iota(\mathcal{G}), \mathcal{C}) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Grpd}}(\mathcal{G}, \mathrm{Iso}(\mathcal{C})) \\ \mathcal{F} \mapsto& \mathcal{F}_{\mathrm{res}} \end{align*}
