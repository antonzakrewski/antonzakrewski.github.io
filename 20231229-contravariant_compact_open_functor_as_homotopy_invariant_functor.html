contravariant compact open functor as homotopy invariant functor_

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, then the contravariant compact open functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}(-,X): \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow \mathrm{Top} \end{align*}

is homotopy invariant

2. Proof

corollary of precomposition with homotopic maps for the mapping spaces
