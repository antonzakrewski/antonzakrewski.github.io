resultant and root

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain, \(f_1,f_2 \in R[X]\) be polynomials and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Res}(f_1,f_2) \end{align*}

be the univariate resultant.

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathrm{Res}(f_1,f_2) = 0\)
  2. there exists a algebraic closure \(L\) of the quotient field \(Q(R)\) and an \(\alpha \in L\) such that
\begin{align*} f_1(\alpha) = f_2(\alpha) = 0 \end{align*}
