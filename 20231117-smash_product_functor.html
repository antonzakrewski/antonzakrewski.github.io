unary smash product functor

1. Definition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space. Then the unary smash product functor is defined as covariant functor

\begin{align*} (-, \wedge X): \mathrm{Top}_* \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top}_* \\ (Y,y_0) \mapsto& Y \wedge X \\ (f: (Y,y_0) \rightarrow (Z,z_0)) \mapsto& (f \wedge \mathrm{id}_{X}: Y \wedge X \rightarrow Z \wedge X) \end{align*}

where \(f \wedge \mathrm{id}_{X}\) is the smash map
