Content of my talk

motivation - necessary of set theory

  • we need some form of "smallness"
    • we want some form of "locally smallness" for yoneda
    • we want some form of "small (co-)limits" and (small) (co-)completeness:
      • large cocomplete category and thin category / large complete category and thin category
  • set theory + limits ~> give "some control over (co-)limits"
    • e.g. colimit of the identity functor as terminal object / limit of the identity functor as initial object

filtered

  • kappa filtered infinity category
  • kappa filtered colimits and kappa small limits commute in Anima
  • kappa filtered colimit of kappa filtered categories as kappa filtered categories
  • product of kappa filtered categories as kappa filtered category
  • kappa filtered infinity colimit

compact

  • kappa infinity compact object
  • motivation for regular:
    • kappa filtered for a singular cardinal
    • kappa compact functor - we might actually need larger
  • kappa small colimit of kappa compact objects
  • representable functor as kappa compact object
