spectrum of a ring as locally ringed space

1. Proposition

let \(A\) be a ring and \((\mathrm{Spec}(A), \mathcal{O})\) the spectrum of \(A\). Then \(\mathcal{O}\) is a locally ringed space

2. Proof

corollary of:
