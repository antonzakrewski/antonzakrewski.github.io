flat module over a PID and torsion free module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a principal ideal domain and \(M\) an \(A\)-module.

TFAE:

  1. \(M\) is torsion-free
  2. \(M\) is flat

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

\begin{align*} M \simeq \mathrm{colim}_{N \subseteq M} N \end{align*}

submodule of a torsion free module as torsion free module module as filtered colimit of finitely generated modules

classification of finitely generated modules over a pid pid as coherent ring

module as filtered colimit of finitely generated modules
