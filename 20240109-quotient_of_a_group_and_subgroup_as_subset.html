quotient of subgroup as G-Set

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(H \subseteq G\) a subgroup. Then the quotient

\begin{align*} G/H = \{[g]\} \end{align*}

where \([g]\) is the Linksnebenklasse and multiplication is defined as

\begin{align*} g \cdot [g'] = [g \cdot g'] \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. welldefined

2.2. unitality

follows from

\begin{align*} e \cdot [g] =& [e \cdot g] \\ =& [g] \end{align*}

2.3. associativity

follows from

\begin{align*} g_1 \cdot (g_2 \cdot [g_3] =& g_1 \cdot [g_2 \cdot g_3] \\ =& [g_1 \cdot g_2 \cdot g_3] \\ =& (g_1 \cdot g_2) \cdot [g_3] \end{align*}
