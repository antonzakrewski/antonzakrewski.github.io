finitely generated k-algebra, closed point and finite field extension

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a finite type K-Algebra for a field \(K\). Let \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) be the zariski spectrum and \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) a prime ideal. TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{p}\) is a closed Point
  2. \(k \subseteq k(\mathfrak{p})\) is a finite field extension
  3. \(k \subseteq k(\mathfrak{p})\) is an algebraic field extension

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Using maximal ideal as closed point in a zariski topology and maximal ideal of a commutative ring and quotient ring as field we conclude that \(A/\mathfrak{p}\) is a field

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

corollary of: endliche Körpererweiterung als algebraische Körpererweiterung

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
