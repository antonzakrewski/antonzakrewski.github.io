factorial of p-1

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be a field of characteristic \(p\), then

\begin{align*} (p - 1)! \equiv -1 \mod{p} \end{align*}

2. Proof

By smallest nontrivial polynomial for a prime field with all roots, we conclude

w.l.o.g. for \(\mathbb{F}_p\), as it is closed under multiplication and addition.

\begin{align*} \prod_{i=1}^{p-1} (p - i) =& (p - 1) \cdot ... \cdot (p - (p-1)) \\ =& (p - 1) \cdot ... \cdot (1) =& (p-1)! \end{align*}

Dabei gilt durch anwenden vom kleinen Satz von Fermat und Polynomdivision \(X^{p - 1} = 1\) f√ºr \(x \neq 0\)

\begin{align*} \prod_{i = 1}^{p-1} (X - i) = X^{p-1} - 1 \end{align*}

Durch Einsetzen von \(p = 0\) folgt

\begin{align*} (p-1)! =& \prod_{i=1}^{p-1} (p - i) \\ =& p^{p-1} - 1 \\ =& 0 - 1 \end{align*}
