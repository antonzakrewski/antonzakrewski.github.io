composition of fully faithful functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{E}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}_1: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{F}_2: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{E}\) be fully faithful functors. Then the composition \(\mathcal{F}_2 \circ \mathcal{F}_1\) is also fully faithful

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • composition of faithful functors
