tensor product of graded-commutative algebras

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(H,H'\) be graded-commutative R-Algebras. Then the tensor product of graded Algebras is defined as

\begin{align*} H \otimes_R H' \end{align*}

by definition on homogeneous elements

\begin{align*} (x \otimes_R x) \cdot (y \otimes_R y') =& (-1)^{\vert x' \vert \cdot \vert y \vert} (xy \otimes yx) \end{align*}
