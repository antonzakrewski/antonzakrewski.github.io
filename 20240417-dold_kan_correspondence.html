dold-kan correspondence

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, \(\Delta^{\mathrm{op}}( \mathcal{A})\) the corresponding category of simplicial objects and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})^{\geq 0}\) the category of non negatively chain complexes Then there exists an equivalence

\begin{align*} \Delta^{\mathrm{op}} \mathcal{A} \cong \mathcal{C}^{\geq 0}_{*} \mathcal{A} \end{align*}
