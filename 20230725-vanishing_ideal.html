vanishing ideal

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(T \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) a subset.

Then the vanishing ideal is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathcal{I}(T) = \{f \in K[X_1,...,X_n] \vert f(x) = 0, \forall x \in T \} \end{align*}
