open covering under continuous preimage

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces, \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a continuous map and \(U_i\) an open cover of \(Y\). Then the preimages \(f^{-1}[U_i]\) form an open covering of \(X\)

2. Proof

2.1. open

follows from continuity
