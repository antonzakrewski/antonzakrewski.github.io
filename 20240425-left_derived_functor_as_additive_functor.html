left derived functor as additive functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(\mathcal{F}\) a right exact functor and \(\mathcal{LF}_n\) the left derived functor. Then \(\mathcal{LF}_n\) is an additive functor

corollary of:
