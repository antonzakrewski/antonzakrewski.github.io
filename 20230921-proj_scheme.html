Proj scheme

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a N-graded ring

Then the Proj scheme is defined as \((\mathrm{Proj}, \mathcal{O}_{\mathrm{Proj}})\), where \(\mathrm{Proj}\) is Proj and \(\mathcal{O}_{\mathrm{Proj}}\) is the structure sheaf of Proj

2. Proof

Consider the covering by the standard open of proj: Let \(\mathfrak{p}\) be a non irrelevant homogeneous ideal, then there exists an \(f \in S_+ \setminus \mathfrak{p}\) and therefore

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p} \not\in V((f)) \end{align*}

resp \(\mathfrak{p} \in D(f)\)

Thus \(\mathrm{Proj}\) is covered by \(D_+(f)\)
