oriented bordism

1. Definition

Let \(X_0,X_1\) be closed and orientated \(n\)-dimensional Manifolds. An oriented bordism from \(X_0\) to \(X_1\) is a compact oriented \(n+1\)-dimensional manifold with boundary and a homeomorph

\begin{align*} h: X_0 \coprod X_1 \rightarrow \partial W \end{align*}

such that

  1. \(h\) is orientation preserving on \(X_0\)
  2. \(h\) is orientation reversing on \(X_1\)
