epimorphism in abelian category and quotient object

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(f: A \rightarrow B\) an epimorphism Then there exists a quotient object

\begin{align*} A/\mathrm{ker}(f) \simeq B \end{align*}
