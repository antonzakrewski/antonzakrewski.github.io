associativity of the tensor product for bimodules

1. Proposition

Let \(A_1,A_2,B_1,B_2\) be rings, \(M_1\) an \\B1,A1\)- bimodule, \(M_3\) an \(A_2,B_2\)-bimodule and \(M_2\) an \(A_1,A_2\) bimodule. Then

\begin{align*} (M_1 \otimes_{A_1} M_2) \otimes_{A_2} M_3 \cong (M_1 \otimes_{A_1} M_2) \otimes_{A_2} M_3 \end{align*}

are isomorphic as \(B_1,B_2\)-bimodules

see:

  • tensor product of bimodules

2. Proof

using the tensor hom adjunction and isomorphism between covariant hom-sets and objects and bookkeeping hom set of bimodules

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{B_1,B_2}((M_1 \otimes_{A_1} M_2) \otimes_{A_2} M_3, L) \cong& \mathrm{Hom}_{B_1,A_2}(M_1 \otimes M_2, \mathrm{Hom}_{B_2}(M_3,L)) \\ \cong& \mathrm{Hom}_{B_1,A_1}(M_1, \mathrm{Hom}_{A_2}(M_2, \mathrm{Hom}_{B_2}(M_3,L))) \\ \cong& \mathrm{Hom}_{B_1,A_1}(M_1, \mathrm{Hom}_{B_2}(M_2 \otimes_{A_2} M_3, L)) \\ \cong& \mathrm{Hom}_{B_1,B_2}(M_1 \otimes_{A_1} (M_2 \otimes_{A_2} M_3),L) \end{align*}
