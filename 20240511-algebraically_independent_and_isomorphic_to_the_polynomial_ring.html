algebraically independent and isomorphic to the polynomial ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain, \(S\) an R-Algebra and \(s_i \in S\) elements.

TFAE:

  1. the polynomial ring \(R[X_i] \cong \langle s_i\rangle\)
  2. \(s_i\) are algebraically independent

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

follows as there are no relations on \(R[X_i]\)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from Homomorphiesatz für Ringe
