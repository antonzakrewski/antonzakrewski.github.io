left coslice join functor preserves colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{sSet}\) be the category sSet, \(S\) a simplicial set and

\begin{align*} - \star S: \mathrm{sSet} \rightarrow S / \mathrm{sSet} \end{align*}

the left coslice join functor

Then \(- \star S\) preserves colimits

2. Proof

using functor preserving colimits and coproducts plus coequalizers it suffices to show that \(- \star S\) preserves coproducts and coequalizers
