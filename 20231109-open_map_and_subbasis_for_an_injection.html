open map and subbasis for an injection

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, \(\mathcal{S}\) a subbasis of \(X\) and \(f: X \rightarrowtail Y\) a continuous injection

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is an open map
  2. for \(O \in \mathcal{S}\), it holds that \(f[O]\) is open

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

special case

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Consider

\begin{align*} \mathcal{B} =& \{ \bigcap_{i=1}^n S_i \vert S_1,...,S_n \in \mathcal{S}\} \end{align*}

as generated basis Then for \(O \in \mathcal{B}\), we conclude by injectivity of \(f\) (cf. Durchschnitt und Bild einer injektiven Abbildung) that

\begin{align*} f[\bigcap_{i=1}^n S_i] = \bigcap_{i=1}^n f[S_i] \end{align*}

where \(f[S_i]\) is open, thus also the finite intersection \(\bigcap_{i=1}^n f[S_i]\)
