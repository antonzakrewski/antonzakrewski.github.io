adjoint equivalence of categories and fully faithful and essentially surjective functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a functor

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}\) is fully faithful and essentially surjective.
  2. \(\mathcal{F}\) induces an adjoint equivalence

Requires some form of Axiom of choice

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

For \(d \in \mathcal{U}\), choose a \(c\) and an isomorphism \(\varphi_d: d \simeq \mathcal{F}\) Then we define the other functor

\begin{align*} \mathcal{U}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ d \mapsto& c \\ (f: d_1 \rightarrow d_2) \mapsto& \end{align*}

for \(d \in \mathcal{D}\) let

\begin{align*} \epsilon: FUd \rightarrow d \end{align*}

be an isomorphism

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}(\mathcal{U} d) \arrow[]{d}{\epsilon_d}& \mathcal{F}(\mathcal{U} d') \arrow[]{d}{\epsilon_{d'}}\\ d \arrow[]{r}{f}& d' \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath} \begin{align*} \mathcal{U}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ \end{align*}

2.1.1. triangle identity

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}(c) \arrow[]{rr}{\mathrm{id}} \arrow[]{rd}{\mathcal{F}\eta_{c}}& & \mathcal{F}(c) \arrow[]{ld}{\epsilon_{\mathcal{F}(c)}^{-1}}\\ & \mathcal{F}\mathcal{U}\mathcal{F}(c) & \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

by fullness ~> existence by faithfullness ~> uniquenes

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

2.2.1. faithful

2.2.2. fully

2.2.3. essentially surjective
