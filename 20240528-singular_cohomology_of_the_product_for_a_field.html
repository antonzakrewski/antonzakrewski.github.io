singular cohomology of the product for a field

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(X\) a topological space such that \(H^*(X;R)\) is degreewise finitely generated. Then

\begin{align*} \mu: H^*(X;K) \otimes_K H^*(Y;K) \rightarrow H^*(X \times Y;K) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of

  • kunneth formula for singular cohomology
  • tor for a field

where the split gives an isomorphism

\begin{align*} H^*(X;K) \otimes_K H^*(Y;K) \simeq& H^*(X \times Y; K \otimes_K K) \end{align*}

where (cf. tensor product with the Ring isomorphic)

\begin{align*} K \otimes_K K \simeq K \end{align*}
