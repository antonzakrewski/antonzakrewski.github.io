coproduct of categories

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}_i\) be a categories for a indexing class \(I\). then the coproduct category \(\mathcal{C} \coloneqq \coprod_{i \in I} \mathcal{C}_i\) is defined as

  1. the disjoint union of \(\mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C}_i)\) as class of objects
  2. \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}_i}(A_i,B_i)\) for \(A_i,B_i \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C}_i)\) and \(\emptyset = \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A_j,B_i)\) for \(A_i \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C}_i), B_j \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C}_j)\) for different categories \(\mathcal{C}_i\) and \(\mathcal{C}_j\)
