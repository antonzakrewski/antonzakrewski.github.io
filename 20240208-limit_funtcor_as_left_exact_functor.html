limit funtcor as left exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category admitting small limits of a shape \(\mathcal{J}\) and \(\mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{J}, \mathcal{A})\) the functor category. Then the lim functor is left exact functor

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • limits commute with limits
