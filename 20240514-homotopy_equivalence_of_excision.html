chain homotopy equivalence of excision

1. Proposition

Let \((X,A)\) be a pair of spaces, \(U \subseteq A\) a subspace with \(\overline{U} \subseteq \mathrm{int}(A)\) and \(M\) an abelian group Then the induced map

\begin{align*} C^{\mathrm{sing}}(\iota): C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A;M) \rightarrow C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X \setminus U, A \setminus U; M) \end{align*}

for the inclusion

\begin{align*} \iota: (X \setminus U, A \setminus U) \hookrightarrow (X,A) \end{align*}

is a chain homotopy equivalence
