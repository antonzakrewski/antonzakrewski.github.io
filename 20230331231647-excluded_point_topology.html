excluded point topology

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(X\) be a set and \(x_0 \in X\). The excluded point topology \({EPX}_{x_0}\) on \(X\) is defined by:

\begin{align*} {EPX}_{x_0} := \{O \subseteq X \vert x_0 \notin O \lor O = X\} \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. a) trivial sets in a topology

By construction \(X \in {EPX}_{x_0}\) and \(x_0 \in X\) and therefore \(X \in {EXP}_{x_0}\)
