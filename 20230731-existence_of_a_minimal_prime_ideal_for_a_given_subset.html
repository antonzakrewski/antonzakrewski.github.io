existence of a minimal prime ideal for a strict ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(S \subseteq A\) a strict ideal, then there exists a minimal prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p}\) with \(S \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\)
