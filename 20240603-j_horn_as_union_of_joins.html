j horn as union of joins

1. Proposition

Let \(\Delta^n\) be the represented simplicial set, \(\partial \Delta^m\) the boundary and \(\Lambda_{i}^n\) the i-horn Then the join and union is given by

\begin{align*} \Lambda_j^n \star \Delta^m \cup \Delta^n \star \partial \Delta^m =& \Lambda_j^{n+1+m} \\ \partial \Delta^m \star \Delta^n \cup \Delta^m \star \Lambda_j^n =& \Lambda_{m + 1 + j}^{n+1+m} \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from

  • i-horn joined with delta n
  • boundary of a n simplex joined with delta n

2.1. a)

Here the union contains all vertices that neither contain

  1. \((1,...,j-1,j+1,...n)\)
  2. \((m+1,...,m+n+1)\)

This excludes precisely the vertices

  1. \((1,...,m + n + 1)\) i.e. \(\Delta^{n+m+1} \setminus \partial \Delta^{n+m+1}\)
  2. \(1,...j-1,j+1,...m+n+1)\) i.e. \(\partial \Delta^{n+m+1} \setminus \Lambda_j^{n+m+1}\)

2.2. b)

follows analogously
