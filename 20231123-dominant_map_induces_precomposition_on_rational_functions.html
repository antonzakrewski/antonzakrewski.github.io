dominant map induces precomposition on field rational functions

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(V,W\) affine varieties and

\begin{align*} f: V \rightarrow W \end{align*}

dominant rational map

Then the map

\begin{align*} f^{ \rightarrow}: K(W) \rightarrow K(V) \end{align*}

is a welldefined field and vectorspace-homomorphism
