non free covering like group action locally non free

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(X\) a a topological space such that \(G\) acts covering-like on \(X\). Suppose \(G\) acts non freely on \(x\), then it acts non freely in a neighbourhood \(U\) of \(x\).
