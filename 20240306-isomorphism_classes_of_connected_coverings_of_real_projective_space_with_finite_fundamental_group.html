isomorphism classes of connected coverings of a wedge real projective space with finite fundamental group

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) be the real projective space for \(n \geq 2\). Then there exists two isomorphism classes of connected coverings of \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n \vee \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) with finite fundamental group.

2. Proof

we conclude by fundamental group of a wedge for deformation retractible points, that

\begin{align*} \pi_1(\mathbb{R} \mathbb{P}^n \vee \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n) \cong \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} * \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

Thus by coverings of a path connected space and isomorphism classes of coverings and finite subgroup of the coproduct of C2 we conclude, that there exist at most \(3\) coverings.

As \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n \wedge \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) is semi-locally simply connected space (it is locally deformation retractible), it follows, that the <> functor is essentially surjective, hence there also exist \(3\) coverings.
