deck action on the fibre

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(B\) be a topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map and \(b \in B\) Then deck group \(\mathrm{Deck}(b)\) acts canonically on the fibre of \(b \in B\)

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{Deck}(p) \times \mathrm{Fib}_b^p \rightarrow& \mathrm{Fib}_b^p \\ (f,b) \mapsto& f(b) \end{align*}
