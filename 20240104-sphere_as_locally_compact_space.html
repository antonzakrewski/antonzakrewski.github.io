sphere as locally compact space

1. Proposition

For \(n \in \mathbb{N}_0\), the n-sphere is locally compact hausdorff

2. Proof

2.1. n = 0

follows from

\begin{align*} S^1 = * \coprod * \end{align*}

where \(*\) is open and finite and thus compact (cf. finite set as compact set)

Hausdorffness follows as discrete space

2.2. \(n \geq 1\)

follows from:

  • manifold as locally compact hausdorff space
  • sphere as manifold
