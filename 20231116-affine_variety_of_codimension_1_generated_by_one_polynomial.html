affine variety of codimension 1 generated by one polynomial

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space. Then an affine variety \(X \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) with dimension \(n - 1\) is generated by one polynomial
