weak equivalence between simplicial sets

1. Definition

Let \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) be a morphism of simplicial sets Then \(f\) is said to be a weak equivalence, if for a Kan complex \(K\) the precomposition for pi0 of the inner hom is a Bijection

\begin{align*} f^{ \rightarrow}: \pi_0 \underline{Hom}(Y,K) \rightarrow \pi_0(X,K) \end{align*}
