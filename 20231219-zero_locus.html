zero locus

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(B\) a commutative A-Algebra and \(A[X_i]\) a polynomial ring. Suppose \(f_i \in A[X_i]\) are polynomials, then the zero locus of \(f_i\) for \(B\) is defined as

\begin{align*} Z(f_i)(B) \coloneqq \{(x_i) \in B^i \vert \forall j \in I : f_j(x_i) = 0 \} \end{align*}
