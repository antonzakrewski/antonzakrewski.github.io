universal coefficient formula for homology

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a hereditary ring, \(C\) a chain complex of projectives and \(M\) a \(R\)-module. Then there exists a natural short exact sequence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 & H(C) \otimes_R M & H(C \otimes M) & \mathrm{Tor}(HtC), M)[1] & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

which splits (althoguh not naturally)

2. Proof

Application of the algebraic kunneth formula where the splitting follows from the kunneth formula
