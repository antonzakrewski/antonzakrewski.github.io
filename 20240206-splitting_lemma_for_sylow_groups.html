splitting lemma for sole sylow groups

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a finite group of group order \(\vert G \vert = \prod p_i^{\nu_i}\) for prime numbers \(p_i \in \mathbb{P}\). Suppose for each \(p_i\), there exists a sole sylow group.

Then

\begin{align*} G \cong \bigoplus S_{p_i} \end{align*}
