skyscraper sheaf

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(x \in X\), \(\mathcal{C}\) a complete category and \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\). Then the skyscraper sheaf is defined as direct image sheaf for the inclusion \(\iota: \{x\} \hookrightarrow X\) and the singleton sheaf \(\mathcal{F}\)
