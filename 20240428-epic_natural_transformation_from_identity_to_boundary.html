epic natural transformation from identity to boundary

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Then for the identity functor and boundary functor there exists a epic natural transformation

\begin{align*} \pi: \mathrm{id}_{\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})} \Rightarrow& \mathrm{Bound} \\ (A_n) \mapsto& (\pi_n: A_n \twoheadrightarrow \mathrm{Bound}(A_n)) \end{align*}
