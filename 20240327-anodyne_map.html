inner anodyne map

1. Definition

Let \(A,B\) be simplicial sets and \(g: A \rightarrow B\) a natural transformation Then \(g\) is said to be a inner anodyne map, if it satisfies the left lifting property with respect to left fibrations \(f: X \rightarrow Y\)
