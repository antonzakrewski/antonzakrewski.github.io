forgetful functor from the coslice category preserves limits

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(c \in \mathcal{C}\) an object and \(\mathcal{C} \uparrow c\) the coslice category. Then the forgetful functor from the coslice category preserves limits

2. Proof

follows dually from forgetful functor from the slice category preserves colimits, as

\begin{align*} (\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \uparrow c)^{\mathrm{op}} \simeq \mathcal{C} \uparrow c \end{align*}
