coherent sheaf of modules

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a scheme and \(\mathcal{F}\) a quasicoherent sheaf of modules for on open cover \(O_i\) with \(\mathcal{F}_{\vert O_i} \simeq \tilde{M}_i\).d Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is said to be coherent, if each \(M_i\) is a finitely generated module over \(A_i\)
