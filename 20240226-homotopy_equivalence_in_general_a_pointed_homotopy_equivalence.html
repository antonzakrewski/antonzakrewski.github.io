homotopy equivalent spaces not in general pointed homotopy equivalent

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0), (Y,y_0)\) be based spaces, such that \(X,Y\) are homotopy equivalent spaces. Then \((X,x_0)\) and \((Y,y_0)\) are in general not pointed homotopy equivalent.

2. Proof

follows from the counterexample of contractible space in general not a deformation retract
