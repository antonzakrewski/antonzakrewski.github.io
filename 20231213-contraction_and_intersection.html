contraction and intersection

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be a commutative ring, \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) be a ring-homomorphism and \(\mathfrak{b}_1,\mathfrak{b}_2 \subseteq B\) be ideals

Then it holds for the contraction

\begin{align*} (\mathfrak{b}_1 \cap \mathfrak{b}_2)^c = \mathfrak{b}_1^c \cap \mathfrak{b}_2^c \ \end{align*}
