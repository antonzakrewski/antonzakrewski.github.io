functor as homomorphism for the automorphism group

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be locally small category and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a functor. Then \(\mathcal{F}\) induces a group-homomorphism between the automorphism group of an object \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\)

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}: \mathrm{Aut}(A) \rightarrow \mathrm{Aut}(\mathcal{F}(A)) \\ f \mapsto& \mathcal{F}(f) \end{align*}
