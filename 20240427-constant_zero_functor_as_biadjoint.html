constant zero functor as left and right adjoint

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be pointed categories. Then the constant functor

\begin{align*} \Delta_0^{\mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D} \\ \Delta_0^{\mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \end{align*}

are both left and right adjoints
