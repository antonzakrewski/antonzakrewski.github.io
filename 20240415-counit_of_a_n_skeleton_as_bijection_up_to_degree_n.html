counit of a n skeleton as bijection up to degree n

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a simplicial set, \(\mathrm{sk}_n\) the n skeleton functor. Then for the Set of k-simplices, the canonical morphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: (\mathrm{sk}_n(X))_k \rightarrow (X)_k \end{align*}

is an isomorphism for \(k \leq n\)
