quotient sheaf on a topological space

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a pre-abelian category, \(X\) a topological space and

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_1, \mathcal{F}_2: \mathrm{Op}(X)^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathcal{A} \end{align*}

sheaves with a monomorphism \(\mathcal{F}_2 \rightarrow \mathcal{F}_1\)

Then the quotient sheaf is defined as quotient object, i.e. the sheafification of the

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_1/\mathcal{F}_2: O \mapsto \mathcal{F}_1(O)/\mathcal{F}_2(O) \end{align*}

see:
