homogeneous ideal

1. Definition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a graded ring and \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq R\) an ideal. Then \(\mathfrak{a}\) is said to be a homogeneous ideal, if

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{a} = \bigcap_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} (\mathfrak{a} \cap R_n) \end{align*}
