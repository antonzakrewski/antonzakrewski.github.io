pid with infinitely many prime ideals as jacobson ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a principal ideal domain with infinitely many prime ideals Then \(A\) is a Jacobson ring

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Noetherian domain with infinite maximal ideals and prime ideals as maximal ideals as Jacobson Ring
  • PID as noetherian ring
