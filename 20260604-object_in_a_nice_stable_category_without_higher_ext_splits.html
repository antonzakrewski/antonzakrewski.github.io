object in a nice stable category without higher ext splits

Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a stable infinity category equipped with a <nice> t-structure.
Assume that \(\mathrm{Ext}_{\mathcal{C}}^{n}(A,B) = 0\) for objects \(A,B \in \mathcal{C}^{\heartsuit}\) and \(n \geq 2\).

Then each object \(X \in \mathcal{C}\) is equivalent to

\begin{align*} X \simeq \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} \pi_n(X)[n] \end{align*}
