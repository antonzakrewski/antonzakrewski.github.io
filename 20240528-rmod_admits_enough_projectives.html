RMod admits enough projectives

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod Then \(\mathrm{RMod}\) admits enough projectives

2. Proof

corollary of

  • existence of a projective resolution unique up to homotopy for an R-Module
  • projective resolution and enough projectives
