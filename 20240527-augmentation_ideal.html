augmentation ideal

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(G\) a group and \(R[G]\) the group ring Then the augmentation ideal is defined as kernel of the augmentation map

\begin{align*} \mathrm{ker}(\varphi: R[G] \rightarrow R) \end{align*}
