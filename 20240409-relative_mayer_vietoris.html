relative mayer vietoris for singular homology

1. Proposition

Let \((X,Y)\) be a pair of spaces and \(A,B,C,D \subseteq X\) with \(C \subseteq A, D \subseteq B\). Suppose furthermore, that \(\mathrm{int}(A) \cup \mathrm{int}(B) = X, \mathrm{int}(B) \cup \mathrm{int}(C) = Y\)

Then there exists a long exact sequence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... & H_n(A \cap B, C \cap D) \arrow[]{r}{} & H_n(A,C) \oplus H_n(B,D) \arrow[]{r}{}& H_n(X,Y) \arrow[]{r}{} & ... \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
