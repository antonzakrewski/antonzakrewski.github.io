cross product as unital operation

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, and \(L\) an \(R\)-modules over a commutative ring \(R\) and \(\alpha \in H^p(X;L)\). Then the unit is actually a multiplicative neutral element modulo projections, i.e.

\begin{align*} \eta \times \alpha =& p^* \alpha \\ \alpha \times \eta =& q^* \alpha \end{align*}

where

\begin{align*} p: * \times X \rightarrow X \\ q: X \times * \rightarrow X \\ \end{align*}

are the projections
