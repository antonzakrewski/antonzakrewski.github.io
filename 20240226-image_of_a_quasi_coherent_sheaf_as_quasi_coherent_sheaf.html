image of a quasi coherent sheaves as quasi coherent sheaf

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a scheme, \(M,M'\) be quasicoherent sheaves of modules and

\begin{align*} f: M \rightarrow M' \end{align*}

a morphism of sheaves of a module

Then the image is also a quasi-coherent sheaf of modules
