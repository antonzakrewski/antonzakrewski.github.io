contraction of the extension as superset

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be a commutative ring, \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) be a ring-homomorphism and \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq A\) be an ideal. Then for the contraction and extension

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{a} \subseteq (\mathfrak{a}^{e})^{c} \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of preimage of an image, where

\begin{align*} \varphi[\mathfrak{a}] \subseteq \mathfrak{a}^{e} \end{align*}
