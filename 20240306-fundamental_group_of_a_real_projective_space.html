fundamental group of a real projective space

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) be the real projective space, then the fundamental group is given by

\begin{align*} \pi_1(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n) = \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n \geq 2 \\ \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. \(n \geq 2\)

corollary of:

  • covering like group action on space with trivial fundamental group
  • finite group acting on a hausdorff space as covering like group action
  • real projective space as quotient of a sphere
  • Sphere modulo C2 homeomorphic to the real projective space

2.2. \(n = 1\)

  • real projective space homeomorphic to the circle
  • fundamental group of a circle
