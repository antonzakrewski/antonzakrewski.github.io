finite field as perfect field

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be a finite Field. Then \(F\) is a perfect field.

2. Proof

Let \(\mathrm{char}(F) = p \in \mathbb{P}\) (see: characteristic, Charakteristik eines Integritätsbereich als Primzahl oder Null). Then the Frobeniushomomorphismus

\begin{align*} \varphi_p: F \rightarrow& F \end{align*}

is welldefined and since \(F\) is a field, it is also an algebraic ring-monomorphism (cf. ringhomomorphism from an field as monomorphism).

By <Äquivalenz von Bijektivität, Surjektivität und Injektivität bei gleich mächtigen, endlichen Mengen>, it follows that \(F\) is a ring-isomorphism. Hence by perfect field and frobenius as automorphism, \(F\) is a perfect field.
