functor category of the fundamental groupoid and 2 categorical pullback

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(A,B\) a cover with \(\mathrm{int}(A) \cup \mathrm{int}(B) = X\) and \(\tau_{\leq 1}\) the fundamental groupoid. Let \(\mathcal{G}\) be an arbitrary groupoid, then there exists a natural equivalence

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(X), \mathcal{G}) \simeq \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(B), \mathcal{G}) \widehat{\times}_{\mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A \cap B), \mathcal{G})} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A), \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

for the 2-categorical pullback of a functor diagram

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} & \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A), \mathcal{G}) \arrow[]{d}{\iota^{ \rightarrow } } \\ \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(B), \mathcal{G}) \arrow[]{r}{\iota^{ \rightarrow}}& \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A \cap B), \mathcal{G}) \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2. Proof

Note that by

  • contravariant hom-functor dualizes small colimits to limits
  • external hom functor and limits

and Seifert van Kampen about groupoids there exists an isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(X), \mathcal{G}) \simeq \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(B), \mathcal{G}) \times_{\mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A \cap B), \mathcal{G})} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A), \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

Then by isofibration induces equivalence of 2-pullback and pullback in Cat there exists an equivalence

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(B), \mathcal{G}) \times_{\mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A \cap B), \mathcal{G})} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A), \mathcal{G}) \simeq \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(B), \mathcal{G}) \widehat{\times}_{\mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A \cap B), \mathcal{G})} \mathrm{Fun}(\tau_{\leq 1}(A), \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

as \(\iota: \tau_{\leq 1}(A \cap B) \rightarrow \tau_{\leq 1}(B)\) is object-injective functor and therefore by precomposition with object-injective functor on groups as isofibration on the functor category \(\iota^{ \rightarrow}\) is an isofibration
