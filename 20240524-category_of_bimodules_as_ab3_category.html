category of bimodules as ab3 category

1. Proposition

Let (R,S) be rings and \(\mathrm{R-S-Mod}\) the category of bimodules. Then \(\mathrm{R-S-Mod}\) is an Ab3 category

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • category RMod as Ab3 category
  • isomorphism between R-S-Mod and A-Mod
