glatt isotop

1. Definition

Let \(K_1,K_2\) be knots. Then \(K_1,K_2\) are said to be smoothly isotop, if there exists a smooth map

\begin{align*} F: S^1 \times [0,1] \rightarrow \mathbb{R}^3 \end{align*}

such that

\begin{align*} F_0(S^1) =& K_1 \\ F_1(S^1) =& K_2 \end{align*}

and

  1. \(F_t\) is a smooth embedding
