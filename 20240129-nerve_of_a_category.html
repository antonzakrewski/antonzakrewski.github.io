nerve of a category

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, then the nerve is defined as functor

\begin{align*} N(\mathcal{C}): \Delta^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ [n] \mapsto& \mathrm{Fun}([n], \mathcal{C}) \\ (f: [n] \rightarrow [m]) \mapsto& (f^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Fun}([n], \mathcal{C}) \mapsto \mathrm{Fun}([m], \mathcal{C})) \end{align*}

uses large cardinal axiom
