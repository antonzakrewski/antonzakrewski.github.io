adjoint as kind of inverse

1. Thesis

Sometimes, it holds, that

\begin{align*} \mathcal{L} \circ \mathcal{R} \circ \mathcal{L} =& \mathcal{L} \\ \mathcal{R} \circ \mathcal{L} \circ \mathcal{R} =& \mathcal{R} \end{align*}

examples

  • abelianization as left adjoint
  • forgetful functor from Top to Set admits right adjoints / forgetful functor from Top to Set admits left adjoints

counterexamples

  • free group functor as left adjoint
  • free vector space
