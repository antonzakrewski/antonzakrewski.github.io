filtered colimits commute with finite limits in Set

1. Proposition

filtered colimits commute with finite limits in category set

2. Proof

w.l.o.g. it suffices to show, that

  1. filtered colimits commute with finite products
  2. filtered colimits commute with equalizers

as by product and equalizer imply existence of a limit and limits commute with limits this implies the general statement

2.1. products

Let \(A_i^j\) be sets for \(I = \{1,...,n\}\) finite, such that \(\prod_{i} A_i^j\) is a finite product. Then let

\begin{align*} \varphi: (\coprod_{j} \prod_{i} A_i^j)/\sim \rightarrow& \prod_{i} (\coprod_j A_i^j/\sim) \\ [(a_i)] \mapsto& ([a_i]) \\ \varphi^{-1}: \prod_i (\coprod_{j} A_i^j/\sim) \rightarrow& (\coprod_{j} \prod_{i} A_i^j)/\sim \\ ([a_i^j]) \mapsto& [(f_j(a_i))] \end{align*}

where \(f_j\) are inductively morphisms, such that
