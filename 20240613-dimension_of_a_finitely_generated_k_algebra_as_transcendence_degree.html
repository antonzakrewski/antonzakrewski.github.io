dimension of a finitely generated k-algebra as transcendence degree

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(A\) a finite type k-Algebra Then the dimension of \(A\) as ring is given by the transcendence degree

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dim}(A) = \mathrm{tr}(A) \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. \(\mathrm{dim}(A) \leq \mathrm{tr}(A)\)

  • dimension of a k-algebra smaller than the transcendence degree

2.2. \(\mathrm{dim}(A) \geq \mathrm{tr}(A)\)
