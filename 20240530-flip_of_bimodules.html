flip of bimodules

1. Proposition

Let \(R,S\) be rings and \(M\) an \(R,S\)-bimodule Then \(M\) admits canonically the structure of a \(S^{\mathrm{op}}, R^{\mathrm{op}}\) bimodule via

\begin{align*} s \cdot_{S^{op}} m \cdot_{R^{op}} r \coloneqq r \cdot m \cdot s \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from isomorphism between category of bimodules and A-Mod where

\begin{align*} S^{\mathrm{op}} \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} R^{\mathrm{op}}^{\mathrm{op}} = S^{\mathrm{op}} \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} R \\ \simeq R \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} S^{\mathrm{op}} \end{align*}

(cf. commutativity of the tensor product where \(\mathbb{Z}\) is commutative)
