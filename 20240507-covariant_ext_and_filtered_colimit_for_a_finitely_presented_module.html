covariant ext and filtered colimit for a finitely presented module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod, \(M\) a finitely presented module and \(\mathrm{Ext}_R^{n}\) the Ext functor Then the induced morphism between colimits and functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{colim}(\mathrm{Ext}^n_R(M,-) \rightarrow \mathrm{Ext}_R^n(M, \mathrm{colim}(-)) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  1. covariant hom from a finitely presented Module preserves filtered colimits
  2. right derived functor from an AB5 category commuting with filtered colimits commutes with filtered colimits
  3. category RMod as Ab5 category
