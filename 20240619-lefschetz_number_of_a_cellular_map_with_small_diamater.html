lefschetz number of a cellular map with small diamater

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a finite cw-complex with a metric and \(f: X \rightarrow X\) a continuous map without a fixed point. Suppose \(f\) is a cellular map and each cell of \(X\) has diameter \(\leq \frac{\delta(f)}{2}\) where \(\delta(f)\) is the displacement
