dimension of a ring and dimension of spec as topological space

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the zariski spectrum with the Zariski topology Then the ring dimension is given by the topological dimension

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dim}(\mathrm{Spec}(A)) = \mathrm{dim}(A) \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from

  • closed, nonempty irreducible set is generated by a prime ideal
