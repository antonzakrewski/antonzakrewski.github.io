graded module of boundaries on a chain complex of modules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) the category of chain complexes. Then for a chain complex \(C \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod}))\) the graded module of boundaries on \(C\) is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{GMod}(B(C)) \end{align*}

where

  1. \(\mathrm{GMod}(-)\) is the forgetful functor from chain complexes of modules to graded modules
  2. \(B(-)\) is the boundary functor of chain complexes

2. Proof
