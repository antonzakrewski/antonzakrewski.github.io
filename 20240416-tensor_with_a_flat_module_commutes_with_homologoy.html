tensor with a flat module commutes with homology

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) the category of chain complexes. Let \(M\) be a flat module, then the homology functor commutes with

\begin{align*} H_n(C) \otimes_R M \simeq H_n(C \otimes_R M) \end{align*}

2. Proof

Letting

\begin{align*} M = ... 0 \rightarrow 0 \rightarrow M \end{align*}

Tensoring results in

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... & N_2 \otimes M & N_1 \otimes M & N_0 \otimes M \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

By definition we get

\begin{align*} H_n(C) \otimes_R M =& \mathrm{ker}(\delta)/\mathrm{im}(\partial) \otimes_R M\\ =& (\mathrm{ker}(\partial) \otimes_R M)/\mathrm{ker}(\mathrm{coker}(\partial) \otimes_R M) \\ =& (\mathrm{ker}(\partial) \otimes_R M)/\mathrm{ker}(\mathrm{coker}(\partial \otimes_R M)) =& H_n(C \otimes_R M) \end{align*}
