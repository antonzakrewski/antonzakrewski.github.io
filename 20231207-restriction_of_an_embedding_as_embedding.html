restriction of an embedding as embedding

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces and \(A \subseteq X\) a subspace. Suppose \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) is a topological embedding, then the restriction

\begin{align*} f_{\vert A}: A \rightarrow Y \end{align*}

is also a restriction
