co-extension for the rational circle group as injective module

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathbb{Z}}(R,\mathbb{Q}/\mathbb{Z}) \end{align*}

as \(R\)-module via co-extension of scalars is an injective module

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Initial Object in Ring
  • co-extension of scalars as right adjoint
  • right adjoint of an exact left adjoint between additive categories preserves injective objects
  • restriction of scalars as exact functor
  • rational circle group as injective group
