additive inverse of a functor with ab-enriched codomain

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an Ab-enriched category, \(I\) an indexing category. Then for a functor \(\mathcal{F}\) and the canonical Ab-enrichment on the functor category \(\mathrm{Fun}(I,\mathcal{A})\) the inverse functor is defined as

\begin{align*} -\mathcal{F}: I \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ A \mapsto& \mathcal{F}(A) \\ f \mapsto& -\mathcal{F}(f) \end{align*}
