surjective morphism of sheaves and covering

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) be sheaves on \(X\) and

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G} \end{align*}

a morphism of sheaves

TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is a surjective morphism of sheaves
  2. for each open set \(U\) and section \(s \in \mathcal{G}(U)\) and \(\mathcal{G}(U)\) there exists an open cover \(U_i\) and sections
\begin{align*} t_i \in \mathcal{F}(U_i) \end{align*}

such that

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}(t_i) = s_i \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. shaky

2.1.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.1.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

2.2. formal
