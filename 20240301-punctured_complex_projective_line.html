punctured complex projective line

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{C}\mathbb{P}^1\) and \(S\) the complex projective line, where \(S\) is a finite subset. Then \(\mathbb{C}\mathbb{P}^1 \setminus S\) is homotopy equivalent to

\begin{align*} \bigvee_{S \setminus \{s\}} S^1 \end{align*}
