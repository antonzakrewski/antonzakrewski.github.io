forgetful functor from Vect

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field. Then the forgetful functor from category vectk is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Forget}: \mathrm{Vect}_K \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ V \mapsto& V \\ f \mapsto& f \end{align*}
