K-automorphisms for an algebraic field extension and simple field extension

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(L/K\) an algebraic field extension with degree \(n\) and \(\alpha \in L\)

TFAE:

  1. there exist \(n-1\) K-Automorphisms \(\sigma_i: L \rightarrow L\) with \(\sigma_i(\alpha) \neq \sigma_{j}(\alpha)\)
  2. \(L = K(\alpha)\) is a simple field extension

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

By assumption, \(\alpha^0,...,\alpha^{n-1}\) form a basis (cf. simple field extension and basis)
