closed singular n-simplex

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space and \(x \in C_n(X)\) be a singular n-chain. Then \(x\) is said to be closed, if for the singular boundary map \(d_n: C_n(X) \rightarrow C_{n-1}(X)\), it hold, that

\begin{align*} d_n(x) = 0 \end{align*}
