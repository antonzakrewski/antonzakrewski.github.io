injective object and RLP against monomorphisms

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category with a terminal object \(1\) and \(I\) an object.

TFAE:

  1. \(I\) is an injective object
  2. every morphism \(I \rightarrow A\) admits the right lifting property against monomorphisms
  3. the terminal morphism \(I \rightarrow *\) admits the right lifting property against monomorphisms
