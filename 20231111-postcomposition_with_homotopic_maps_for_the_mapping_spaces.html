postcomposition with homotopic maps for the mapping spaces

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y,Z\) be topological spaces \(f,f': Y \rightarrow Y\) be homotopic continuous maps and \(\mathrm{Map}(X,Z), \mathrm{Map}(Y,Z)\) the mapping spaces with the compact open topology Then the postcomposition maps

\begin{align*} f^{ \leftarrow}, f'^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Map}(X,Y) \rightarrow \mathrm{Map}(X,Z) \end{align*}

are homotopic
