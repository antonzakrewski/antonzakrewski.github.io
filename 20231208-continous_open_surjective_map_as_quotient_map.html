continous, open surjective map as quotient map

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) be a continuous, open map. Then \(f\) is a quotient map

2. Proof

Suppose \(f^{-1}[O] \subseteq Y\) is open, then by surjective image of a preimage and \(f\) being open, we conclude, that

\begin{align*} f[f^{-1}[O]] = O \end{align*}

and \(O\) is open

The other implication follows from continuity
