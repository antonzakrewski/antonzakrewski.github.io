left derived functor for a functor commuting with colimits of a shape commutes with colimits of this shape

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a right exact functor preserving colimits of a shape \(I\). Then there exists isomorphism of delta functors for the left derived functor

\begin{align*} \mathcal{LF}(\mathrm{colim}_i(-)) \cong \mathrm{colim}_i(\mathcal{LF}(-)) \end{align*}
