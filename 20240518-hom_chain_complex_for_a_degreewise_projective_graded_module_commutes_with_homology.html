hom chain complex for a degreewise projective graded module commutes with homology

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod, \(P\) a projective graded module as chain complex. Then the covariant hom chain complex functor commutes with homology functor, i.e.

\begin{align*} H(\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(P,-)) \cong \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(P,H(-)) \end{align*}

Furthermore this map is induced by evaluation
