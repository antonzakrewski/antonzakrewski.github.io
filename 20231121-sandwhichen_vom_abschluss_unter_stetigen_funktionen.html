1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a continuous map. Then for \(A \subseteq X\) it holds, that

\begin{align*} \overline{f[ \overline{A}]} = \overline{f[A]} \end{align*}
