aut group of a splitting field acts on roots

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(f \in K[X]\) a polynomial, then the automorphism group \(\mathrm{Aut}_K(K(f))\) acts on the roots of the splitting field \(K(f)\)

2. Proof

analogously to galois group acts on roots
