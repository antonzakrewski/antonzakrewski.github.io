monodromy action of a path

1. Definition

Let \(E,B\) be topological spaces, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map and \(\gamma: b \leadsto b'\) a path.

Then the monodromy action of \(\gamma\) is defined as map

\begin{align*} \tau_{\gamma}: \mathrm{Fib}_{b} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Fib}_{b'} \\ e \mapsto& \overline{\gamma_{e}}(1) \end{align*}

where \(\overline{\gamma}\) is the uniquely lifted path at \(b\)
