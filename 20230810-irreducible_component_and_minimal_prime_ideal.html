bijection between irreducible components and minimal prime ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the Zariski spectrum equipped with the Zariski topology. Then there exists an order reversing bijection between the minimal prime ideals and irreducible components

2. Proof

2.1. welldefined

  • vanishing set of a minimal prime ideal as irreducible component

2.2. surjective

  • irreducible component generated by a minimal prime ideal

2.3. injective

  • Vanishing set as Injection

2.4. order reversing
