standard open of proj

1. Definition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring. Then for a homogeneous element \(f \in S_+\), the standard open of Proj is defined as

\begin{align*} D_+(f) = \{\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Proj}(S) \vert f \not\in \mathfrak{p}\} \end{align*}

2. Proof

By definition, it holds, that

\begin{align*} D_+(f) = \mathrm{Proj}(S) \setminus V((f)) \end{align*}

where \(V((f))\) is closed
