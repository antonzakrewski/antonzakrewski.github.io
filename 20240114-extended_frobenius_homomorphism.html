n-th frobenius endomorphism

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring with prime characteristic. Then for \(n \in \mathbb{N}_0\), the \(n\)-th frobenius homomorphism is defined as

\begin{align*} F^n: R \rightarrow& R \\ x \mapsto& x^{p^n} \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from

  • composition of ring homomorphisms as ring homomorphism
  • frobenius endomorphism
  • \(n = 0\) results in the identity morphism
