functor category for a cocomplete codomain as cocomplete category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a cocomplete category, \(\mathcal{J}\) an indexing category and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{J}, \mathcal{C}) \end{align*}

the functor category

Then \(\mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{J}, \mathcal{C})\) is also cocomplete

2. Proof

corollary of colimit of functors for a cocomplete codomain
