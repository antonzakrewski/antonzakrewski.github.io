intersection of compact open subbasis sets for different open sets

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces, \(\mathrm{Map}(X,y)\) the mapping space with the compact open topology

Then for open sets \(U_i \subseteq Y\) and a compact set \(K \subseteq X\), it follows. that

\begin{align*} \bigcap_{i \in I} \mathcal{O}_{K, U_i} = \mathcal{O}_{K, \bigcap_{i \in I} U_i} \end{align*}
