forgetful functor from K-Algebra to K-Vec as essentially surjective functor

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field. Then the forgetful functor from K-Algebra to K-Vec is essentially surjective

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • free R-module as R-Algebra
  • vector space as free module
