1. Proposition

Let be a field and the affine space. Then has the initial topology for with the cofinite topology or equivalently for with only as closed set

2. Proof

2.1. finer

Suppose is a polynomial and closed. Then is also a polynomial, hence is closed. Thus is closed as finite union of closed sets. Hence is continuous (cf. Urbilder abgeschlossene Mengen unter stetigen Abbildungen)

Suppose is closed in the usual topology. Then it is generated as zero set of finitely many polynomials
