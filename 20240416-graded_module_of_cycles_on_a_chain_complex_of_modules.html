graded module of cycles on a chain complex of modules

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) the category of chain complexes. Then for a chain complex \(C \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod}))\) the graded module of cycles is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}(C(C)) \end{align*}

where

  1. \(B\) is the cycle functor of chain complexes
  2. \(\mathrm{GMod}(-)\) is the forgetful functor from chain complexes of modules to graded modules
