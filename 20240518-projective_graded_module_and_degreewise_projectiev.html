1. Proposition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a graded ring and \(M = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} M_n\) a graded module. TFAE:

  1. \(M\) is a projective graded module
  2. \(M\) is degreewise a projective module
