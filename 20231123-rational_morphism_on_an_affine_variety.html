rational morphism on an affine variety

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(X \subseteq \mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) be an affine variety. A rational morphism on \(X\) is a map

\begin{align*} f: X' \rightarrow& \mathbb{A}_K^m p \mapsto& (f_1(p),...,f_n(p)) \end{align*}

for rational functions on \(X\) \(f_i \in K(X)\), where the domain is

\begin{align*} X = \bigcap_{i=1}^n \mathrm{dom}(f_i) \end{align*}
