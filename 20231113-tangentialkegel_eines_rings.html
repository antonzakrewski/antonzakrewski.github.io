tangent cone of a ring

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{q}\) an ideal and \(\mathfrak{q}^n\) the \(n\)-th power of \(\mathfrak{q}\). Then the tangent cone with respect to \(\mathfrak{q}\) is defined as direct sum

\begin{align*} \mathrm{G}_{\mathfrak{q}}(A) = \bigoplus_{i = 1}^{\infty} A/\mathfrak{m}^i \end{align*}
