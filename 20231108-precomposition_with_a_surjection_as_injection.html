precomposition with a surjection as injection

1. Proposition

Given category set, \(f: X \twoheadrightarrow Y\) a surjection. Then the precomposition

\begin{align*} f^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Set}}(Y,Z) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Set}}(X,Z) \end{align*}

is an injection

2. Proof

follows from:

  • precomposition with an epimorphism as injection
  • surjective map as epimorphism
