chain homotopic maps and difference chain homotopic to the zero morphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Suppose \(X_*, Y_*\) are chain complexes and \(f_1,f_2: X_* \rightarrow Y_*\) are chain maps.

TFAE:

  1. \(f_1 - f_2\) is chain homotopic to the zero map
  2. \(f_1 \sim f_2\)

2. Proof

corollary of addition of chain homotopic maps, as

\begin{align*} f_1 - f_2 \sim& 0 && \vert + f_2 \\ f_1 \sim f_2 && \vert + (- f_2) \end{align*}
