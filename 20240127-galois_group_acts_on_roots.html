galois group acts on roots

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathrm{Gal}(f)\) the galois group of a separable polynomial \(f \in K[X]\). Then \(\mathrm{Gal}(f)\) acts on the set of roots

2. Proof

follows from automorphism group acting on the set, where the restriction is welldefined by root under A-Algebra homomorphisms
