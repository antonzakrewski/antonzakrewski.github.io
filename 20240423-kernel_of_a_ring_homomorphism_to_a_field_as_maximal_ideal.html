kernel of a ring epimorphism to a field as maximal ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(K\) a field and

\begin{align*} \varphi: R \rightarrow K \end{align*}

an algebrabic ring-epimorphism

Then \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi)\) is a maximal ideal

2. Proof

follows from the correspondence theorem for ideals and quotient rings and field as local Ring
