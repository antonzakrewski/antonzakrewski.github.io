functor preserving colimits and coproducts plus coequalizers

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a functor. TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}\) preserving colimits
  2. \(\mathcal{F}\) Functor preserving coproducts and coequalizer

2. Proof

corollary of

  • coproduct and coequalizer imply existence of a colimit as each
