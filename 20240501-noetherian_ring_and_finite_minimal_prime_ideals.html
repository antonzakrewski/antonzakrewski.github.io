noetherian ring and finite minimal prime ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a noetherian ring, then there exists only finitely many minimal prime ideals

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Spectrum of a noetherian ring as noetherian space
  • decomposition into irreducible components
  • bijection between irreducible components and minimal prime ideals
