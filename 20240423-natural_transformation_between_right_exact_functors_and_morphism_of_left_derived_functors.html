natural transformation between right exact functors and morphism of left derived functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be an abelian categories, \(\mathcal{F},\mathcal{G}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) right exact functors and \(\eta: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}\) a natural transformation Suppose \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) admit left derived functors \(\{\mathcal{LF}_n\}, \{\mathcal{LG}_n\}\)

Then this induces a unique morphism of delta functors

\begin{align*} \mathcal{L}(\eta): \mathcal{LF}_n \rightarrow \mathcal{LG}_n \end{align*}

2. Proof

Follows from the universal property of \(\{\mathcal{LF}_n\}\).
