2. Proof

\begin{align*} H_*(L;\mathbb{F}_p) =& H_*(S^{2d-1}/ \mathbb{F}_q; \mathbb{F}_p) \\ =& H_*(S^{2d-1}; \mathbb{F}_p) \otimes_{\mathbb{F}_p \mathbb{F}_q} \mathbb{F}_p \end{align*}
