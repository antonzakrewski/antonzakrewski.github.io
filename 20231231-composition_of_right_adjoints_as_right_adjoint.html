composition of right adjoints as right adjoint

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{E}\) be categories, \(\mathcal{L}_1: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D},\mathcal{L}_2: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{E}\) left adjoints with \(\mathcal{R}_1,\mathcal{R}_2\) as right adjoints Then the composition

\begin{align*} \mathcal{R}_2 \circ \mathcal{R}_1 \end{align*}

is also a right adjoint for \(\mathcal{R}_2 \circ \mathcal{R}_1\)

2. Proof

analogously composition of left adjoints as left adjoint (dual)
