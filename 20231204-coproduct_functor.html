binary coproduct functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category containing finite products.

Then the binary coproduct is defined as

\begin{align*} \coprod: \mathcal{C} \times \mathcal{C} \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ A,B \mapsto& A \coprod B \\ (f: A \rightarrow A', g: B \rightarrow B') \mapsto& \left((f \coprod g): (A \coprod A') \rightarrow (B \coprod B')\right) \end{align*}
