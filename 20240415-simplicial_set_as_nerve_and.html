1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a simplicial set. TFAE:

  1. \(X \simeq N([\mathcal{C}])\) for some category \(\mathcal{C}\)
  2. the restriction \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet}}(\Delta^n, X) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{sSet}}(\Lambda_{i}^n, X)\) is a bijective for \(n \geq 2, 0 < i < n\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
