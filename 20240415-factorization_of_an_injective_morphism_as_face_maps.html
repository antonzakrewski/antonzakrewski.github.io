factorization of an injective morphism as face maps in a simplex category

1. Proposition

Let \(\Delta\) be the simplex category and \(f: [k] \rightarrowtail [k + n]\) an injection Then there exist face maps \(\partial_i\) such that

\begin{align*} \partial_i \circ \partial_{i'} ... \circ \partial_{i''} = f \end{align*}
