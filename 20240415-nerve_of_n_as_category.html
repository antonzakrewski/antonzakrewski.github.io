nerve of n as standard simplex

1. Proposition

Let \([m] = \{0,...,m\}\) be the partially ordered set as category. Then the nerve is

\begin{align*} N([m]) \cong \Delta^m \end{align*}

2. Proof

By definition we get

\begin{align*} N([m]) = \Delta^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set} [n] \mapsto& \mathrm{Fun}([n], [m]) [f: [n] \rightarrow [n']] \mapsto& f^{ \rightarrow} \mathrm{Fun}([n'], [m] \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}([n], [m]) \end{align*}

where using monotone map between posets determines functor we get the natural correspondence

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}([n],[m]) \cong \mathrm{Hom}_{\Delta}([n],[m]) \cong (\Delta^m)_n \end{align*}

and similar for \(f: [n] \rightarrow [n']\)
