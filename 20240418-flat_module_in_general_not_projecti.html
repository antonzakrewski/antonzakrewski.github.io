flat module in general not projective

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M\) a flat \(R\)-module. Then \(M\) is in general not projective

2. Proof

Consider

\begin{align*} \iota: \mathbb{Z} \rightarrow \mathbb{Q} \end{align*}

Here \(\mathbb{Q}\) is a flat module (cf. localization of a ring as flat module)
