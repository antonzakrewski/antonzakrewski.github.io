M-flat module and sum

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(A\)-module. Suppose \(N_i\) are M-flat modules, then so is \(\oplus N_i\)

2. Proof

follows from

  1. tensor functor as right exact functor
  2. right exact functor as additive functor
  3. limit functor as left exact functor
  4. left exact functor between abelian categories preserves monomorphisms

where for a submodule inclusion \(M \hookrightarrow M'\)

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} M' \otimes (\oplus N_i) \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[]{d}{\cong} & M \otimes (\oplus N_i) \arrow[]{d}{\cong}\\ M' \otimes N_i \arrow[]{r}{} & M \otimes N_i \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
