1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be sets and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) an injection.

Then for a family \(A_i \subseteq X\), it holds for the intersection and image

\begin{align*} f[\bigcap_{i \in I} A_i] = \bigcap_{i \in I} f[A_i] \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. \(\subseteq\)

follows from image and intersection

2.2. \(\supseteq\)

\begin{align*} f(x_i) = y \end{align*}
