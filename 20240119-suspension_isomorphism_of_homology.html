1. Proposition

Let \(X \neq \emptyset\) be a topological space, then there exists a natural isomorphism

\begin{align*} H_{n+1} ( \Sigma (x), \{N\}) \simeq H_n(X, \{x\}) && \forall x \in X \end{align*}
