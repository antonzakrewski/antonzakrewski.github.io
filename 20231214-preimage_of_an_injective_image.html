preimage of an injective image

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be sets and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) an injective map. Then for \(A \subseteq X\), it follows for the preimage and image , that

\begin{align*} f^{-1}[f[A]] = A \end{align*}

2.2. b)

Suppose \(x \in X\) such that \(f(x) = f(a) \in f[A]\) for some \(a \in A\) Then by injectivity, we conclude, that \(x = a\) and hence

\begin{align*} x \in A \end{align*}
