Tor functor symmetric for a commutative ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod Then for a module \(A\) and the Tor Functor it holds that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Tor}_n^R(A,-) \simeq \mathrm{Tor}_n^R(-,A) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • commutativity of the tensor product
  • natural isomorphism between right exact functors and isomorphism of left derived functors
