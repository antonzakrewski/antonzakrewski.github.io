projective linear group

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathrm{GL}_n(K)\) the General Linear Group.

then the projective linear group is defined as quotient group

\begin{align*} P \mathrm{GL}_n \coloneqq \mathrm{GL}_{n+1}(K) / C(\mathrm{GL}_{n+1}(K) \end{align*}

where \(C(\mathrm{GL}_{n+1}(K)\) is the center
