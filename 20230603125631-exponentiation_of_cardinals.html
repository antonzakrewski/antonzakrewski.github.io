exponentiation of cardinals

1. Definition

Let \(\kappa, \lambda\) be Cardinals, then the exponentiation \(\kappa^{\lambda}\) is defined as the Cardinal number of the set

\begin{align*} \{f: \lambda \rightarrow \kappa \} \end{align*}
