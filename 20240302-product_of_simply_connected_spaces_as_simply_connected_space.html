product of simply connected spaces as simply connected space

1. Proposition

Let \(X_i\) be simply connected topological spaces, then the product is also a simply connected space.

2. Proof

  1. Wegzusammenhang des Produkts wegzusammenhängender Räume
  2. homotopy group functor commutes with products

where

\begin{align*} \pi_1(\prod X_i, (x_i)) \cong \prod \pi_1(X_i, x_i) = \prod 1 \cong 1 \end{align*}
