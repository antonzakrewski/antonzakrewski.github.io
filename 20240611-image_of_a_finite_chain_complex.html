image of a finite chain complex

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) be the category of chain complexes Suppose \(C_*\) is a finite chain complex and \(C_* \rightarrow D_*\) a chain map Then the image is also a finite chain complex
