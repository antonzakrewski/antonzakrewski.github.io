associativity of the categorical product

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(A_i, A_j \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) be objects for disjoint \(I,J\) (diagrams :( ) and \(\prod\) the categorical product Then

\begin{align*} \prod_{i \in I} A_i \sqcup \prod_{j \in J} A_j \cong \prod_{a \in I \sqcup J} A_a \end{align*}

are canonically isomorphism
