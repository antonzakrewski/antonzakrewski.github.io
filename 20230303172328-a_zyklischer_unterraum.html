A-zyklischer Unterraum

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module, \(U \subseteq M\) a submodule and \(A \in \mathrm{End}_R(M)\) an module-endomorphism. Then \(U\) is said to \(A\)-cyclic, if there exists an element \(m \in U\) such that

\begin{align*} \langle m, Am, A^2m, ...\rangle \end{align*}
