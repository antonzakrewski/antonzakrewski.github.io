separable polynomial modulo p

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a UFD and \(\mathfrak{p} \subseteq A\) a prime ideal. Suppose \(f \in A[X]\) is a polynomial such that

\begin{align*} \overline{f} \in (A/\mathfrak{p})[X] \end{align*}

is separable. Then \(f\) is also separable
