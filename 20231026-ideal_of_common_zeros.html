ideal of vanishing polynomials

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(A \subseteq R^n\). Then the ideal of common zeros, \(I(A)\), is defined as ideal in the polynomial ring

\begin{align*} \{f \in R[X_1,...,X_n] \vert f(a) = 0 \forall a \in A\} \end{align*}
