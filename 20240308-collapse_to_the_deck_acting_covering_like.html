collapse to the deck acting covering like

1. Proposition

Let \(E,B\) be topological spaces, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map, where \(E\) is connected and \(B\) is locally path connected. Then the map

\begin{align*} p': E/\mathrm{Deck}(p) \rightarrow B \end{align*}

is a covering map

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • deck acting covering like
  • orbital projection of a free action as covering map and covering-like
  • surjective covering map and composition
