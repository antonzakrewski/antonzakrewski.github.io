S3 of boundary inclusions generates inner anodyne maps

1. Proposition

Let \(S_3 = \{(\partial \Delta^n \rightarrow \Delta^n) \boxtimes (\iota: \Lambda_1^2 \rightarrow \Delta^2\}\) be <> for boundary inclusions and the horn inclusion Then the saturated closure of \(S_3\) is given by the inner anodyne maps
