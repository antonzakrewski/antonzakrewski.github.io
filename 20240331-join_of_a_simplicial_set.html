join of a simplicial set

1. Definition

Let \(X,Y\) be simplicial sets. Then the join is defined as

\begin{align*} (X \star Y): \Delta \rightarrow& \mathrm{Set} \\ [m] \mapsto& \coprod_{I_m,J_m \in \mathrm{Cut}([m])} X(I_m) \times Y(J_m) \\ (\varphi: [m] \rightarrow [n]) \mapsto& ( \varphi^{X \star Y}: \coprod X(I_n) \times Y(J_n) \rightarrow \coprod X(I_m) \times Y(J_m) \end{align*}

where \([m]\) is a finitely linearly ordered set and \((I_m,J_m)\) a cut and

\begin{align*} \varphi^{X \star Y}: \coprod X(I_n) \times Y(J_n) \rightarrow \coprod X(I_m) \times Y(J_m) \\ \end{align*}

is defined by the universal property of the coproduct using the maps

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} & \arrow[bend right]{lddd}{} X(I_m) \times Y(J_m) \arrow[]{rd}{} \arrow[dashed]{dd}{\exists! } \arrow[]{ld}{} \arrow[bend left]{rddd}{} & \\ X(I_m) \arrow[]{dd}{X(\alpha)} & & Y(J_m) \arrow[]{dd}{Y(\alpha)}\\ & \arrow[]{ld}{} X(I_n) \times Y(J_n) \arrow[]{rd}{}& \\ X(I_n) & & Y(J_n) \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
