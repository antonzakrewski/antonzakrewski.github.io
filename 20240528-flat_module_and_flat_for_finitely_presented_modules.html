tor 1 as zero and tor 1 as zero for finitely presented modules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module.

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathrm{Tor}_1^R(M,-) = 0\) is the zero functor
  2. \(\mathrm{Tor}_1^R(M,N) = 0\) for all finitely generated module \(N\)
  3. \(\mathrm{Tor}_1^R(M,N) = 0\) for all finitely presented modules \(N\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

special case

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

special case

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from

  1. tor functor commutes with filtered colimits
  2. module as filtered colimit of finitely presented modules
