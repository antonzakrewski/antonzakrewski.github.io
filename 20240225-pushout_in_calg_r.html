Pushout in CAlgR

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{CAlg}_R\) the category CAlgR Then \(\mathrm{CAlg}_R\) admits pushouts

2. Proof

Let

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[]{d}{} & C B \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

be a diagram.

Then consider the tensor product

\begin{align*} B \otimes_A C \end{align*}
