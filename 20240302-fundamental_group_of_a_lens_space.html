fundamental group of a lens space

1. Proposition

Let \(L(p,q_1,...,q_n)\) be a lens space. then the fundamental group is given by

\begin{align*} \mathbb{Z}/ p \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary free and proper action on a locally compact hausdorff space as covering like map covering like group action on space with trivial fundamental group
