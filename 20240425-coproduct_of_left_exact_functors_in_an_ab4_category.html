coproduct of left exact functors in an AB4 category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, where \(\mathcal{B}\) is Ab4. Suppose \(\mathcal{F}_i: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) are left exact functors, then the coproduct

\begin{align*} \coprod \mathcal{F}_i: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B} \end{align*}

is also left exact

2. Proof

colimit of functors for a cocomplete codomain corollary of

where by assumption of \(\mathcal{B}\) the functor

\begin{align*} \coprod \mathrm{Fun}(I,\mathcal{B}) \rightarrow \mathcal{B} \end{align*}

is also left exact
