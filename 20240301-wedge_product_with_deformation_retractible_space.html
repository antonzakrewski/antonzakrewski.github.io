wedge product with deformation retractible space homotopy equivalent

1. Proposition

Let \((X,*), (Y,*)\) be based spaces, such that \((Y,*)\) is a deformation retract onto \(*\). Then the wedge product \(X \wedge Y\) is homotopy equivalent to \(X\)

2. Proof

choose a homotopy

\begin{align*} H_Y: [0,1] \times Y \rightarrow Y \end{align*}

from \(\mathrm{const}(*)\) to \(\mathrm{id}_{Y}\) rel \(*\)

Let \(H_X: [0,1] \times X \rightarrow X\) be the projection onto \(X\).

Then by product with interval category as left adjoint and left adjoint preserves colimits for

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} |[label={[label distance=-2mm]-45:\ulcorner}]| * \arrow[]{r}{} \arrow[]{d}{} & X \arrow[]{d}{} \\ Y \arrow[]{r}{} & X \wedge Y \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

this reduces to

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} X \times [0,1] \coprod Y \times [0,1] \arrow[]{r}{\pi} \arrow[bend left]{rr}{H_X \coprod H_Y} & (X \times [0,1]) \coprod_{* \times [0,1]} (Y \times [0,1]) \arrow[dashed]{r}{H} & X \wedge Y \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Since \(H_X, H_Y\) agree on \(* \times [0,1]\) there exists a continuous map \(H': [0,1] \times X \wedge Y \rightarrow X \wedge Y\), which is obtained by the isomorphis \([0,1] \times (X \wedge Y) \simeq (X \times [0,1]) \coprod_{* \times [0,1]} (Y \times [0,1])\)
