number of sylow groups

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group of group order \(p^n \cdot m\) with \(p \nmid m\). Then for the number of p-Sylow-groups \(s_p\) it holds that

\begin{align*} s_p \equiv& 1 \mod p \\ s_p \mid& m \end{align*}
