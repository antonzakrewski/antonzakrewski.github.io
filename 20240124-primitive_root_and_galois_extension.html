primitive root and galois extension

1. Proposition

Let \(\zeta \in \overline{\mathbb{Q}}\) be a primitive root of unity. Then the simple field extension \(\mathbb{Q}(\zeta)/\mathbb{Q}\) is a galois field extension

2. Proof

2.1. normal

corollary of cyclotomic polynomial as minimal polynomial in Q, where \(\zeta\) by assumption generates the roots.

2.2. separable

corollary of field of characteristic 0 as perfect field
