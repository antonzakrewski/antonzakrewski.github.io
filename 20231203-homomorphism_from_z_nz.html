1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, then there exist a natural Bijection

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Group}}(\mathbb{Z}/n \mathbb{Z}, G) \rightarrow& \{g \in G \vert g^n = e\} \\ f \mapsto& f([1]) \end{align*}
