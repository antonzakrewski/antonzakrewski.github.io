lens space

1. Definition

Let \(p,q_1,...,q_n\) be integers such that \(q_i\) is coprime to \(p\). Then Lens Space \(L(p;q_1,...,q_n)\) is defined as quotient topology of \(S^{2n-1} \subseteq \mathbb{C}^n\) for the group action

\begin{align*} \mathbb{Z}/ p \mathbb{Z} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Aut}(S^{2n -1}) \\ p \mapsto& ((z_1,...,z_n) \mapsto (\mathrm{e}^{2 \pi_ \mathrm{i} \frac{q_i}{p} \cdot z_1},....,\mathrm{e}^{2 \pi \mathrm{i} \frac{q_n}{z}})) \end{align*}
