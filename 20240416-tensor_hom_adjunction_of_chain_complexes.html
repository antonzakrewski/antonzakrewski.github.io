tensor hom adjunction of chain complexes

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) be the category of chain complexes and \(C_*\) a chain complex Then there exists an adjunction

\begin{align*} C \otimes_R - \dashv \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C,-) - \otimes_R C \dashv \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C,-) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • tensor hom isomorphism of chain complexes
  • forgetful functor from hom chain complex to hom set
  • isomorphisms under functors
