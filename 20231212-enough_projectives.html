enough projectives

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category. Then \(\mathcal{C}\) is said to have enough projectives, if for every object \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\), there exists

  1. an projective object \(P\)
  2. an epimorphism \(f: A \rightarrow P\)
