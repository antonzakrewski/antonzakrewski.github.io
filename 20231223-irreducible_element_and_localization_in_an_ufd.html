irreducible element and localization in an UFD

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be an UFD, \(S \subseteq A\) a multiplicatively closed set, \(S^{-1}A\) the localization and \(\alpha \in A \setminus S\).

TFAE:

  1. \(\alpha \in A\) is irreducible
  2. \(\frac{\alpha}{1} \in S^{-1}A\) is irreducible

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
