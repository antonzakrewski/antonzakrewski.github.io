finite coproduct and finite product in an ab-enriched category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an Ab-enriched category (or even CMon enriched category), \(A_i\) finitely many objects and \(C\) TFAE:

  1. \(C = \bigoplus A_i\) is the biproduct
  2. \(C = \coprod A_i\) is the coproduct
  3. \(C = \prod A_i\) is the product

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

special case

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
