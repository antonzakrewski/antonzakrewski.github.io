coproduct of cw complexes as cw complex

1. Proposition

Let \((X_i, \emptyset, \mathrm{SK}_n(X_i))\) be CW-Complexes. Then the categorical coproduct as topological sum is also canonically a CW-complex.

2. Proof

follows from:

  • colimits commute with colimits
