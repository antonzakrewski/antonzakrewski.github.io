Beweis: Regel von de L'Hospital

1. Beweis

för \(a \in \mathbb{R} \cup \{-\infty, \infty\}\)

1.1. \(\lim\left(\frac{0}{0}\right)\)

\begin{align*} \lim_{x \to a}\left(g(x)\right) \coloneqq& 0 \\ \lim_{x \to a}\left(f(x)\right) \coloneqq& 0 \\ \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)}{f(x)}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x) - 0}{f(x) - 0}\right) \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x) - g(a)}{f(x) - f(a)}\right) \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{\frac{g(x) - g(a)}{x - a}}{\frac{f(x) - f(a)}{x - a}}\right) \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g'(x)}{f'(x)}\right) \end{align*}

1.2. \(\lim\left(\frac{\infty}{\infty}\right)\)

\begin{align*} g_i(x) \coloneqq& \frac{1}{g_i(x)} \Rightarrow g_i'(x) = \frac{-1}{g(x)^2} g'(x) \\ f_i(x) \coloneqq& \frac{1}{f_i(x)} \Rightarrow f_i'(x) = \frac{-1}{f(x)^2} f'(x) \\ \lim_{x\to a}\left(g(x)\right) \coloneqq& \pm \infty \\ \lim_{x\to a}\left(f(x)\right) \coloneqq& \pm \infty \\ \Rightarrow \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{1}{g(x)}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(g_i(x)\right) = 0 \\ \Rightarrow \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{1}{f(x)}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(f_i(x)\right) = 0 \\ \end{align*} \begin{align*} \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)}{f(x)}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{\frac{1}{f(x)}}{\frac{1}{g(x)}}\right) \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f_i(x)}{g_i(x)}\right) \\ …& \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f_i(x)'}{g_i(x)'}\right) \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{\frac{-1}{f(x)^2} \cdot f'(x)}{\frac{-1}{g(x)^2} \cdot g'(x)}\right) \\ =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)^2}{f(x)^2}\right) \cdot \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}\right) \\ \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)}{f(x)}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)^2}{f(x)^2}\right) \cdot \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}\right) && \vert \cdot \left(\lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)^2}{f(x)^2}\right)\right)^{-1}\\ \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{g(x)}{f(x)}\right) \cdot \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f(x)^2}{g(x)^2}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}\right) \\ \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f'(x)}{g'(x)}\right) \end{align*}

1.3. \(\lim\left(\infty \cdot 0\right)\)

Durch Umformen

\begin{align*} \lim_{x \to a}\left(f(x)\cdot g(x)\right) =& \lim_{x \to a}\left(\frac{f(x)}{\frac{1}{g(x)}}\right) \end{align*}
