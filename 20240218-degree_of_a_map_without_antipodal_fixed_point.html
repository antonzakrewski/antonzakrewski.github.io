degree of a map without antipodal fixed point

1. Proposition

Let \(f: S^n \rightarrow S^n\) be a continuous map on the n-spheres for \(n \geq 1\). Suppose \(f(x) \neq -x\) for all \(x \in S^n\), then the singular homological degree is

\begin{align*} \mathrm{deg}(f) = 1 \end{align*}

2. Proof

using

  1. singular homology degree of the antipodal map
  2. multiplicativity of the singular homological degree on the sphere
  3. degree of a fixed point less map
