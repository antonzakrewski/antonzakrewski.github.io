cokernel functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a pointed category admitting cokernels and \(\mathrm{Fun}(\Delta^1, \mathcal{A})\) the arrow category. Then the cokernel functor is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{coker}: \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta^1, \mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathcal{A} \\ \end{align*}
