polynom im Fixring für eine integral extension

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain, \(\varphi: S \hookrightarrow R\) an integral extension and \(G \subseteq \mathrm{Aut}_S(R)\) an subgroup of the automorphism group in the coslice category. Then for \(\alpha \in R\), the polynomial

\begin{align*} \prod (X - \sigma_i(\alpha)) \in L^G \end{align*}

is welldefined
