stalk functor

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(x \in X\) a point and \(\mathrm{Op}(X)^{\mathrm{op}}\) the Category of Open Subsets. Let \(\mathrm{Op}(X)_x^{\mathrm{op}}\) be the full subcategory containing objects \(O \subseteq X\) with \(x \in O\) and $ι: \mathrm{Op}(X)

Suppose \(\mathcal{C}\) is a (complete) category admitting colimits of the shape \(\mathrm{Op}(X)_x^{\mathrm{op}}\), then the stalk functor for the category of presheaves (resp. category of sheaves) is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{stalk}_x: \mathrm{Psh}(X) \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \\ \mathcal{F} \mapsto \mathrm{colim}(\mathcal{F} \circ \iota) \\ \mathrm{stalk}_x: \mathrm{Sh}(X) \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \\ \mathcal{F} \mapsto \mathrm{colim}(\mathcal{F} \circ \iota) \end{align*}
