singular cohomology of the klein bottle with F2 coefficients

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be the klein bottle and \(\mathbb{F}_2\) the field with two elements. Then the singular cohomology functor is given by

\begin{align*} H^n(K,\mathbb{F}_2) \simeq \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n = 0 \\ \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} if n \in \{1,2,3\} 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • Z/2Z homology of the klein bottle
  • universal coefficient Theorem for cohomology

where

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Ab}}(\mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \simeq \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_{\mathbb{Z}}^{n}(\mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \simeq \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

(cf. ext 1 of two finitely generated abelian groups)
