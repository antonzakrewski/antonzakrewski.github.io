covariant co-Yoneda Lemma

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a small category and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set}\) a set-valued functor Then there exists a canonical isomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} / \mathcal{F}} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(X,-) \rightarrow \mathcal{F}(-) \end{align*}

where \(\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}/\mathcal{F}\) is the slice category by yoneda

2. Proof

follows from the contravariant co-Yoneda Lemma for \(\mathcal{D} = \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}\) and thus \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{D}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set}\),

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}/\mathcal{F}} (\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(X,-)) \arrow[]{d}{\simeq} & \\ \mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}/\mathcal{F}} (\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}}(-,X)) \arrow[]{d}{\simeq}& \\ \mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{D}/\mathcal{F}} (\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{D}}(-, X)) \arrow[]{r}{\simeq}& \mathcal{F}(-) \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
