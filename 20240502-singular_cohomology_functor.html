singular cohomology group functor

1. Definition

Let \((X,A)\) be a pair of spaces and \(M\) an abelian group.. Then the singular cohomology functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} H^n(-,-;M): \mathrm{TopPairs}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ab} \\ (X,A) \mapsto& H_n(C_{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A,M) \\ (f: (X,A) \rightarrow (Y,B)) \mapsto& f^{ \rightarrow}: H^n(Y,B;M) \rightarrow H^n(X,A;M) \end{align*}

see where \(H_n(-)\) is the homology functor and \(C_{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A;M)\) the cosingular chain complex
