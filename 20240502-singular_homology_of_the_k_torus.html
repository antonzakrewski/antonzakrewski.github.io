singular homology of the k-torus

1. Proposition

Let \(T^n\) be the n-torus. Then the singular homology is given by

\begin{align*} H_k(T^n) \cong \mathbb{Z}^{\binom{n}{k}} \end{align*}

2.2. induction step

Let \(T^{n+1} \coloneqq T^{n} \times S^1\). Then by homology of the product of spaces with flat homology we conclude that

\begin{align*} H_k(T^{n+1}) =& H_{k}(T^{n} \times S^1) \\ =& \bigoplus_{p+q = k} H_p(T^n) \otimes H_q(S^1) \\ =& H_k(T^n) \otimes H_0(S^1) \oplus (H_{k-1}(T^n) \otimes H_1(S^1)) \oplus 0 \\ =& H_k(T^n) \otimes H_1(S^1) \\ \cong& \mathbb{Z}^{\binom{n}{k}} \oplus \mathbb{Z}^{\binom{n}{k-1}} \\ \cong& \mathbb{Z}^{\binom{n+1}{k}} \\ \end{align*}
