tensor product of integer quotient rings

1. Proposition

Let \(n,m \in \mathbb{Z}\), then

\begin{align*} \mathbb{Z} / n \mathbb{Z} \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} \mathbb{Z} / m \mathbb{Z} \cong \mathbb{Z} / \mathrm{gcd}(m,n)\mathbb{Z} \end{align*}
