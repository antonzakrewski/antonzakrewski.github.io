associated sheaf of modules functor from Mod

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(X = \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the affine scheme. Then the sheaf functor is defined as functor from category AMod to category sheaves of modules

\begin{align*} \mathrm{~}: \mathrm{AMod} \rightarrow& \mathcal{O}_x-\mathrm{Mod} \\ M \mapsto& \tilde{M} \\ (f: M \rightarrow N) \mapsto& (\tilde{f}: \tilde{M} \rightarrow \tilde{N}) \end{align*}

where for open \(O \subseteq X\)

\begin{align*} \tilde{f}(O): \tilde{M}(O) \rightarrow& \tilde{N}(O) \\ (s: \mathfrak{q} \mapsto \frac{m}{b}) \mapsto& (f(s): \mathfrak{q} \mapsto \frac{f(m)}{b}) \end{align*}
