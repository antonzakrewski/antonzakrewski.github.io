sum of modules surjects onto tensor product

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(M,N\) \(A\)-modules. Then there exists a canonical algebrabic ring-epimorphism from the sum

\begin{align*} \varphi: M \oplus N \twoheadrightarrow M \otimes_A N (m,n) \mapsto& m \otimes_A n \end{align*}
