n-sheeted covering for a finite group by orbital projection

1. Proposition

Let \(m,n \in \mathbb{N}\) be natural numbers. Then there exists a space \(X\) and a finite group \(G\) with group order \(\vert G \vert = m \cdot n\) such that the orbital collapse

\begin{align*} p: X \rightarrow X/G \end{align*}

is a \(n\)-sheeted covering
