covering of a locally path connected space as coproduct of coverings

1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map. Then there exist topological spaces \(E_i,B_i\) such that

\begin{align*} p = \coprod p_i: \coprod E_i \rightarrow \coprod B_i \end{align*}

where \(B_i\) is path connected

2. Proof

follows from locally path connected space as coproduct of path components and coproduct of covering maps in both arguments as covering map
