singular cohomology of a cw complex

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \emptyset, \mathrm{SK}_n(X))\) be a CW-Complex Then the singular cohomology is given by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{lim} H^*(\mathrm{Sk}_n(X)) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • isomorphism on integral singular homology and isomorphism on general singular homology
  • homology of a cw complex as colimit of skeletons
  • covariant hom-functor preserves small limits
