fully faithful embedding of countably graded objects into chain complexes

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category, \(M\) a countable set and \(\mathrm{G}^M(\mathcal{A})\) the category of graded objects. Then there exists a fully faithful functor

\begin{align*} \iota: \mathrm{G}^M(\mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}) \end{align*}
