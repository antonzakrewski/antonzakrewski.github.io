standard open and unit

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(f \in A\) an element

TFAE:

  1. \(f \in A^{\times}\) is a unit.
  2. the standard open is \(D(f) = \mathrm{Spec}(A)\)

2. Proof

By definition

\begin{align*} D(f) =& \mathrm{Spec}(A) \ V(f) \end{align*}

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Therefore

\begin{align*} =& \mathrm{Spec}(A) \ V((f)) \\ =& \mathrm{Spec}(A) \ V(A) \\ =& \mathrm{Spec}(A) \setminus \emptyset \\ =& \mathrm{Spec}(A) \end{align*}

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

It follows that

\begin{align*} V(f) = \emptyset \end{align*}

hence by existence of a maximal ideal for a strict ideal we are done, as \((f)\) is not a strict ideal and thus \(f\) is a unit (cf. unit and trivial ideal)
