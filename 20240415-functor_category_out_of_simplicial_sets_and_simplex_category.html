colimit preserving functor category out of simplicial sets and simplex category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a cocomplete category. Then

\begin{align*} (\Delta^{\bullet})^{\rightarrow}: \mathrm{Fun}^{\mathrm{colim}}(\mathrm{sSet}, \mathcal{D}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta, \mathcal{D}) \end{align*}

is an equivalence, where

  1. \(\mathrm{Fun}^{\mathrm{colim}}(\mathrm{sSet}, \mathcal{D})\) is the full subcategory containing the colimit preserving functors
  2. \(\Delta^{\bullet}: \Delta \rightarrow \mathrm{sSet}\) is the yoneda embedding of the simplex category into sSet

2. Proof

2.1. essentially surjective
