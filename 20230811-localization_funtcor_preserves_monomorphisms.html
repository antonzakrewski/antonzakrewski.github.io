module localization functor preserves injections

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(M,N\) be \(R\)-modules and \(\varphi: M \rightarrow N\) an algebraic module-monomorphism. Then for a multiplicatively closed \(S \subseteq R^{-1}\) the localization

\begin{align*} \varphi_{S^{-1}}: S^{-1}M \rightarrow S^{-1}N \end{align*}

is also an injection

2. Proof

follows from:

  • localization as exact functor
  • exact sequence and injective homomorphism
