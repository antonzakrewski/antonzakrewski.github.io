tensoring functor in Ab

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{Ab}\) be the Category Ab, then the tensoring functor is defined

\begin{align*} \otimes: \mathrm{Ab} \times \mathrm{Ab} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ab} \\ (A,B) \mapsto& A \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} B \\ (f,g) \mapsto& f \otimes g \\ \end{align*}

2. Proof

This can be regarded as functor composition of the tensoring functor and the forgetful functor from ZMod to Ab as isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Forget} \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}}( \mathrm{Forget}^{-1}(-), \mathrm{Forget}^{-1}(-)) \end{align*}
