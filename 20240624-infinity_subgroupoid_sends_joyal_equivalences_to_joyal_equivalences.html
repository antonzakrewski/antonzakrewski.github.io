infinity subgroupoid sends joyal equivalences to joyal equivalences

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{sSet}\) be the category sSet, \(f: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a joyal equivalence between infinity categories. Then the induced map on maximal infinity-subgroupoids

\begin{align*} f^{\cong}: \mathcal{C}^{\cong} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}^{\cong} \end{align*}

is also a joyal equivalence
