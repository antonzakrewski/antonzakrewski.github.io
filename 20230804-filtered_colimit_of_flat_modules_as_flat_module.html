filtered colimit of flat modules as flat module

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module. Suppose \(F_i\) are flat modules and \(I\) a filtered diagram. Then the colimit \(\mathrm{colim}_I F_i\) is a flat module

2. Proof

By definition, we have to show, that

\begin{align*} - \otimes_R (\mathrm{colim}_I F_i) \end{align*}

is a left exact functor.

As the tensor functor is a left adjoint, it commutes with colimits (cf. left adjoint preserves colimits)

Therefore, we have

\begin{align*} - \otimes_R (\mathrm{colim}_I F_i) \cong \mathrm{colim}(- \otimes_R F_i) \end{align*}

here using limit of functors for a complete domain, it follows, that

\begin{align*} (- \otimes_R F_i): \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(I, \mathrm{RMod}) \end{align*}

is left exact.

Furthermore, as \(\mathrm{RMod}\) is an Ab5 category (cf. category RMod as Ab5 category), it follows, that \(\mathrm{colim}_I\) is a left exact functor.

Thus it follows from composition of left exact functors as left exact functor, that

\begin{align*} - \otimes_R (\mathrm{colim}_I F_i) \end{align*}

is left exact
