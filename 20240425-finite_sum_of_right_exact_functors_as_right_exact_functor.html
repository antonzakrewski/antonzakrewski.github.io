finite sum of right exact functors as right exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(\mathcal{F}_i: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) right exact functors. Then the direct sum of \(\mathcal{F}_i\) is also right exact functor
