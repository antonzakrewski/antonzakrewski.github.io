right derived functor from an ab4 category commuting with coproducts commutes with coproducts

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories where \(\mathcal{A}\) is an Ab4 category and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a left exact functor which preserves products. Then there exists isomorphism of delta functors for the right derived functors

\begin{align*} \mathcal{RF}_n(\coprod -) \simeq \coprod \mathcal{RF}_n(-) \end{align*}

2. Proof

Here \(\coprod\) is by assumption exact. Therefore \(\coprod \mathcal{F}(-), \mathcal{F}(\coprod -)\) are also left exact (cf. composition of left exact functors).

Then this claim follows from natural transformation between left exact and morphism of right derived functors
