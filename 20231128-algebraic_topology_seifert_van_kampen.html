Given functors \(\mathcal{C}_1, \mathcal{C}_2\) define a category triples \((X_1,X_2, \alpha)\) with \(\alpha: \mathcal{F}_1(X_1) \rightarrow \mathcal{F}(X_2)\) as an isomorphism

a morphism \((X_1,X_2, \alpha) \rightarrow (Y_1,Y_2, \beta)\) consists of morphism

\begin{align*} f_i: X_i \rightarrow Y_i \end{align*}

such that

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{F}_1(X_1) \arrow[]{d}{\mathcal{F}_1(f_1)} \arrow[]{r}{\alpha} & \mathcal{F}_2(X_2) \arrow[]{d}{\mathcal{F_2(f_2)}}\\ \mathcal{F}_1(Y_1) \arrow[]{r}{\beta}& \mathcal{F}_2(Y_2) \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

for identity ~> normal pullback
