path connected covering induces isomorphism of fundamental group and stabilizer

1. Proposition

Let \(p: E \rightarrow B\) be a path connected covering, \(b \in B\) and \(e \in p^{-1}(b)\). Then the map

\begin{align*} p_*: \pi_1(E,e) \rightarrow \pi_1(B,b) \end{align*}

on the fundamental groups (cf. homotopy group functor) induces an group-isomorphism

\begin{align*} \pi_1(E,e) \cong \mathrm{Stab}_{\mathrm{\pi_1(B,b)} \subseteq \pi_1(B,b) \end{align*}

2. Proof
