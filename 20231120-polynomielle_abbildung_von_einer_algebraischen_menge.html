polynomial map from an algebraic set

1. Definition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(Y \subseteq \mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) an algebraic set. A map

\begin{align*} f: Y \rightarrow \mathbb{A}_K^1 \end{align*}

is said to be polynomial, if there exists a polynomial \(F \in K[X_1,...,X_n]\) such that

\begin{align*} F(p) = f(p) \forall p \in Y \end{align*}
