filtered colimit with finite hom set wlog given by a thin diagram

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(J: \mathcal{J} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) a filtered diagram, such that \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{J}}(j_1,j_2) < \infty\) is finite Then for computing the filtered colimit we may assume w.l.o.g., that for each indexed object \(A_{j_1},A_{j_2}\) the hom-set is thin, i.e.

\begin{align*} \vert \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{J}}(j_1,j_2) \vert \leq 1 \end{align*}
