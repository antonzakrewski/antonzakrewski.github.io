collapse of the real projective space as sphere

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) be the real projective space and \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^{n-1} \subseteq \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) be a subspace. Then the topological collapse is given by

\begin{align*} \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n \setminus \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^{n-1} \simeq S^n \end{align*}
