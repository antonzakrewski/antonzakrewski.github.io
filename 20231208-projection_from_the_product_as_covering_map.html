projection from the product as covering map

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be topological spaces with \(Y \neq \emptyset\) and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{pr}_1: X \times Y \rightarrow X \end{align*}

the projection from the product topology

Then \(\mathrm{pr}_1\) is a quotient map

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • continous, open surjective map as quotient map
  • projection as open map
  • projection as surjective map by construction, as \(Y \neq \emptyset\)
