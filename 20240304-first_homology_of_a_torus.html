first homology of a torus

1. Proposition

Let \(k \geq 1\), then the first singular homology of the k-torus is given by

\begin{align*} H_1(T^k) = \mathbb{Z}^k \end{align*}

2. Proof

  • fundamental group of a torus
  • first homology functor as abelianization of the first homotopy functor
