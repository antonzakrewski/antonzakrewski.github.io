module quotient

1. Definition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(M\) an \(A\)-module and \(N,P \subseteq M\) submodules. Then the module quotient is defined as

\begin{align*} \frac{N}{P} \coloneqq \{a \in A \vert (a)P \subseteq N\} \subseteq A \end{align*}
