chain endofunctor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathrm{Pos}\) be the Category PoSet, then the chain endofunctor is defined as endofunctor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Chain}: \mathrm{Pos} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Pos} \\ P \mapsto& \mathrm{Chains}(P) \\ (f: P \rightarrow P') \mapsto& f[-]: \mathrm{Chains}(P) \rightarrow \mathrm{Chains}(P') \end{align*}

where

  1. \(\mathrm{Chains}(P)\) is the partial order on chains and \(f[-]\) is the map
\begin{align*} f[-]: \mathrm{Chains}(P) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Chains}(P') \\ \{p\} \mapsto& \{f(p)\} \end{align*}
