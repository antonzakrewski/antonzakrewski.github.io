natural transformation from covariant hom functor as element

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a locally small category, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set}\) a covariant functor and \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,-)\) the covariant hom functor for an \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) Then a natural transformation

\begin{align*} \eta: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,-) \rightarrow \mathcal{F} \end{align*}

is precisely given by an element \(\mathcal{F}(A)\), i.e. there exists an isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathrm{NatTrans}(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,-),\mathcal{F}) \cong \mathcal{F}(A) \end{align*}
