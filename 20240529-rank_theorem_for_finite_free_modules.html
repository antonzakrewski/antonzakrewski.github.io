rank theorem of isomorphisms for finitely generated free modules

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(A^n,A^m\) free modules for \(n,m \in \mathbb{N}\).

TFAE:

  1. \(n = m\)
  2. there exists a module-isomorphism \(A^n \simeq A^m\)

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • abelian category as balanced category
  • rank theorem of monomorphisms for finitely generated free modules
  • rank theorem of epimorphisms for finitely generated free modules
