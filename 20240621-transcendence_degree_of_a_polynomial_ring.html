transcendence degree of a polynomial ring

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(K[X_1,...,X_n]\) the polynomial ring Then the transcendence degree is given by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{tr}(K[X_1,...,X_n]) = n \end{align*}
