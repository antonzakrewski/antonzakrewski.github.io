projection as serre fibration

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces and \(X \times Y\) the product. Then the projection

\begin{align*} \pi_X: X \times Y \rightarrow X \end{align*}

is a serre fibration
