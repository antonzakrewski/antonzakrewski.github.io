closed symmetric monoidal structure on the category of chain complexes of Modules over a commutative ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) the category of chain complexes. Then \(\mathrm{Ch}_*(\mathrm{RMod})\) has a closed symmetric monoidal category symmetric monoidal structure given by

  1. the tensor product of chain complexes functor as tensor functor of a monoidal category
  2. \(R[0]\) as tensor unit
  3. the induced isomorphism by \(M_n \otimes N_l \simeq N_= \otimes M_n\) and the coproduct functor and further as isomorphism on chain complexes (cf.isomorphism of chain complexes and isomorphism of points)
  4. the hom chain complex functor as right adjoint
