postcomposition with an isomorphism as isomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,B)\) an isomorphism.

  1. \(f\) is an isomorphism
  2. postcomposition with \(f\) is an isomorphism on hom-sets

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of:

  • covariant hom functor of points
  • isomorphisms under functors

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
