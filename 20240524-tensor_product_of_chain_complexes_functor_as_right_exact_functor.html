tensor product of chain complexes functor as right exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) be the category of chain complexes then the tensor product of chain complexes functor is right exact functor

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • tensor product of chain complexes preserves colimits

or alternatively

  • tensor hom adjunction of chain complexes
