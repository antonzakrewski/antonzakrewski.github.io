right anodyne as saturation of right horn inclusions

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{sSet}\) be the category sSet Then the set of right anodyne map is precisely given by the saturated closurer of right horn inclusions

\begin{align*} \Lambda_j^n \rightarrow \Delta^n \end{align*}

for \(j \neq 0, j \neq 1\)
