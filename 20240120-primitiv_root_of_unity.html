primitive root of unity

1. Definition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(U_n\) the set of roots of unities for \(n \in \mathbb{N}^+\) with \(\mathrm{char}(F) \not\mid n\) Then a primitiv root of unity is a generator of the group \(U_n\) (see: Einheitswurzeln als multiplikative Untergruppe)
