tensor product with unit as adjoint equivalence of monoidal categories

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a monoidal category, \(1\) the tensor unit. Then the functor

\begin{align*} 1 \otimes (-): \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \end{align*}

induces an adjoint equivalences

2. Proof

Let the right unitor resp. the left unitor

\begin{align*} \eta_X: X \rightarrow 1 \otimes X \epsilon_X: \mathcal{F} \mathcal{U}(x) \rightarrow 1 \otimes x \rightarrow X \end{align*}
