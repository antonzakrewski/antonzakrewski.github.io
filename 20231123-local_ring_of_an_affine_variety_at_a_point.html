local ring of an affine variety at a point

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space, \(X \subseteq \mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) be an affine variety and \(x = (x_1,...,x_n) \in X\).

Then the local ring of \(X\) at \(x\) is defined as subring of the rational functions on an affine variety containing regular functions at \(x\)
