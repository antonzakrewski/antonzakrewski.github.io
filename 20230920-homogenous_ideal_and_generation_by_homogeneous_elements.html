homogeneous ideal and generation by homogeneous elements

1. Proposition

Let \(R = \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{N}_0} R_n\) be a graded ring and \(\mathfrak{h}\) an ideal TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{h}\) is a homogeneous ideal
  2. there exists homogeneous elements \(h_i \in R\) for \(i \in I\) such that \((h_i) = \mathfrak{h}\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
