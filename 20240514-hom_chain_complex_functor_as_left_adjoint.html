hom chain complex functor of modules as left exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) be the category of chain complexes. Then the hom chain complex functor

\begin{align*} \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(-,-): \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})^{\mathrm{op}} \times \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod}) \end{align*}

is left exact functor

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • right adjoint as left exact functor
  • tensor hom adjunction of chain complexes
