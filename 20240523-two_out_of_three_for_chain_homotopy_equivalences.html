two out of three for chain homotopy equivalences

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes Then the subcollection of chain homotopy equivalences satisfies two out of three

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • two out of three for isomorphisms
  • homotopy category of chain complexes
  • functor reflects two out of three
