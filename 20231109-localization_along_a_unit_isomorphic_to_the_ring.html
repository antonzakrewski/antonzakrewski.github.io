localization along a unit isomorphic to the ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(a \in R^{\times}\) a unit. Then the localization along \(a\), \(R_{a}\) is isomorphic to the Ring \(R\),

\begin{align*} R_{a} \cong R \end{align*}

2. Proof

  • functor for change of multiplicalitve set for a localization
  • localization along the unit group isomorphic to a ring
  • addition of units to the multiplicative set and conservative functor
