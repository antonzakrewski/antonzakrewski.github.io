left kan extension of a functor along an inclusion

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{D}\) be a cocomplete category and \(\mathcal{C}\) a category Let \(\iota: \mathcal{C}_0 \hookrightarrow \mathcal{C}\) be a small subcategory, then the restriction

\begin{align*} \iota^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}_0, \mathcal{d}) \end{align*}

admits a left adjoint given by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Lan}_{\iota}: \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}_0, \mathcal{D}) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}) \\ \mathcal{F} \mapsto& \begin{cases} \mathrm{Lan}_{\iota}(\mathcal{F}): X \mapsto& \mathrm{colim}_{c \in \mathcal{C}_{0}/x} \end{cases} \eta: \end{align*}
