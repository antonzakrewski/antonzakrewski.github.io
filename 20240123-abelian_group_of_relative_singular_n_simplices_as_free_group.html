abelian group of relative singular n-simplices as free group

1. Proposition

Let \((X,A)\) be a pair of spaces, then the abelian group of relative singular n-simplices is the free group of singular n-simplexes

\begin{align*} \Delta_n \rightarrow X \end{align*}

such that \(\Delta_n \not\subseteq A\) is not a subset

2. Proof

Suppose \(\Delta_n \subseteq A\), then \(\Delta_n \in C^{\mathrm{sing}}_n(A)\), hence \(\Delta_n = 0 \in C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X)/C^{\mathrm{sing}}(A)\). Suppose \(\Delta_n \not\subseteq A\), then \(\Delta_n \not\in C^{\mathrm{sing}}_n(A)\) and thus, since no relations are enforced, we get

\begin{align*} C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A) = \mathbb{Z}[ \Delta_n \not\subseteq A] \end{align*}
