ring as algebra over the dual numbers

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be an ring, \(A[X]/(x^2)\) the dual numbers, then \(A\) is an \(A[X]/(x^2)\)-module via

\begin{align*} a + X^2 \cdot a' =& a \cdot a' X \cdot a' =& 0 \end{align*}
