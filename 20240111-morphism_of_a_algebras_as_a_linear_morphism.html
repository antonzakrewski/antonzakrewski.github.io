morphism of A-Algebras as A-linear morphism

1. Proposition

Let \(B,B'\) be A-Algebras and \(\varphi: B \rightarrow B'\) be ring-homomorphisms TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is a morphism of A-Algebras
  2. \(\varphi\) is an R-Algebrahomomorphismus

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

\begin{align*} \varphi(a \cdot b) =& \varphi(a) \cdot \varphi(b) \\ =& \iota'(b) \cdot \varphi(b) \\ \end{align*}

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

concrete category and element chase Let \(a \in A\), then

\begin{align*} \varphi(\iota(a)) =& \varphi(\iota(a) \cdot 1) \\ =& \iota'(a) \cdot \varphi(1) \\ =& \iota'(a) \end{align*}
