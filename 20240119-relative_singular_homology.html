relative singular homology

1. Definition

Let \((X,A)\) be a pair of spaces, then the relative singular homology is defined as the homolog group of the relative singular chain complex

\begin{align*} H_{n}(X,A) = \mathrm{ker}(\partial^{n-1})/\mathrm{im}(\partial^n) \end{align*}
