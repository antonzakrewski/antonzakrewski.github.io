attaching maps of a CW complex

1. Definition

For a CW-Complex \(X\) and a skeletal filtration \((\mathrm{SK}_n)_{n \geq -1}\), the attaching maps are the pushouts

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} S^n \arrow[]{d}{\iota} \arrow[]{r}{}& \mathrm{SK}_n(X) \arrow[]{d}{} \\ D^n \arrow[]{r}{g_{i}^n} & \mathrm{SK}_{n+1} \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

for an indexing set \(I\), a disk \(D^n\) and the n-sphere \(S^n\)
