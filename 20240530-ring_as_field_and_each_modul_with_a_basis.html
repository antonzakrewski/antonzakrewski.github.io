ring as field and each modul with a basis

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a non-zero ring

TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is a field
  2. each \(R\)-module admits a <basis>

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Existenz einer Basis in einem Vektorraum

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Assume \(R\) is not a field, then by

  • existence of a maximal ideal for a strict ideal
  • maximal ideal of a commutative ring and quotient ring as field

there exists a maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{m} \neq (0)\).

Then the quotient ring \(R/\mathfrak{m}\)

\begin{align*} \varphi: R \twoheadrightarrow R/\mathfrak{m} \end{align*}

admits a canonical A-Algebra structure and therefore also an \(A\)-module structure (cf. forgetful functor from A-Algebra to A-Mod)

Note that each element admits $\mathfrak{p}$-torsion hence no set of elements is linear independent
