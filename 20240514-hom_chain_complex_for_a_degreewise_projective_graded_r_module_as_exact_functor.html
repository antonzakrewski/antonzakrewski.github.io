hom chain complex out of a degreewise projective graded R-Module as exact functor

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod, \(P\) a projective graded module as chain complex. Then the covariant hom chain complex functor

\begin{align*} \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(P,-) \end{align*}

is exact

2. Proof

By definition we obtain

\begin{align*} \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(P,-) = \prod_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} \mathrm{Hom}^{\mathrm{Ab}}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(P_n[n],C) \end{align*}

for a suitable shift.

Since category RMod opp as AB4 category and it suffices to show that

\(\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(P_n[n],C)\) is exact (cf. composition of exact functors as exact functor). This follows by covariant ab-hom functor as exact functor and projective object and exact functor induces exact functor on chain complexes
