topological orbital collapse functor

1. Definition

Let \(G\) be a group and \(\mathrm{G-Set}\) be the category of continuous G-Sets Then the topological orbital collapse functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Orb}: \mathrm{G-Set}_{top} \rightarrow& \mathrm{G-Set}_{\mathrm{top}} \\ X \mapsto& X/G \\ (g: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (\overline{g}: X/G \rightarrow Y/G) \end{align*}

where \(X/G\) is the orbital collapse and \(\overline{g}: X/G \rightarrow Y/G\) is the induced set theoretic map on the collapse
