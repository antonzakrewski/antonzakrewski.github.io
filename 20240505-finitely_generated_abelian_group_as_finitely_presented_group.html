finitely generated abelian group as finitely presented group

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be finitely generated abelian group. Then \(A\) is finitely presented.

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • classification of finitely generated abelian groups

and the presentation including

  1. all commutators
  2. \(x^n\) for \(\mathbb{Z}/n \mathbb{Z}\)
