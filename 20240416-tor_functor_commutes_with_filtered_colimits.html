tor functor commutes with filtered colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod. Then for a module \(M\) and a filtered diagram \(N_i\), the Tor functor preserves filtered colimits, i.e.

\begin{align*} \mathrm{colim}_{i} \mathrm{Tor}_n^R(N_i,M) \rightarrow \mathrm{Tor}_n^R (\mathrm{colim}_{i} N_i, M) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

2. Proof

follows from direct sum preserves coeffacability filtered colimits preserve coeffacability left derived functor commutes with coproducts
