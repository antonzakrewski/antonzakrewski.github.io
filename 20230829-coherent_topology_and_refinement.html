coherent topology and refinement

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(\mathcal{O} = (O_i)\) a family of subsets and \(\mathcal{U} = (U_i)\) a refinement of \(\mathcal{O}\). Suppose \(X\) is coherent with \(\mathcal{U}\), then \(X\) is also coherent with \(\mathcal{O}\)
