Accumulation point

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(A \subseteq X\). A point \(x \in X\) is an accumulation point of \(A\), if for every neighbourhood \(U \in \mathcal{U}(x)\) it holds, that:

\begin{align*} (U \setminus \{x\}) \cap A \neq \emptyset \end{align*}
