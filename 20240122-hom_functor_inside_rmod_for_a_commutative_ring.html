hom functor inside RMod for a commutative ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod. then the (internal ?) hom functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{}(-,-): \mathrm{RMod}^{\mathrm{op}} \times \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow& \mathrm{RMod} \\ (M,N) \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,N) \\ ((f: N_1 \rightarrow M_1), (g: M_2 \rightarrow N_2)) \mapsto& (f^{ \rightarrow} \circ g^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}() \end{align*}
