ext for a cohereditary ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a cohereditary ring Then for \(n \geq 2\) the Ext functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_R^n(-,-) = 0 \end{align*}

is the zero functor

2. Proof

follows from

  • right derived functor and injective coresolution of a length

where we may choose by assumption an injective coresolution of length \(1\)
