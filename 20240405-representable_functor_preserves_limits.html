covariant representable functor preserves limits

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a functor. Suppose \(\mathcal{F}\) is representable, then \(\mathcal{F}\) preserves limits.

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • covariant hom-functor preserves small limits
  • natural isomorphism and preserves limit-
