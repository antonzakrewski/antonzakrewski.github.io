1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(X\) a set and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a group action. Then for \(x \in X\), the orbit \(\mathcal{O}_x\) and the stabilizer \(G_x\), there exists the canonical isomorphism as G-Sets

\begin{align*} \psi: G/G_x \rightarrow& \mathcal{O}_x \\ [g] \mapsto& g \cdot x \end{align*}
