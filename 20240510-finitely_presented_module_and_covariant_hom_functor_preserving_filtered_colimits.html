finitely presented module and covariant hom functor preserving filtered colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(M\) an \(R\)-module TFAE:

  1. \(M\) is a finitely presented module
  2. the covariant hom functor \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,-)\) preserves filtered colimits
  3. covariant ab-hom functor \(\mathrm{Hom}^{\mathrm{Ab}}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(M,-)\) preserves filtered colimits

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

  • covariant hom from a finitely presented Module preserves filtered colimits

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
