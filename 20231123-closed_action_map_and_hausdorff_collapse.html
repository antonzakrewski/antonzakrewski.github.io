closed action map and hausdorff collapse

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a topological group, \(X\) a topological space and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a topological group action Suppose the image of the action map,

\begin{align*} A: G \times X \rightarrow& X \times X \\ (g,x) \mapsto& (x, gx) \end{align*}

is closed.

Then the orbital collapse

\begin{align*} X/G \end{align*}

is hausdorff

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • hausdorff quotient space and open quotient map and closed relation
  • projection to the orbits as open map
