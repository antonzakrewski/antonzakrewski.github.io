finitely generated abelian group and dimension as Q Vectorspace

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a finitely generated abelian group.

Then it holds for the rank of an abelian group

\begin{align*} \mathrm{rk}(A) = \mathrm{dim}(A \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} \mathbb{Q}) \end{align*}
