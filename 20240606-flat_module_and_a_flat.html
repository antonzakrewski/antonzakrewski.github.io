flat module and A-flat

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{AMod}\) the category AMod and \(M\) an \(A\)-module

TFAE:

  1. \(M\) is A-flat
  2. \(M\) is flat

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of:

  • module as quotient of a free module
  • M-flat module and sum
  • quotient of an M-flat module

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

special case
