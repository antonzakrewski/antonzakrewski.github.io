singular cohomology ring of the real projective space

1. Proposition

Let \(n \geq 1\), then the singular cohomology ring is given by

\begin{align*} H^*(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{F}_2) \cong P_{\mathbb{F}_2}(w)/(w^{n+1}) \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let \(w \in H^1(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{F}_2) \cong \mathbb{F}_2\) be the non-zero element

Proof byinduction

2.1. induction basis, \(n = 1\)

Follows from

  1. real projective space of dimension 1 homeomorphic to the circle
  2. singular cohomology ring of the sphere

and

\begin{align*} \Lambda_{\mathbb{F}_2}(w) \cong P_{\mathbb{F}_2}(w)/(w^2) \end{align*}

as \(\vert w = \vert = 1\)

2.2. induction step

using inclusion of real projective spaces induces projection of singular cohomology rings
