currying of module homomorphisms as isomorphism of Modules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M,N,L\) \(R\)-modules. Then the map

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{R,R}(M \times N, L) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{R}(N,\mathrm{Hom}_{R}(M,L)) f(\cdot, \cdot) \mapsto& (m \mapsto f(m,\cdot) \end{align*}

from bilinear maps is an Isomorphism of Modules
