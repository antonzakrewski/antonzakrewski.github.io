faithful functor from a balanced category as conservative functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a balanced category and \(\mathcal{F}\) a faithful functor. Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is conservative

2. Proof

Suppose \(f \in \mathrm{Mor}(\mathcal{C})\) is a morphism, such that \(\mathcal{F}(f)\) is an isomorphism. Then \(\mathcal{F}(f)\) is both a monomorphism and an epimorphism and hence by faithful functor reflects epimorphisms and faithful functor reflects monomorphisms it follows, that \(f\) is an bimorphism. Hence since \(\mathcal{C}\) is balanced, we conclude, that \(f\) is an isomorphism
