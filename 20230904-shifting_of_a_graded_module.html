shifting of a graded module

1. Definition / Proposition

Let (R = \bigoplusn ∈ \mathbb{N}0 Rn) be a graded ring and $M = \bigoplusn ∈ \mathbb{N}0 a graded module Then the shifting of \(M\) by \(m \in M\) is defined as graded module

\begin{align*} \bigoplus_{n = m}^{\infty} M_n \end{align*}
