Yoneda Lemma by yoneda embedding

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a concrete category. Then the yoneda embedding

\begin{align*} Yo: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow& \mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C}) \\ A \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,-) \\ (f: A \rightarrow B) \mapsto& (f^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(B,-) - \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(A,-)) \end{align*}

where \(\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C})\) is the category of presheaves is a contravariant fully faithful embedding
