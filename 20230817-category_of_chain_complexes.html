category of bounded below chain complexes

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a pointed category

The category \(\mathrm{Ch}(\mathcal{A})\) of bounded below chain complexes of \(\mathcal{A}\) consists of

  1. bounded below chain complexes chain complexes in \(\mathcal{A}\) as objects
  2. chain morphisms as morphisms
