abelianized cw complex and product

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be based CW-Complexes, \(X = A \vee B\) the wedge product and \(Y_A,Y_B\) some abelianized space of \(A,B\). Then

\begin{align*} Y_A \times Y_B \end{align*}

is an abelianized space of \(X\)

2. Proof

Note that \(A,B\) are deformation retractible in a neighbourhood of the base point <todo> CW complex as locally contractible space

Thus using fundamental group of a wedge for deformation retractible points we get

\begin{align*} \pi_1(A \vee B) \simeq \pi_1(A) \coprod \pi_1(B) \end{align*}

Since left adjoint preserve colimits and abelianization as left adjoint (and isomorphisms under functors), we conclude, that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(A \vee B)) \simeq& \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(A) \coprod \pi_1(B)) \\ \simeq& \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(A)) \coprod \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(B)) \end{align*}

As Category Ab admits biproducts, it follows, that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(A)) \coprod \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(B)) =& \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(A)) \prod \mathrm{Ab}(\pi_1(B)) \\ =& \pi_1(Y_A) \prod \pi_1(Y_B) \end{align*}

Since the homotopy group functor commutes with products, we conclude that

\begin{align*} \pi_1(Y_A) \prod \pi_1(Y_B) \simeq \pi_1(Y_A \times Y_B) \end{align*}

Here the map is given by

\begin{align*} f': A \vee B \rightarrow& Y_A \times Y_B \\ a \mapsto& (f(a), *) \\ b \mapsto& (*,f(b)) \end{align*}
