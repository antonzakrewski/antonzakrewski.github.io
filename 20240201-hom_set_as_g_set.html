hom set as g-set

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group and \(A\) a set. Then the hom-set \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Set}}(G,A)\) is a G-Set with

\begin{align*} g \cdot f(-) \coloneqq f(g \cdot -) \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. neutral element

for arbitrary \(g \in G\) it holds, that

\begin{align*} e \cdot f(g) =& f(e \cdot g) \\ =& f(g) \end{align*}

hence

\begin{align*} e \cdot f(-) = f(-) \end{align*}

2.2. associativity

\begin{align*} g_1 \cdot \left( g_2 \cdot f(-) \right) =& g_1 \cdot f(g_2 \cdot -) \\ =& f(g_1 \cdot g_2 -) \\ =& (g_1 \cdot g_2) \cdot f(-) \end{align*}
