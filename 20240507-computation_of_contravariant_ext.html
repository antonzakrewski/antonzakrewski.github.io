computation of contravariant ext

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category admitting enough projectives. Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}^n_R(M,N) \cong H_{-n}(\underline{Hom}_R(P_*, N[0])) \end{align*}

may be computed choosing a projective resolution \(P_* \rightarrow M\) and computing left derived functor of the opposite functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{A}}(-,N)^{\mathrm{op}}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathrm{Ab}^{\mathrm{op}} \end{align*}

and then taking the opposite category, i.e.

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_R^n(M,N) \cong& (\mathcal{L}(\mathrm{Hom}_{R}(-,N)^{\mathrm{op}}))^{\mathrm{op}}(M) \\ \cong& H_{-n}(\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}_{R}(P_*,N)) \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(-,N) \cong ((\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(-,N))^{\mathrm{op}})^{\mathrm{op}} \end{align*}

and taking the right derived functor is by definition the same as the left derived functor of \(\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(-,N)^{\mathrm{op}}\) and taking again the opposite

Note that finding a projective resolution is easier than finding an injective coresolution
