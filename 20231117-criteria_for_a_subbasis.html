criteria for a subbasis

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(\mathcal{S} \subseteq \mathcal{T}\) be a family of open sets

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{S}\) is a Subbasis
  2. for each open set \(O \subseteq X\) and \(x \in O\) there exist \(U_{1},...,U_n \in \mathcal{S}\) such that
\begin{align*} x \in \bigcap_{i = 1}^n U_i \subseteq O \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

by assumption,

\begin{align*} O = \bigcup_{i \in I} \bigcap_{j = 1}^{m_i} U_{i,j} \end{align*}

Hence choosing \(U_{i,1},...,U_{i,m_i}\) with \(x \in \bigcap_{j = 1}^n U_{i,j}\) suffices

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
