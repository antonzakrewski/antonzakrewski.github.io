retract of cw complexes, and weakly locally contractible

1. Proposition

Let \(X \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n\) be compact. TFAE:

  1. there exists a finite cw-complex \(Y\) and a retract \(Y \rightarrow X\).
  2. \(X\) is semi locally contractible
  3. \(X\) has an open neighbourhood \(U \supseteq X\) such that there is a retraction \(U \rightarrow X\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

  • retract of semi locally contractible space as semi locally contractible space
  • cw complex as locally contractible space
