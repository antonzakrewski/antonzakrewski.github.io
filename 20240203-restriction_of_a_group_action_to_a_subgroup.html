restriction of a group action to a subgroup

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(X\) a set and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a group action. Suppose \(H \hookrightarrow G\) is a subgroup, then there exists a group action

\begin{align*} \varphi_{\vert H}: H \times X \rightarrow X \\ (h,x) \mapsto \varphi(\iota(h), h) \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • composition of group homomorphisms as group homomorphism
  • group action as group homomorphism
