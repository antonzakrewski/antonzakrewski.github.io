additivity of eigenvalues for a coproduct

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(V,W\) vector spaces and \(f: V \rightarrow V, g: W \rightarrow W\) vectorspace-endomorphisms TFAE:

  1. \(\alpha \in K\) is an eigenvalue of \(f \oplus g: V \oplus W \rightarrow V \oplus W\)
  2. \(\alpha \in K\) is an eigenvalue of \(f\) or eigenvalue of \(g\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
