injective module homomorphism and localization at ideals

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring / commutative ring, \(M,N\) \(R\)-module and \(\varphi: M \rightarrow N\) a module-homomorphism.

TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is injective
  2. for each prime ideal \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(R)\), the localization at the ideal \(\varphi_{\mathfrak{p}}: M_{\mathfrak{p}} \rightarrow N_{\mathfrak{p}}\) is injective
  3. for each maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{m} \in \mathrm{Spec}(R)\), the localization at the ideal \(\varphi_{\mathfrak{m}}: M_{\mathfrak{m}} \rightarrow N_{\mathfrak{m}}\) is injective

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

follows from localization functor preserves injections

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

special case

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
