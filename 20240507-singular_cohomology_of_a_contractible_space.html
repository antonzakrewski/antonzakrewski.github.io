singular cohomology of a contractible space

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a contractible space. Then the singular cohomology is given by

\begin{align*} H^n(X,M) \simeq \begin{cases} 0 & \mbox{if } n \geq 1 \\ M & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

\begin{align*} C^{\mathrm{sing}}(*) \simeq \mathbb{Z}[0] \end{align*}

up to chain homotopy equivalence

Hence by

\begin{align*} \simeq \underline{Hom}(\mathbb{Z}[0], M[0]) \end{align*}
