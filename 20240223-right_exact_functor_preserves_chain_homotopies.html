right exact functor preserves chain homotopies

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be additive categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a right exact functor and

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}^*: \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{B}) \end{align*}

be the induced functor on chain complexes.

Then \(\mathcal{F}^*\) preserves chain homotopies

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • right exact functor as additive functor
  • additive functor preserves chain homotopies
