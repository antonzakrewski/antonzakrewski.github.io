infinity categories and join as infinity category

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y\) be simplicial sets. TFAE:

  1. the join \(X \star Y\) is a infinity category
  2. \(X,Y\) are infinity categories

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Suppose there exists a map from an inner horn

\begin{align*} \Lambda_j^n \rightarrow X \star Y \end{align*}
