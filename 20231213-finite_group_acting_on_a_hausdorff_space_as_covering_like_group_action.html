finite group acting on a hausdorff space as covering like group action

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a finite topological group, \(X\) a topological space and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a topological group action. Suppose \(X\) is hausdorff and \(G\) acts freely, then \(G\) is a covering-like group action

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • free and proper action on a locally compact hausdorff space as covering like map

as locally compactness is needed to reduce the case to finitely many \(g_1,...,g_n\)
