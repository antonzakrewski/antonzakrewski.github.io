retractable product with interval homeomorphic to pushout

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(A \subseteq X\) a subspace. Suppose there exists a retraction

\begin{align*} r: X \times [0,1] \rightarrow A \times [0,1] \cup X \times \{0\} \end{align*}

Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{id}_{}: A \times [0,1] \cup X \times \{0\} \rightarrow A \times [0,1] \coprod_{A \times \{0\}} X \times \{0\} \end{align*}

is a homeomorphism
