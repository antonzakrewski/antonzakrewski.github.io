fundamental groupoid functor

1. Definition

Given the category Top and the category of groupoids the fundamental groupoid functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \pi_{\leq 1}(-): \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Grpd} \\ X \mapsto& \pi_{\leq 1}(-) \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& \pi_{\leq \begin{cases} \pi_{\leq 1}: x \mapsto& f(x) \\ ([\lambda: [0,1] \rightarrow X]) \mapsto& ([f \circ \lambda: [0,1] \rightarrow X]) \end{cases} \end{align*}

where \(\pi_{\leq x}\) is the fundamental groupoid
