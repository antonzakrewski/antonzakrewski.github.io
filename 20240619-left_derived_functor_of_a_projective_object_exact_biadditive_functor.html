left derived functor of a projective object exact biadditive functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories with enough projectives and \(\mathcal{C}\) another abelian category. Suppose

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \times \mathcal{B} \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \end{align*}

is a projective object exact biadditive functor.

Then for \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{A}), B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{B})\) and the functors

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_{A} \coloneqq \mathcal{F}(A,-) \mathcal{B} \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \\ \mathcal{F}_{B} \coloneqq \mathcal{F}(-,B) \mathcal{A} \rightarrow& \mathcal{C} \end{align*}

the left derived functor is canonically naturally isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathcal{LF_{A}}_n(B) \cong \mathcal{LF_{B}}_n(A) \end{align*}
