finite coproduct of spectra homoeomorphic to the spectrum of the product

1. Proposition

Let \(A_i\) be commutative rings for \(i \in \{1,...,n\}\) and \(\mathrm{Spec}(A_i)\) the zariski spectrum equipped with the Zariski topology. Then

\begin{align*} \coprod_{i=1}^n \mathrm{Spec}(A_i) \cong \mathrm{Spec}(\prod_{i = 1}^n A_i) \end{align*}

are homeomorphic (see: topologische Summe, direct product of rings)

2. Proof

2.1. bijection

corollary of:

  • prime ideal in a finite product of rings

2.2. continuous

follows from the induced morphism between colimits and functor after consider spec as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Spec}: \mathrm{CRing}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Top} \end{align*}

2.3. closed

by injective image and intersection and image and union it suffices to show this for a basis of closed sets. Hence let \(V(\mathfrak{a}) \subsesetq \mathrm{Spec}(A_i)\) be closed. Then it follows for \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\) that

\begin{align*} \bigoplus R_i \times \mathfrak{a} \subseteq R_i \bigoplus \mathfrak{p} \end{align*}

and analogously that \(\bigoplus R_i \times \mathfrak{a} \subseteq R_i \bigoplus \mathfrak{p}\) implies \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\)

hence

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Spec}(\prod \pi)^{-1}[V(\mathfrak{a})] = V(\mathfrak{a} \oplus \bigoplus R_i) \end{align*}
