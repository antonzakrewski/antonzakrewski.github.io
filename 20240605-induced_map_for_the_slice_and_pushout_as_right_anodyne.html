induced map for the slice and pushout as right anodyne

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B,S,T\) be simplicial sets, \(i: A \rightarrow B, g: S \rightarrow T\) be monomorphisms of simplicial sets, such that \(g\) is right anodyne map Then the canonical map

\begin{align*} \iota \widehat{\star} g: A \star T \coprod_{A \star S} B \star S \rightarrow B \star T \end{align*}

is also right anodyne
