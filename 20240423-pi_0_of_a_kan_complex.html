pi0 of a kan complex and 1-morphism

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a Kan complex. Then for pi0 and \(x, y \in X_0\) TFAE:

  1. \(x \sim y\)
  2. there exists a \($1\)-simplex \(x \rightarrow y\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

it suffices to show, that the existence of a \(1\)-simplex defines an equivalence relation.
