sheaf functor from Mod as fully faithful functor

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(X = \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the affine scheme. Then the sheaf functor from Mod is a fully faithful functor

2. Proof

using associated sheaf of a module isomorphic to the module we get for

\begin{align*} \tilde{f}: \tilde{M} \rightarrow \tilde{N} \end{align*}

the diagram

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathcal{O}_M(X) \arrow[]{d}{\simeq} \arrow[]{r}{g} & \mathcal{O}_N(X) \arrow[]{d}{\simeq}\\ M \arrow[dashed]{r}{g'} & N \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Thus using

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}_X}(\tilde{M}, \tilde{N}) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{A}(M,N) \\ g \mapsto& g' \\ \varphi^{-1}: \mathrm{Hom}_{A}(M,N) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{O}_X}(\tilde{M}, \tilde{N}) \\ f \mapsto& \tilde{f} \end{align*}
