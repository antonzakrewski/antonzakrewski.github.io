set of orientations as torsor over the unit group

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a connected manifold and \(R\) a ring. Then the set of R-Orientations is a g-torsor over the unit group

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • bijection between orientations and natural isomorphisms
  • module automorphisms of a ring as torsor over the units
  • after fixing a natural isomorphism:
    • natural transformations between connected constant diagrams
