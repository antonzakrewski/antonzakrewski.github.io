homotopy group functor commutes with products

1. Proposition

Given category Top* and category Group, and the homotopy group functor,

it commutes with small categorical products

2. Proof

follows from:

  • sphere as locally compact space
  • based mapping space and product as homeomorphism for a locally compact space

hence the canonical map

is a homeomorphism

then we get a factorization

where the functor commutes with product (cf. forgetful functor Grp to Set as right adjoint)

  • functor from Top to the set of path components commuting with products
  • forgetful functor from Grp to Set as conservative Functor
