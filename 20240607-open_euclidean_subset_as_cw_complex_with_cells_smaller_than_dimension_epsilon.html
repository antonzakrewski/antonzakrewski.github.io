open euclidean subset as cw complex with cells smaller than dimension epsilon

1. Proposition

Let \(U \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n\) be an open subset and \(\varepsilon > 0\). Then \(U\) admits a CW-Structure where each Cell has diamater less than \(\varepsilon\).

2. Proofsketch

cover \(U\) by n-cubes with diameter \(\leq \varepsilon\). Keep each cube, which is a subset of \(U\).

Then subdivide each cube, which intersects both \(U\) and \(\mathbb{R}^n \setminus U\). Repeat this process then for \(\varepsilon/4\) etc.
