shrinking of a finite open cover for a t4 space

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a T4- topological space and \(U_1,...,U_n\) a finite open cover. Then there exists a refinement \(V_i \subseteq U_i\) with

\begin{align*} \overline{V}_i \subseteq U_i \end{align*}

2. Proof

Take \(U_1\) and consider \(A_1 \coloneqq X \setminus (\bigcup_{i \neq 1} U_i)\) Then \(A_1 \subseteq U_1\) is closed, so by Äquivalente Charakterisierung eines T4-Raums there exists a closed neighbourhood \(\tilde{V}_i \subseteq U_i\) Taking the interior \(V_1 \coloneqq \mathrm{int}(\tilde{V})_1\) suffices, as \(A_1 \subseteq V_1\) and hence \(V_1,U_2,...,U_n\) covers \(X\).

Repeating this process iteratively with \(V_1\) as new \(U_1\) gives the refinement.
