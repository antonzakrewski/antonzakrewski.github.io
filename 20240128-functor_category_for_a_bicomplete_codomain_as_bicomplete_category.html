functor category for a bicomplete codomain as bicomplete category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, where \(\mathcal{D}\) is bicomplete and \(\mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D})\) the functor category Then \(\mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D})\) is also bicomplete

2. Proof

corollary of

  1. functor category for a cocomplete codomain as cocomplete category
  2. functor category for a complete codomain as complete category
