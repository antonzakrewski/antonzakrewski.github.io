characteristic polynomial of a filtration of a module

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module and \(\mathfrak{q} \subseteq R\) an ideal. Then the characteristic polynomial \(\chi_{\mathfrak{q}}^M \in R[X]\) of \(M\) with respect to \(\mathfrak{q}\) is defined (if it exists ?) as polynomial \(f\) sucht hat for almost all \(n \in \mathbb{N}\), it holds that

\begin{align*} \chi_{\mathfrak{q}}^M(n) = \mathrm{len}(M/\mathfrak{q}^n M) \end{align*}
