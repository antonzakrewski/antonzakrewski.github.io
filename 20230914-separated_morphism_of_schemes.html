separated morphism of schemes

1. Definition

Let \(X,Y\) be schemes and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a morphism of schemes. Then \(f\) is said to be separated, if the diagonal morphism

\begin{align*} \Delta_f \end{align*}

is a closed immersion
