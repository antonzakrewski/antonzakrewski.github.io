free transitive group action and existence of a unique action path

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(X\) a set and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a group action. TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is free and transitive
  2. for \(x_1, x_2 \in X\), there exists a unique \(g \in G\) such that
\begin{align*} g \cdot x_1 = x_2 \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.1.1. existence

follows from definition

2.1.2. uniqueness

follows from free group action and unique action path

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
