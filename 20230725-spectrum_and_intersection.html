vanishing set of a union

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring and \(T_i \subseteq A\) a family of subsets Then

\begin{align*} V( \sum T_i) =& V(\bigcup T_i) \\ =& \bigcap V(T_i) \end{align*}
