geometric realization of the n-cube

1. Proposition

Let \([0,1]^n\) be the closed disk. Then \([0,1]^n\) is the geometric realization of the represented simplicial set \((\Delta^1)^n\)

2. Proof

using geometric realization preserves finite products it suffices to show that

\begin{align*} \vert \Delta^1 \vert = [0,1] \end{align*}

This holds by n-simplex homeomorphic to the n-disk
