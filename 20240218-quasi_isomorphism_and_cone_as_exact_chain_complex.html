quasi isomorphism and cone as exact chain complex

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Suppose \(A_*,B_*\) are chain complexes and \(f: A_* \rightarrow B_*\) a chain complex.

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is a quasi-isomorphism
  2. \(C(f)\) is an exact chain complex

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
