hom chain complex of bimodules

1. Proposition

Let \(R,S,T\) be rings, \(C,D\) be chain complexes of bimodules, where

  1. \(C_1\) is a chain complex of \((S,R)\) bimodules
  2. \(C_2\) is a chain complex of \((R,S)\) bimodules
  3. \(D_1\) is a chain complex of \((T,R)\) bimodules
  4. \(D_2\) is a chain complex of \((R,T)\) bimodules

Then the hom chain complex

  1. \(\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}_{Mod-R}(C_1,D_1)\) is a chain complex of \(S,T\) bimodules
  2. \(\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}_{R-Mod}(C_2,D_2)\) is a chain complex of \(S,T\) bimodules

with the functorality as chain complex of bimodules.

2. Proof

2.1. a)

corollary of:

  • hom chain complex out of a bimodule
  • hom chain complex into a bimodule

2.2. b)

follows from the flip of bimodules
