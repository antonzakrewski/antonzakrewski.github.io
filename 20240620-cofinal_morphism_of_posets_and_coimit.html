cofinal morphism of posets and colimit

1. Proposition

Let \(I,J\) be upward directed partially ordered sets and \(f: I \rightarrow J\) a monotone map, regarded as categories and functors Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be another category and \(\mathcal{D}: I \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) a diagram.

If \(\mathcal{D} \circ f\) admits a colimit, then \(\mathcal{D}\) admits a colimit

\begin{align*} \tau_i: D(i) \rightarrow D(f(j)) \rightarrow \mathrm{colim}_{J} D \circ f$ \end{align*}
