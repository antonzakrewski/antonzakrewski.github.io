projective nullstellensatz

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(J \subseteq K[X_0,...,X_n]\) be a homogeneous ideal.

Then for the projective zero locus and projective vanishing ideal, it holds

  1. \(V(J) = \emptyset \Leftrightarrow \sqrt{J} \supseteq \langle X_0, ..., X_n \rangle\)
  2. \(V(J) \neq \emptyset \Leftrightarrow I(V(J)) = \sqrt{J}\)

2. Proof

2.1. a)

\begin{align*} V(I(X)) = \overline{X} \end{align*}

where \(\overline{X}\) is the closure in the zariski topology

2.2. b) Warnung !

let

\begin{align*} J = K[X_0,...,X_n]_+ = \langle x_0,...,x_n \rangle \end{align*}

It is a maximal ideal, but

\begin{align*} V(J) = \emptyset \end{align*}

where \(\langle X_0,...,X_n\rangle\) is the irrelevant ideal
