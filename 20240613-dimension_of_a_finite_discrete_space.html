dimension of a finite discrete space

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a finite topological space with the discrete topology Then the dimension is given by \(0\)

2. Proof

follows from:

  • empty set as reducible subspace
  • irreducible space as connected space

hence the only chain is given by

\begin{align*} \{x\} \end{align*}
