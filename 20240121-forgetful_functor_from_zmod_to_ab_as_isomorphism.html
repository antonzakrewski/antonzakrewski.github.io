forgetful functor from ZMod to Ab as isomorphism

1. Proposition

Given Category Ab and category RMod for \(R = \mathbb{Z}\), the forgetful functor from RMod to Ab is an Isomorphism functor for

2. Proof

Consider

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ab} \rightarrow \mathrm{\mathbb{Z}Mod} \\ M \mapsto& M \\ (f: M \rightarrow M) \mapsto& (f: M \rightarrow M) \end{align*}

which is by correspondence of abelian groups and Z-Modules and abelian group homomorphism as Z-module homomorphism
