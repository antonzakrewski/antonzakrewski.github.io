distributivity of tensoring and localization

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(M,N\) \(A\)-modules and \(S \subseteq A\) a multiplicatively closed subset. Then following holds for the tensor product

\begin{align*} \left( S^{-1}M \right) \otimes_{S^{-1}A} \left( S^{-1}N \right) \cong S^{-1}(M \otimes_{A} N) \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let

\begin{align*} \varphi: \left( S^{-1}M \right) \otimes_{S^{-1}A} \left( S^{-1}N \right) \rightarrow& S^{-1}(M \otimes_{A} N) \\ (m/s) \otimes (n/t) \mapsto& (m \otimes n)/st \end{align*}
