category of bimodules as ab5 category

1. Proposition

Let (R,S) be rings and \(\mathrm{R-S-Mod}\) the category of bimodules. Then \(\mathrm{R-S-Mod}\) is an Ab5 category

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • category RMod as Ab5 category
  • bimodule as module over a tensored ring
