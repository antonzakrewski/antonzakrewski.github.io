based mapping space and continuous bijection for the wedge product

1. Proposition

Let \((X_i,x_i)\) be a family of based spaces and \((Y,y_0)\) another based space. Then for the Based Mapping space and the categorical coproduct, the wedge product (cf. wedge product as coproduct), the natural map

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_*( \bigwedge_{i \in I} X_i, Y) \rightarrow \prod_{i \in I} \mathrm{Map}_*(X_i, Y) \end{align*}

is a continuous Bijection

2. Proof

2.1. continuous

induced morphism between limits and functor as \(\mathrm{\bigwedge}\) is the limit in \(\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}\)

2.2. bijection

corollary of:

  • contravariant hom-functor dualizes small colimits to limits
