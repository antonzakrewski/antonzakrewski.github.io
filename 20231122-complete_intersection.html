complete intersection

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space. An affine variety \(Y \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) is said to be a complete intersection, if the vanishing ideal \(I(Y)\) is generated by \(n - \mathrm{dim}(Y)\) elements.
