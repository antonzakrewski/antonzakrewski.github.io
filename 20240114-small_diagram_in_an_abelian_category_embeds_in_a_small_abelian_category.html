small diagram in an abelian category embeds in a small abelian category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(I: \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{A}\) a (discrete) small diagram. Then there exists an small abelian subcategory \(\mathcal{A}_{\mathrm{sub}}\) such that \(I\) factorizes.
