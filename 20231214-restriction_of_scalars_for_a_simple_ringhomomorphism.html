simple restriction of scalars for a ringhomomorphism

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be rings and \(\varphi: A \rightarrow B\) a ring-homomorphism. Then \(B\) is canonically an \(A\)-module for

\begin{align*} (a \cdot b) = \varphi(a) \cdot b \end{align*}
