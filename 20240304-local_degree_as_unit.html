local degree as unit

1. Proposition

Let \(f: S^n \rightarrow S^n\) a continuous map, such that for an \(x \in S^n\) the local degree

\begin{align*} \mathrm{deg}_{x}(f) \end{align*}

exists.

Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{deg}_x(f) = \pm 1 \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from multiplicativity of the singular homological degree on the sphere and singular homological degree of the identity on the sphere
