birational iso and precomposition as isomorphism on function fields

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(V,W\) affine varieties and \(f: V \rightarrow W\) be a dominant rational map.

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is an isomorphism in \(\mathrm{AVar}\)
  2. \(f^{ \rightarrow}: \mathcal{O}(W) \rightarrow \mathcal{O}(V)\) is a field-isomorphism and vectorspace-isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • contravariant functor from affine varieties to the function fields as fully faithfull functor
  • fully faithful functor as conservative functor
