free vector space functor

1. Definition

Given category set, a field \(K\) and category vectk, the free vector space functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{FreeVectK}: \mathrm{Set} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Vect}_K \\ A \mapsto& K\langle A\rangle \\ f \mapsto& f^{ K \langle A\rangle} \end{align*}

where \(K \langle A\rangle\) is the K-vectorspace defined by the basis \(A\) and \(f^{ K \langle A\rangle}\) is the linear extension of \(f\)
