opposite category of chain complexes and opposite abelian category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes then there exists an isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})^{\mathrm{op}} \cong \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}^{\mathrm{op}}) \end{align*}
