inverse of a commutator

1. Proposition

Let \((G, \circ)\) be a group, \(g,h \in G\). Then

\begin{align*} [g,h]^{-1} =& [h,g] \\ =& hgh^{-1}g^{-1} \\ \end{align*}

2. Proof

Multiplication results in

\begin{align*} ghg^{-1}h^{-1} \cdot hgh^{-1}g^{-1} =& e \end{align*}
