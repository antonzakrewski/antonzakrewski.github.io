1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(M,N\) finitely generated modules over \(R\). Then the tensor product \(M \otimes_R N\) is also finitely generated

2. Proof

since elementary tensors generate tensor product, follows that

\begin{align*} \varphi: M \times N \twoheadrightarrow M \otimes_R N \\ (m,n) \mapsto& (m \otimes n) \end{align*}

is a epimorphism by surjective image of a finitely generated ideal and finite sum of finitely generated module as finitely generated module
