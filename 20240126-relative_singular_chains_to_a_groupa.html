relative singular chains to a group

1. Definition

Let \((X,A)\) be a pair of spaces and \(M\) a group. Then the relative singular chains is defined as

\begin{align*} C_{*}^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A,M) = C_{*}^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A) \otimes_{\mathbb{Z}} M \end{align*}
