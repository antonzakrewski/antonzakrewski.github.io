cofibration and currying lift

1. Proposition

Let \((A,X)\) be topological spaces and \(\iota: A \rightarrow X\) a continuous map.

TFAE:

  1. \(\iota: A \rightarrow X\) is a cofibration
  2. for each topological space \(Y\) and homotopy \(H: [0,1] \times X \rightarrow Y\) there exists a lift
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{d}{\iota} \arrow[]{r}{H'}& \mathrm{Map}([0,1],Y) \arrow[]{d}{\mathrm{ev}_0} \\ X \arrow[]{r}{f} \arrow[dashed]{ru}{\overline{H}'}& Y \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

where

  1. \(H': A \rightarrow \mathrm{Map}([0,1], Y)\) is the currying of mapping spaces of locally compact spaces as homeomorphism
  2. \(\mathrm{ev}_0\) is evaluation map from a locally compact as continuous map

2. Note

Since \([0,1]\) is locally regular compact, currying is an isomorphism is Set (cf. currying of mapping spaces bijective for locally compact spaces)

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}([0,1] \times X, Y) \simeq X \times \mathrm{Map}([0,1], Y) \end{align*}

Here we choose

\begin{align*} H' \simeq H \end{align*}

with

\begin{align*} H' \in \mathrm{Map}(A, \mathrm{Map}([0,1],Y)) H \in \mathrm{Map}(A \times [0,1],Y)) \end{align*}

3. Proof

3.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Choosing \(H',\overline{H}'\) as indicated in the note suffices.

3.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

evaluating at \(0\) results in

\begin{align*} \overline{H}(x,0) = f(x) \end{align*}

for all \(x \in X\) and commutativity of

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{r}{H'} \arrow[]{d}{\iota}& \mathrm{Map}([0,1],Y) \\ X \arrow[]{ru}{\overline{H}} & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

ensures

\begin{align*} \overline{H}(a,t) = H(a,t) \end{align*}

for all \(a \in A\)
