locally contractible space

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space.

Then \(X\) is said to be locally contractible, if for every point \(x \in X\) and every open neighbourhood \(U\) there exists a neighbourhood \(V \subseteq O\) such that \(V\) is contractible
