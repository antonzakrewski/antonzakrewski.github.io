natural equivalence between groupoids of functor category and 2-pullback

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{Z}\) be the additive group, \(B \mathbb{Z}\) the categorification and \(G\) a groupoid.

Then there exists a naturally equivalence between the functor category \(\mathrm{Fun}(B \mathbb{Z}, \mathcal{G}) \simeq \mathcal{G} \times_{\mathcal{G} \times \mathcal{G}} \mathcal{G}\) for the 2-categorical pullback of
