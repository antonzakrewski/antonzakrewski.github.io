hom chain complex and preserved chain homotopy

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) be the category of chain complexes Suppose \(C,D,E\) are chain complexes, \(\varphi_1, \varphi_2: C \rightarrow D\) chain maps with a chain homotopy \(h: \varphi_1 \simeq \varphi_2\).

Then the precomposition

\begin{align*} \varphi_1^{ \rightarrow}, \varphi_2^{ \rightarrow}: \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(D,E) \rightarrow \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C,E) \end{align*}

are chain homotopic with the homotopy

\begin{align*} h^{ \rightarrow} \in \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(\underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(D,E), \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C,E))_1 \end{align*}

(cf. hom chain complex and chain homotopy as 1-chain )
