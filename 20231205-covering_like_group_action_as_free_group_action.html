covering like group action as free group action

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a topological group, \(X\) a topological space and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a covering-like topological group action

Then \(\varphi\) is a free action.

2. Proof

follows from definition of a covering-like map, as \(g \cdot x = x\) if and only if for a set \(U\) containing \(x\)

\begin{align*} gx = x \in gU \cap U \neq \emptyset \end{align*}

But by assumption, \(gU \cap U \neq \emptyset\) iff \(g = e\)
