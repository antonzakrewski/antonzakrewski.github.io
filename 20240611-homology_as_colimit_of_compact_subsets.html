homology as colimit of compact subsets

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(K \in K_{X}\) the directed poset of compact subspaces. Then

\begin{align*} H_*(X) \cong \mathrm{colim}_{C \in \mathcal{K}_X} H_*(C) \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from:

  • singular chains as colimit of compact subsets
  • homology functor for an ab5 category commutes with filtered colimits
