category of matrices

1. Definition

Let \(R\) be a ring. Then the category of matrices is defined as category containing

  1. natural numbers \(n \in \mathbb{N}_0\) as objects
  2. \(m \times n\) matrices as morphism \(n \rightarrow m\)

and matrix multiplication as composition and the identity matrix as identity morphism
