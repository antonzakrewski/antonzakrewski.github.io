ext for a hereditary ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a hereditary ring Then for \(n \geq 2\) the Ext functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ext}_R^n(-,-) = 0 \end{align*}

is the zero functor

2. Proof

follows from

  • right derived functor and injective resolution of a length

where each injective coresolution in \(\mathrm{RMod}^{\mathrm{op}}\) is a projective resolution, hence may be chosen of length \(1\) (cf. hereditary ring and projective resolution of length 1)
