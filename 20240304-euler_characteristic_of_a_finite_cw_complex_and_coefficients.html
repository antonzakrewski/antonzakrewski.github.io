euler characteristic of a finite cw complex and field as coefficients

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a finite cw-complex, \(K\) a field and \(\chi_{\mathrm{hom}}(X)\) the homological euler characteristic (resp. combinatorial euler characteristic of a cw-complex). Then it holds, that

\begin{align*} \chi_{\mathrm{hom}}(X) = \sum_{n \geq 0} (-1)^{n} \mathrm{dim}_K(H_n(X;K)) \end{align*}
