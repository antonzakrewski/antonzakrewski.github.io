conjugation with isomorphism as isomorphism on automorphism groups

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a locally small category, \(\varphi: X \rightarrow Y\) an isomorphism. Then conjugation

\begin{align*} \varphi_{#}: \mathrm{Aut}_{\mathcal{C}}(X) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Aut}_{\mathcal{C}}(Y) \\ f \mapsto& \varphi \circ f \circ \varphi^{-1} \end{align*}

is an group-isomorphism on the automorphism groups

2. Proof

2.1. group homo

Follows from

\begin{align*} \varphi_{#}(f \circ g) =& \varphi \circ f \circ g \circ \varphi^{-1} \\ =& \varphi \circ f \circ \mathrm{id}_{X} \circ g \circ \varphi^{-1} \\ =& \varphi \circ f \circ \varphi^{-1} \circ \varphi \circ g \circ \varphi^{-1} \\ =& \varphi_{#}(f) \circ \varphi_{#}(g) \end{align*}

2.2. iso

follows from

\begin{align*} \varphi^{-1}_{#}( \varphi_#(f)) = \varphi^{-1}_#( \varphi \circ f \circ \varphi^{-1}) \\ =& \varphi^{-1} \circ \varphi \circ f \varphi^{-1} \circ \varphi \\ =& \mathrm{id}_{X} \circ f \circ \mathrm{id}_{Y} \\ =& f \end{align*}
