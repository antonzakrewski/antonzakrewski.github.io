locally finite cw-complex

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathrm{SK}_n(X))\) be a CW-Complex.

Then \(X\) is said to be locally finite, if each cell \(\beta_{i}[D^{n+1}] \subseteq X\) is disjoint from almost all other cells of a cw-complex
