unit group functor of an algebra

1. Definition

Given the category of A-Algebra and Category Ab, the unit group functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Forget}: \mathrm{A-Alg} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ab} \\ (p: A \rightarrow B) \mapsto& B^{\times} \\ (f: (p: A \rightarrow B) \rightarrow (p': A \rightarrow C)) \mapsto& f_{\vert B^{\times}}: B^{\times} \rightarrow C^{\times} \end{align*}
