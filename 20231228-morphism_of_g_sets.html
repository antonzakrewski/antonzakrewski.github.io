morphism of G-Sets

1. Definition

Let \(G\) be a group, \((X,\varphi)\) and \((Y,\psi)\) be G-Sets. Then a morphism is a map

\begin{align*} f: X \rightarrow Y \end{align*}

such that

\begin{align*} \psi(g,f(x)) = f(\varphi(g,x)) \end{align*}
