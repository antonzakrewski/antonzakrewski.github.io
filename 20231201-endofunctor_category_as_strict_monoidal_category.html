endofunctor category as strict monoidal category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category.

Then the endofunctor category \(\mathrm{End}(\mathcal{C}\) is a strict monoidal category

2. Proof

Let

  1. composition of functors as tensor functor
\begin{align*} (\circ): \mathrm{End}(\mathcal{C}) \times \mathrm{End}(\mathcal{C}) \rightarrow& \mathrm{End}(\mathcal{C}) \\ (\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}) \mapsto& \mathcal{F} \circ \mathcal{G} \end{align*}

be the tensor functor.

  1. \(\mathrm{id}_{\mathcal{C}}\) be the tensor unit
  2. left unitor, right unitor and associator as identity
