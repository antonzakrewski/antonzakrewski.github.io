category of commutative graded Algebras

1. Definition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a commutative graded ring. Then the category of commutative graded Algebras is defined as the full subcategory of the category of graded algebras containing commutative graded rings as codomain
