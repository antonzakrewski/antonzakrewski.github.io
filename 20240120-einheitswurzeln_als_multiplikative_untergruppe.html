roots of unity as group

1. Satz

Let \(\overline{K}\) be an algebraically closed field and \(U_n\) the root of unities for \(n \in \mathbb{N}^+\) Then \(U_n\) is a multiplicative subgroup

2. Beweis

Kriterium für eine Untergruppe

2.1. nonempty

follows from \(1^n = 1\) and hence \(1 \in U_n\)

2.2. multiplication

Let \(a,b \in U_n\), then

\begin{align*} (a \cdot b^{-1})^n =& a^n \cdot \left(b^{-1} \right)^n \\ =& 1 \cdot \left(b^n \right)^{-1} \\ =& 1^{-1} \\ =& 1 \end{align*}
