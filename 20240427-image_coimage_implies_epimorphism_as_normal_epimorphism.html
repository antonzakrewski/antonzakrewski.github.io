image coimage implies epimorphism as normal epimorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a pre-abelian category such that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{im}(f) \cong \mathrm{coim}(f) \end{align*}

(aka. abelian category)

Then each epimorphism is a normal epimorphism

2. Proof

follows dually from image coimage implies monomorphism as normal monomorphism
