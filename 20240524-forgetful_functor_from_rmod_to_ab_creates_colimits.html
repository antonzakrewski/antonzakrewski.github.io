forgetful functor from RMod to Ab creates colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Forget}: \mathrm{RMod} \rightarrow \mathrm{Ab}\) the forgetful functor from RMod to Ab. Then \(\mathrm{Forget}\) creates colimits

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • forgetful functor from RMod to Ab as conservative functor
  • forgetful functor from RMod to Ab as left adjoint
  • conservative left adjoint creates colimits
  • Category RMod as bicomplete category
