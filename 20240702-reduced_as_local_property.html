reduced as local property

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring.

TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is a reduced ring
  2. \(A_{\mathfrak{p}}\) is a reduced ring for \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}(A)\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

  • localization of a reduced ring as reduced ring

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

using localization and radical we conclude that

\begin{align*} \mathcal{N}_{S^{-1}A} =& \sqrt{S^{-1}(0)} \\ =& S^{-1}\sqrt{(0)} \\ =& S^{-1}(0) \\ =& \left(\frac{0}{1}\right) =& (0) \end{align*}
