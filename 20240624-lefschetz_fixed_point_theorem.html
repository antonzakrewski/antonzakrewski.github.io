lefschetz fixed point theorem

1. Proposition

Let \(K \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n\) be a compact, semi-locally contractible subspace and \(f: K \rightarrow K\) a continuous map, which is fixed point free

Then the lefschetz number is given by

\begin{align*} \Lambda(f) = 0 \end{align*}
