skyscraper sheaf functor

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(x \in X\) a point and \(\mathcal{C}\) a complete category. Then the skyscraper sheaf functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Sky}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Sh}(X) \\ A \mapsto& \iota_* \circ \mathrm{singleton}(A) \\ f \mapsto& \iota_* \circ \mathrm{singleton}(f) \\ \end{align*}

where

  1. \(\mathrm{singleton}\) is the singleton sheaf functor
  2. and \(\iota_*\) is the direct image sheaf functor

resp. as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Sky}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathrm{Psh}(X) \end{align*}

as composition with the forgetful functor from PSh(X) to Sh(X)
