ring homomorphism for the scalar multiplication

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring and \(M\) an \(A\)-module. Then the map

\begin{align*} \varphi: A \rightarrow& \mathrm{End}_{\mathbb{Z}}(M) \\ a \mapsto& (a \mapsto& a \cdot m) \end{align*}

is a ring-homomorphism
