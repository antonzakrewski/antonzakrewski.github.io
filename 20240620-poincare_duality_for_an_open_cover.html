poincare duality lemma for an open cover

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be an \(n\)-dimensional manifold, \(U_1 \cup U_2 = M\) an open cover Suppose furthermore \(\sigma_M \in H_n^{\mathrm{lf}}(M;R)\) is an R-Orientation such that

\begin{align*} - \cap \sigma_{U_i}: H^n_c(U_i;R) \rightarrow& H_{n-p}^{\mathrm{lf}}(U_i;R) \\ - \cap \sigma_{U_1 \cap U_2}: H^n_c(U_1 \cap U_2;R) \rightarrow& H_{n-p}^{\mathrm{lf}}(U_1 \cap U_2;R) \\ \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

Then

\begin{align*} - \cap \sigma_{M}: H^n_c(M;R) \rightarrow& H_{n-p}^{\mathrm{lf}}(M;R) \end{align*}

is also an isomorphism

2. Proof
