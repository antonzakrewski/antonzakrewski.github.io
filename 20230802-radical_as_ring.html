radical as Ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring and \(\mathfrak{a}\) an ideal TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{a} = R\)
  2. \(\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}} = R\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of radical of an ideal and the ideal

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

By assumption, there exists an \(n \in \mathbb{N}\) such that \(1^n = 1\in \mathfrak{a}\). Hence von einer Einheit erzeugtes Ideal und triviales Ideal
