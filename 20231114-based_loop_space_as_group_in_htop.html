based loop space as group in hTop*

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space and \(\Omega_{x_0}(X)\) the based loop space. Then \(\Omega_{x_0}(X)\) is a group object in Category hTop

2. Proof

By definition, we get

\begin{align*} \Omega_{x_0}(X) = \mathrm{Map}_{*}(S^1,X) \end{align*}

Then, since \(\mathrm{Map}_*(-,-)\) is homotopy invariant (cf. contravariant based compact open functor as homotopy invariant functor)

  1. group object and functor preserving finite limits
  2. contravariant based compact open functor dualizes finite coproducts

it follows, that the group object in \(\mathrm{hTop}^{\mathrm{\mathcal{C}}\) is preserved
