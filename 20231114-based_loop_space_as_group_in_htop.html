based loop space as group in hTop*

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space and \(\Omega_{x_0}(X)\) the based loop space

2. Proof

\begin{align*} \Omega_{x_0}(X) = \mathrm{Map}_{*}(S^1,X) \end{align*}

and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_{*}(S^1,X) \times \mathrm{Map}_{*}(S^1,X) \simeq \mathrm{Map}(S^1 \vee S^1, X) \end{align*}

<product mapping spaces> and the multiplication, precomposition with the comultiplication in \(S^1\)

unit map as precomposition with the unit

these maps satisfy unitality and associativity up to homotopy

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_{*}(S^1,-) \end{align*}

preserves pointed homotopies

see: precomposition with homotopic maps for the based mapping space
