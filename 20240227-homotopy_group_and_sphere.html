element of a homotopy group and map out of the sphere

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(\pi_n(X,x)\) the \(n\)-th homotopy group. Then there exists a natural correspondence between elements

  1. \(g \in \pi_n(X,x)\)
  2. pointed maps \(f: (S^n, \rightarrow X\) up to homotopy relative to \(1\).
  3. maps \(f: D^n \rightarrow X\) with \(f[\partial D^n] = \{x\}\) up to homotopy rel \(\partial D^n\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

follows from

\begin{align*} S^n = D^n / (\partial D^n) \end{align*}

as topological collapse and the universal property

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} D^n \arrow[]{r}{\pi} \arrow[swap]{rd}{f} & S^n \arrow[dashed]{d}{f'}\\ & X \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)
