unit group functor

1. Definition

Given category CRing and category Group, the unit group functor is defined

\begin{align*} \mathrm{UnitGrp}: \mathrm{CRing} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Grp} \\ R \mapsto& R^{\times} \\ (\varphi: R \rightarrow R') \mapsto& \varphi_{\vert R^{\times}}: R^{\times} \rightarrow R^{\times} \end{align*}
