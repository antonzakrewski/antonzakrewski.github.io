flat module over a dedekind domain as torsion free module

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a dedekind domain, \(M\) a \(A\)-module TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is flat module
  2. \(A\) is torsion-free

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
