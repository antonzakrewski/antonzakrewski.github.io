Tangens - Definitionslücken

1. Satz

\begin{align*} f(x) \coloneqq& \tan{(0.5 \pi + x \cdot \pi)} \\ \mathbb{D}_{f} =& \mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

2. Beweis

  • Division durch Null
\begin{align*} \tan(x) =& \frac{\sin(x)}{\cos(x)} \\ \cos(x) = 0 \Leftrightarrow \tan(x) =& \frac{\sin(x)}{0} = undef \end{align*}
