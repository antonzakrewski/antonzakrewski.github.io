initial morphism in general neither epi nor mono

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) and \(\emptyset\) the initial object. Then the initial morphism

\begin{align*} \emptyset \rightarrow A \end{align*}

is in general neither epi nor mono

2. Proof

is dual to

  • terminal morphism in general neither epi nor mono

cf. Duality of Epimorphism and Monomorphism
