Category Rmod as abelian category

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod. Then \(\mathrm{RMod}\) is an abelian category

2. Proof

  1. zero object in RMod
  2. Category RMod as bicomplete category
  3. Homomorphiesatz für Moduln / abelian category and coimage isomorphic to image
