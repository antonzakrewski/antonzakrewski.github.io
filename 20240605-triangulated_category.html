triangulated category

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an additive category Then \(\mathcal{A}\) is said to be a triangulated category, if it is equipped with

  1. translation functor
  2. a family of exact triangles satisfiyng following axioms

a) TR1 of a triangulated category b) rotation of a triangulated category c) TR3 of a triangulated category d) octahedral axiom of a triangulated category
