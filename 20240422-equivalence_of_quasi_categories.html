natural equivalence of quasi categories

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be simplicial sets, \(Y\) a quasicategory and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) is a morphism of simplicial sets. Then \(f\) is said to be a natural equivalence, if it is an equivalence in the inner hom

\begin{align*} \underline{Hom}(K,Y) \end{align*}
