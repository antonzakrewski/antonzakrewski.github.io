euclidean space as contractible space

1. Proposition

The euclidean space \(\mathbb{R}^n\) is contractible

2. Proof

Given by

\begin{align*} H: \mathbb{R}^n \times [0,1] \rightarrow& \mathbb{R}^n \\ ((x_1,...,x_n),t) \mapsto& (1 - t) \cdot (x_1,...,x_n) \end{align*}
