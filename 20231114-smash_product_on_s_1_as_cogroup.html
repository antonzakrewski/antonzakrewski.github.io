smash product on S1 as cogroup

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0)\) be a based space and \(S^1\) the pointed 1-sphere Then the \(S^1 \wedge X\) is a cogroup

2. Proof

by the distributivity of the smash and wedge product, the smash product functor preserves categorical coproducts (cf. wedge product as coproduct of based spaces) Furthermore, the smash product preserves the terminal object, hence by

smash product and homotopic maps

\begin{align*} S^1 \wedge X \rightarrow (S^1 \vee S^1) \wedge X \cong (S^1 \wedge X) \vee (S^1 \wedge X) \end{align*}
