galois covering and collapse to the deck

1. Proposition

Let \(E,B\) be topological spaces and \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a galois covering map, \(E\) is connected and \(B\) is locally path connected Then the orbital collapse

\begin{align*} p': E/\mathrm{Deck}(p) \rightarrow B \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • injective local homeomorphism as homeomorphism for the image
  • collapse to the deck acting covering like

where

\begin{align*} p'^{-1}[b] = \{[e]\} \end{align*}

is a singleton, as \(\mathrm{Deck}(p)\) is a transitive group action.
