fully faithful left adjoint and unit as isomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{L}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) a left adjoint

  1. \(\mathcal{L}\) is fully faithful
  2. the unit is an isomorphism
