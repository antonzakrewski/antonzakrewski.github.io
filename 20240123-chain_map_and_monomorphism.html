chain map and monomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Suppose \(M_n,M_n'\) are chain complexes and \(\iota: M_n \rightarrow M_n'\) a chain map.

TFAE:

  1. \(\iota\) is a monomorphism
  2. each component \(\iota_n: M_n \rightarrow M_n'\) is a monomorphism

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
