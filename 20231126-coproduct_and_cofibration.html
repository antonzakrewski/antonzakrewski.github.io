coproduct and cofibration

1. Proposition

Let \(\iota: A_n \rightarrow X_n\) be cofibrations. Then

\begin{align*} \coprod \iota_n: \coprod_n A_n \rightarrow \coprod_n X_n \end{align*}

is also a cofibration

2. Proof

cofibration and retraction:

by assumption, there exists retractions

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Ret}_n: X_n \times [0,1] \rightarrow& X_n \times \{0\} \cup A_n \times [0,1] \end{align*}

applying the glueing lemma for an open covering, shows, that the natural map

\begin{align*} \coprod \mathrm{Ret}_n: (\coprod X_n ) \times [0,1] \rightarrow (\coprod X_n) \times \{0\} \cup (\coprod A_n) \times [0,1] (X_n,t) \mapsto \mathrm{Ret}_n(X_n,t) \end{align*}

results in the desired retraiction
