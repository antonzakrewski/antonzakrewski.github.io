homotopy group functor as homotopy invariant functor

1. Proposition

Given category Top*, the homotopy group functor

\begin{align*} \pi_n: \mathrm{Top}_* \rightarrow& \mathrm{Grp} \\ (X,x) \mapsto& \pi_n(X,x) \end{align*}

is a homotopy invariant functor

2. Proof

follows from:

  • precomposition with homotopy invariant functor as homotopy invariant functor
  • contravariant based compact open functor as homotopy invariant functor

where \(\pi_n\) is defined as functor composition

\begin{align*} \pi_n = \pi_0 \circ \mathrm{Map}((S^n,1), (X,x)) \end{align*}
