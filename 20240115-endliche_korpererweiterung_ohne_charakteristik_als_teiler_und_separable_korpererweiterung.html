finite field extension without characteristic as divisor and separable field extension

1. Proposition

Let \(K\) be a field of positive characteristic \(p = \mathrm{char}(K)\) and \(L/K\) as a finite field extension. Suppose for the degree \([L : K]\) it holds, that

\begin{align*} p \not\mid [L : K] \end{align*}

Then \(L/K\) is a separable field extension.

2. Proof

Using Separabilitätsgrad gleich Grad und endliche, separable körpererweiterung it suffices to show, that

\begin{align*} [L : K] = [L : K]_s \end{align*}

By Separabilitätsgrad und Charakteristik teilt Grad für eine endliche Körpererweiterung there exists an \(n \in \mathbb{N}_0\) such that

\begin{align*} p^n \cdot [L : K]_s = [L : K] \end{align*}

since \(p \not\mid [L : K]\) it follows, that \(n = 0\) and hence

\begin{align*} [L : K]_s = [L : K] \end{align*}

showing that \(L/K\) is separable
