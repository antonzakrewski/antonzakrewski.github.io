length of a resolution

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} ... P_2 \arrow[]{r}{} & P_{1} \arrow[]{r}{} & A \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

a resolution

Then the length is defined as minimal \(n \in \mathbb{N} \cup \{\infty\}\) such that

\begin{align*} P_{n'} = 0 \end{align*}

for \(n' \geq n\) (? >)
