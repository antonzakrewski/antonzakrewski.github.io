quotient chain complex

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complex. Suppose \(M_n\) is a chain complex and \(\iota: U_n \rightarrow M_n\) a chain subcomplex.

Then the quotient complex is defined as cokernel

\begin{align*} \mathrm{coker}(\iota) \end{align*}
