deck action in general not free

1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map and \(\mathrm{Deck}(p)\) the deck transformation. Then \(p\) is in general not free

2. Proof

Let \(E = \{1,2,3\}\) with the indiscrete topology and \(B = \{*\}\) for the trivial covering map. Then we get the symmetric group \(S_3\) as deck transformation. Furthermore, \(\varphi((1,2),3) = 3\) hence \(\mathrm{Deck}(p)\) is not free
