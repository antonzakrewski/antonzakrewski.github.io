topological space as presheaf

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, then \(X\) is a presheaf from \(\mathrm{Open}_X \rightarrow \mathrm{Top}\) from the category of open Subsets as cofiltered category to category Top sending

  1. an Open set \(O \in \mathrm{Open}_X\) to the subspace \(O\)
  2. an restriction \(O \rightarrow O'\) to the inclusion of subspaces \(\iota: O' \rightarrowtail O\)
