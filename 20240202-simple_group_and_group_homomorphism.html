simple group and group homomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group. TFAE:

  1. each group-homomorphism is either injective or the zero morphism
  2. \(G\) is a simple group

2. Proof

follows from kernel as normal subgroup, where for \(\varphi: G \rightarrowtail H\) injective is equivalent to \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi) = 1\) and \(0: G \rightarrow H\) with \(\mathrm{ker}(0) = G\)
