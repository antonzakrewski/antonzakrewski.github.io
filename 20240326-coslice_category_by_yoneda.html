1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a locally small category. Then for a set-valued presheaf \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set}\) there exists the associated slice category

\begin{align*} \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F} \end{align*}
