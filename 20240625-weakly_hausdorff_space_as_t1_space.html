weakly hausdorff space as T1 space

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a weakly hausdorff space Then \(X\) is a T1 space

2. Proof

using Äquivalente Charakterisierung eines T1-Raums it suffices to show that each point \(x \in X\) is a closed set. Then this follows from the continuous map

\begin{align*} \mathrm{const}: \{x\} \hookrightarrow X \end{align*}

where \(\{x\}\) is trivially compact and hausdorff
