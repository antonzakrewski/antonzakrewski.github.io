infinite real projective space

1. Definition

Let \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) be the real projective space. Then \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^{\infty}\) is defined as filtered colimit

\begin{align*} \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^{\infty} \coloneqq \mathrm{colim}_{n \in \mathbb{N}} \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n \end{align*}
