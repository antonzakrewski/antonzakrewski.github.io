homotopy category of simplicial sets

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a simplicial set. Then the homotopy category \(hX\) is defined as category containing

  1. \(X_0 = X([0])\) as objects
  2. for \(x_0 \rightarrow x_1 \in X_0\) the morphisms are generated by 1-simplices \(f: \Delta^1 \rightarrow X\) with \(x_0 = d_1(f), x_1 = d_0(f)\) and following relations

a) \(s_0(x)\) as identity morphism b) for every 2-simplex \(\sigma: \Delta^2 \rightarrow X\) with boundary \((f,g,h)\) there exists the relation

\begin{align*} h = g \circ f \end{align*}

c) the relation is composition compatible, i.e.

\begin{align*} f_1 \sim f_2, g_1 \sim g_2 \Rightarrow f_1 \circ f_2 \sim g_1 \sim g_2 \end{align*}
