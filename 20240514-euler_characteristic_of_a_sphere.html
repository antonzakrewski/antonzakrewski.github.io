euler characteristic of a sphere

1. Proposition

the homological euler characteristic of the n-sphere is given by

\begin{align*} \chi(S^n) = \begin{cases} 2 & \mbox{if } n \equiv 0 \mod{2} \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • singular homology of a 0-sphere
  • singular homology of a sphere

and the definition of the homological euler characteristic
