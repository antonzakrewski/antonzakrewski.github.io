homogeneous ideal as graded submodule

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring and \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq R\) an ideal TFAE:

  1. \(\mathfrak{a}\) is a homogeneous ideal
  2. \(\mathfrak{a}\) is a graded submodule over \(R\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
