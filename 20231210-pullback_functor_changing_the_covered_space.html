pullback functor changing the covered space

1. Proposition

Let \(B,B',E\) be topological spaces, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map and \(f: B' \rightarrow B\) a continuous map. Then \(f\) induces a functor

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_f: \mathrm{Cov}(B) \rightarrow \mathrm{Cov}(B') \end{align*}

for the category of covering maps

2. Proof

corollary of:

  1. pullback and covering map
  2. pullback induces functor for slice category
  3. category Top as complete category

where 1) ensures, that the objects are welldefined
