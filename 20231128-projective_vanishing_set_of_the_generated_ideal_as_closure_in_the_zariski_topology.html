projective vanishing set of the generated ideal as closure in the zariski topology

1. Proposition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(\mathbb{P}_F^n\) the projective space, \(A \subseteq \mathbb{P}_F^n\) a subset

Then for the projective zero locus \(Z\) and the projective vanishing ideal \(I\), it holds that

\begin{align*} Z(I(A)) = \overline{A} \end{align*}

where \(\overline{A}\) is the closure in the projective zariski topology
