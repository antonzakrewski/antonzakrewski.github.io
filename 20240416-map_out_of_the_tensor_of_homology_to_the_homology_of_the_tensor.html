map out of the tensor of homology to the homology of the tensor

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}, \mathrm{ModR}\) the category RMod. Then for the tensor product of chain complexes functor and the general tensor functor there exists a natural

\begin{align*} \mu: H(C) \otimes_{R} H(D) \rightarrow H(C \otimes_R D) \end{align*}

where \(C \in \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod}), D \in \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{ModR})\) are chain complexes

2. Proof

2.1. a)

Follows from

  • tensor with a boundary and element as boundary
  • tensor product of cycles gives cycle of tensor product

2.2. alternatively

  • FHHF for right exact
  • tensor functor as right exact functor
