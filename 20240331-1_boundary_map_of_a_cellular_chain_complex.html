1-boundary map of a cellular chain complex

1. Definition

Let \((X, \emptyset, \mathrm{SK}_n(X))\) be a CW-Complex. Then for the 1-boundary map on the cellular chain complexes is given by

\begin{align*} \varphi: C^{\mathrm{cell}}_{1} \rightarrow& C_{0}^{\mathrm{cell}} \\ \sigma \mapsto& \sigma(0) - \sigma(1) \end{align*}
