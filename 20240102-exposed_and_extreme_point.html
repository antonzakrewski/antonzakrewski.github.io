closure of exposed points as extreme point

1. Proposition

Let \(M \subseteq V\) be a convex set for \(V\) as normiert real vector space. Then it holds, that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{cl}{\mathrm{Exp}(M)} = \mathrm{Ext}(M) \end{align*}

where \(\mathrm{Exp}\) are the exposed point and \(\mathrm{Ext}\) the extreme points.
