integral closure and localization

1. Proposition

Let \(A \hookrightarrow B\) be a commutative ring extension and \(C \hookrightarrow B\) the integral closure. Suppose \(S \subseteq A\) is a multiplicatively closed subset, then \(S^{-1}C\) is the integral closure of

\begin{align*} S^{-1} A \hookrightarrow S^{-1}B \end{align*}
