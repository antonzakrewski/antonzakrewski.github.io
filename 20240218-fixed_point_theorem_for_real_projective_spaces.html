fixed point theorem for real projective spaces

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{RP}^n\) be the real projective space.

TFAE:

  1. \(n\) is even
  2. each continuous map
\begin{align*} g: \mathbb{RP}^n \rightarrow \mathbb{RP}^n \end{align*}

has a fixed point

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
