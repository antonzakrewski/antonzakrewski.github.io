boundary of a standard simplex

1. Definition

Let \(\Delta^n\) be the standard n-simplex. Then the boundary is defined as sub simplicial set \(\partial \Delta^n \subseteq \Delta^n\) where

\begin{align*} (\partial \Delta^n)_k = \{f \in (\Delta^n)_k \vert f \text{non surjective}\} \end{align*}
