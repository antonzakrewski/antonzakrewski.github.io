local ring homomorphism

1. Definition

Let \(A,B\) be local rings with maximal ideals \(\mathfrak{m}_A \subseteq A, \mathfrak{m}_B \subseteq B\) A local ring homomorphism is a ring homomorphism \(\varphi: \mathfrak{m}_A \rightarrow \mathfrak{m}_B\) such that

\begin{align*} \varphi[\mathfrak{m}_A] \subseteq \mathfrak{m}_B \end{align*}
