precomposition with a continuous map induces contravariant functor on sheafs

1. Proposition

Given category Top \(\mathrm{Top}\) and Category Cat \(\mathrm{Cat}\), precomposition induces a contravariant functor for presheaves for a category \(\mathcal{Z}\)

\begin{align*} {}^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Top} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Cat} \\ X \mapsto& \mathrm{Fun}(\mathrm{Op}_X,Z) \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& \left(\mathcal{F}_f^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Fun}(\mathrm{Op}_Y,Z) \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(\mathrm{Op}_X,Z)\right) \end{align*}
