M-flat and flat for cyclic submodules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(N,M\) \(R\)-modules.

TFAE:

  1. \(N\) is M-flat
  2. \(N\) is \(M')\)-flat for cyclic submodules \(M' = \langle m\rangle\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

follows from:

  • M-flat for a submodule

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

follows from:

  • Mi-flatness and flat over the sum
  • quotient-flatness of an M-flat module

where

module as quotient of the sum of cyclic submodules
