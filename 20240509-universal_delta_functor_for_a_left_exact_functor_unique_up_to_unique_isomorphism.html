universal delta functor for a left exact functor unique up to unique isomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories and \(\mathcal{F}_0: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a left exact functor Then a universal delta functor extending \(\mathcal{F}_0\) is, if it exists, unique up to unique natural isomorphism

2. Proof

follows dually to universal delta functor for a right exact functor unique up to unique isomorphism
