quasi coherent sheaf of modules and affine part as associated sheaf

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be scheme and \(\mathcal{F}\) an \(\mathcal{O}_X\) sheaf of modules

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}\) is quasicoherent
  2. for eahc open subset \(O \subseteq X\) with \(X_{O} \simeq \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) there exists an \(A\)-module \(M\) such that for the associated sheaf of modules functor from Mod and the restriction of a sheaf to an open set
\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}_{\vert O} \simeq \tilde{M} \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
