domain of a rational function_

1. Definition

Let \(F\) be an algebraically closed field, \(V,W\) affine varieties and

\begin{align*} f: V \rightarrow W \end{align*}

a rational function.

Then the domain of \(f\) is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{dom}(f) = \bigcup_{(U,f)} U \end{align*}
