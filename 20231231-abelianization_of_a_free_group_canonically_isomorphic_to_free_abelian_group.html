1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a set, \(\mathbb{Z}(X)\) the free abelian group and \(F(X)\) the free group. Then the Abelianization \(\mathrm{Ab}(F(X))\) is canonical isomorphic

\begin{align*} \mathrm{F}(X) \cong \mathrm{Ab}(\mathbb{Z}(X)) \end{align*}

  1. free abelian group functor as left adjoint
