Z/2Z homology of the klein bottle

1. Proposition

let \(K\) be the klein bottle, then the singular homology with coefficients \(\mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}\) is given by

\begin{align*} H_k(K, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \cong \begin{cases} \mathbb{F}_2 & \mbox{if } k = 0\\ \mathbb{F}_2 \oplus \mathbb{F}_2 & \mbox{if } k=1 \\ \mathbb{F}_2 & \mbox{if } k = 2\\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

using universal coefficient formula for singular homology and homology of the klein bottle

2.1. \(k = 0\)

corollary of

  • zeroth homology with coefficients

2.2. \(k = 1\)

Then \(H_{0}(K) = \mathbb{Z}\) is flat, hence \(\mathrm{Tor}_1^{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) = 0\)

\begin{align*} H_1(K) \otimes \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \rightarrow H_1(K; \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

This evaluates as

\begin{align*} H_1(K, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) = \mathbb{Z} \otimes \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

showing that

\begin{align*} H_1(K; \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z})) \cong& (\mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \otimes \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \\ \cong& (\mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z})^2 \end{align*}

where distributivity of the tensor product

2.3. \(k = 2\)

Then \(H_{2}(K) = 0\) and thus the map

\begin{align*} H_{k}(K, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Tor}(H_{k-1}(K), \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \cong \mathrm{Tor}(\mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \end{align*}

is an isomorphism

Since tor is additive in both variables this reduces to

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Tor}(\mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \oplus \mathrm{Tor}(\mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \cong 0 \oplus \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}

where the latter follows from tor 1 functor for an integral domain and quotient of a pid

2.4. \(k \geq 3\)

Then both \(H_{k-1}(K), H_k(K)\) are zero and thus by singular homology with coefficients and two zeros
