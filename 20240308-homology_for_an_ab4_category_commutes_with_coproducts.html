homology for an ab4 category commutes with coproducts

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an Ab4 category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Then the homology functor \(H_n(-)\) commutes with coproducts

2. Proof

follows, as \(\coprod\) commutes with every finite limit, hence also image and kernewa
