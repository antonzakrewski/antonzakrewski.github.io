category of graded modules

1. Definition

Let \(M\) be a monoid and \(R = \bigoplus_{m \in m} R_m\) a graded ring. Then the category of graded modules is the category containing

  1. graded modules over \(R\) as objects
  2. graded module homomorphisms as morphisms
