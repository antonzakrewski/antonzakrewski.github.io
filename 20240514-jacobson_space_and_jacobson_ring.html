jacobson space and jacobson ring

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathrm{Spec}(R)\) the zariski spectrum with the Zariski topology. TFAE:

  1. \(\mathrm{Spec}(R)\) is a Jacobson space
  2. \(R\) is a Jacobson ring

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

Suppose \(\mathfrak{p}\) is a prime ideal. Then \(V(\mathfrak{p})\) is by definition closed. Then by definition of the zariski topolog

\begin{align*} V(\mathfrak{p}) =& \overline{V(\mathfrak{p})} \\ =& \overline{V(\mathfrak{p}) \cap \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m})} \end{align*}

hence using Vereinigung von Abschlüssen als Teilmenge des Abschlusses der Vereinigung we conclude that

\begin{align*} V(\mathfrak{p}) \supseteq& \overline{V(\mathfrak{p}) \cap \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m})} \\ \supseteq& \bigcup V(\mathfrak{p} \cap V(\mathfrak{m}) \\ =& \bigcup V(\mathfrak{p} \cup \mathfrak{m}) \\ =& \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m}) \\ =& V(\bigcap \mathfrak{m}) \\ \end{align*}

This shows that (cf.

\begin{align*} \sqrt{\mathfrak{p}} = \bigcap \mathfrak{m} \end{align*}

and as \(\mathfrak{p}\) is radical we get

\begin{align*} \mathfrak{p} = \bigcap \mathfrak{m} \end{align*}

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Let \(V(\mathfrak{a})\) be closed. Then by vanishing set and radical we may assume that \(\mathfrak{a} = \sqrt{\mathfrak{a}}\) is a radical ideal

Further we get

\begin{align*} V(\mathfrak{a}) =& V(\sqrt{\mathfrak{a}}) \\ =& V(\bigcap \mathfrak{p}) \\ =& \bigcup V(\mathfrak{p}) \end{align*}

Therefore it suffices to show this for \(\mathfrak{p}\) prime, as

\begin{align*} \overline{V(\mathfrak{a}) \cap \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m})} \subseteq \bigcup \overline{V(\mathfrak{p}) \cap \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m})} \end{align*}

As \(R\) is a jacobson ring we get

\begin{align*} V(\mathfrak{p}) =& V(\bigcap \mathfrak{p}) \h =& \bigcup V(\mathfrak{p}) \\ \end{align*}

and

\begin{align*} \overline{\bigcup V(\mathfrak{m}) \cap \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m})} =& \overline{\bigcup V(\mathfrak{m})} \\ \supseteq& \bigcup \overline{V(\mathfrak{m})} \\ =& \bigcup V(\mathfrak{m}) \\ =& V(\mathfrak{p}) \end{align*}

Note that \(\overline{Z} = Z \supseteq \overline{Z \cap (\bigcup V(\mathfrak{m}))}\) hence it suffices to show that

\begin{align*} Z \subseteq \overline{Z \cap (\bigcup V(\mathfrak{m}))} \end{align*}
