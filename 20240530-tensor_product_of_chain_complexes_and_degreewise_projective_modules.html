tensor product of chain complexes and degreewise flat modules

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring, \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{RMod})\) be the category of chain complexes Suppose \(F_*\) is a chain complex of flat modules. Then the tensor product of chain complexes functor

\begin{align*} (\otimes_R F_*) \end{align*}

(resp. \(F_* \otimes_R -\)) is an exact functor

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • composition of exact functors as exact functor
  • category RMod as Ab4 category
