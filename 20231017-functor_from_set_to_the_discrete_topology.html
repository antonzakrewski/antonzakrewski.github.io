discrete space functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Given the category set, the functor \(\mathrm{disc}\) to the category Top is defined by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{disc}: \mathrm{Set} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top} \\ X \mapsto& (X, \mathcal{P}(X)) \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f: (X, \mathcal{P}(X)) \rightarrow (Y, \mathcal{P}(Y))) \end{align*}
