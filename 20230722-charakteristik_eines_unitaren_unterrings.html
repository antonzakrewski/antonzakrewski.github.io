charakteristik eines unitären Unterrings

1. Proposition

Let \(R,S\) be integral domains such that \(R \subseteq S\) is a subring. Then they have the same characteristic

\begin{align*} \mathrm{char}(R) = \mathrm{char}(S) \end{align*}

2. Proof

By assumption, \(1 \in R, S\), and hence \(\sum_{i=1}^{n} 1_R = 0 \Leftrightarrow \sum_{i=1}^{n} 1_S = 0\). Therefore \(\mathrm{char}(R) = \mathrm{char}(S)\)
