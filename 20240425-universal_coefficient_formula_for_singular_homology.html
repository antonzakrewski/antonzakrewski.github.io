universal coefficient formula for singular homology

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(R\) a hereditary ring and \(M\) an \(R\)-module. Then there exists a natural short exact sequence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 & H_n(X;R) \otimes_R M & H_n(X;M) & \mathrm{Tor}(H_{n-1}(X;R),M) & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
