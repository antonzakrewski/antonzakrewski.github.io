Embedding of a monoidal category to the category of endofunctors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a monoidal category, then there exists a monoidal embedding functor to the endofunctor category

\begin{align*} \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathrm{End}(\mathcal{C}) \end{align*}
