composition of open maps

1. Proposition

Let \(X,Y,Z\) be topological spaces and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) and \(g: Y \rightarrow Z\) be open maps. Then the composition \(g \circ f\) is also an open map

2. Proof

Suppose \(O \subseteq X\) is open, then \(f[O]\) is open, therefore also \(g[f[O]] = (g \circ f)[O]\)
