rational functions on an affine variety

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field and \(\mathbb{A}_{K}^n\) the affine space and \(Y \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) an affine variety.

Then the rational functions on \(Y\) is defined as quotient field on the coordinate ring

\begin{align*} K(Y) = Q(\mathcal{O}(Y)) \end{align*}

2. Welldefined

note, that the vanishing ideal is by assumption a prime ideal, hence the quotient ring is an Integral domain (cf. prime ideal and quotient ring as integral domain)
