coslice category for a terminal object contains a zero object

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(1 \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) a terminal object and \(1 / \mathcal{C}\) the coslice category Then \(1/ \mathcal{C}\) is a pointed category

2. Proof

follows from \(\mathrm{id}_{1}: 1 \rightarrow 1\) as zero object

2.1. terminal

follows from

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{rr}{1} & & 1 \\ & 1 \arrow[]{ru}{} \arrow[]{lu}{f}& \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2.2. initial

follows from

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 1 \arrow[dashed]{rr}{f}& & A \\ & \arrow[]{lu}{} 1 \arrow[]{ru}{f}& \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
