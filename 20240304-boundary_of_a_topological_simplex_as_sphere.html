boundary of a topological simplex as sphere

1. Proposition

Given the topological simplex

\begin{align*} \Delta_{\mathrm{Top}}^n \end{align*}

the boundary is homeomorphic to the n-1-sphere

2. Proof

  • homeomorphism induces homeomorphism for the boundaries (?)
  • n-simplex homeomorphic to the n-disk
