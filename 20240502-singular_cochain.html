cosingular chain complex

1. Definition

Let \((X,A)\) be a pair of spaces, \(M\) an abelian group. Then the cosingular chain complex is defined as hom chain complex

\begin{align*} C_{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A;M) \coloneqq \underline{\mathrm{Hom}}(C^{\mathrm{sing}}(X,A), M[0]) \end{align*}

where

  1. \(C^{\mathrm{sing}}(A,M)\) is the singular chain complex
  2. \(M[0]\) is the inclusion from an additive category to the category of chain complexes
