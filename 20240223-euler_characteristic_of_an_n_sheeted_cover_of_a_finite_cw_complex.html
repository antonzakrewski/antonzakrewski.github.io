euler characteristic of an n sheeted cover of a finite cw complex

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a finite cw-complex and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) an n-sheeted covering. Then it holds for the euler characteristic

\begin{align*} \chi(Y) = n \cdot \chi(X) \end{align*}

2. Proof

By covering map of a cw complex induces CW structure on the domain there exists a cw-structure with

\begin{align*} \vert J_n(Y) \vert = n \cdot \vert J_n(X) \vert \end{align*}

for the number of attached cells.

Thus by combinatorial euler characteristic as homological euler characteristic, it follows, that

\begin{align*} \chi(Y) =& \sum_{i=0}^{k} (-1)^k \vert J_n(Y) \vert \\ =& \sum_{i=0}^{k} (-1)^k n \cdot \vert J_n(X) \vert \\ =& n \cdot \chi(X) \end{align*}
