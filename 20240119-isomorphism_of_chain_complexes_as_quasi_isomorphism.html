isomorphism of chain complexes as quasi isomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes. Suppose \(f \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{\mathcal{A}})\) is an isomorphism, then \(f\) is a quasi-isomorphism.
