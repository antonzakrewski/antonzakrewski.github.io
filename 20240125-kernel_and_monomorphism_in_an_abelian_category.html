kernel and monomorphism in an abelian category

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a preadditive category and \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a morphism

TFAE:

  1. \(f\) is a monomorphism
  2. the kernel is the zero object: \(\mathrm{ker}(f) = 0\)

2. Proof

follows dually to cokernel and epimorphism in an abelian category
