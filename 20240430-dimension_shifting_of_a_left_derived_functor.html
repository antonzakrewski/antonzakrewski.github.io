dimension shifting of a left derived functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a right exact functor, \(\mathcal{LF}_n\) the left derived functor. Then for \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{A})\) and \(n \geq 2\) there exists an object \(B \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{A})\) such that

\begin{align*} \mathcal{LF}_{n}(A) \simeq \mathcal{LF}_{n-1}(B) \end{align*}
