right lifting property and pushouts

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a countably cocomplete \(f: X \rightarrow Y\) a morphism and \(X,Y,Z \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) objects. If \(f\) has the right lifting property with respect to $g: W → Z, h: W → V$$ then \(f\) has the right lifting property with respect to the pushout

\begin{align*} V \rightarrow V \coprod_W Z \end{align*}
