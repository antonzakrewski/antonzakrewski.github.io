map into image in an abelian category as epimorphism

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category \(f: A \rightarrow B\) a morphism and \(\mathrm{im}(f)\) the image Then the canonical morphism

\begin{align*} A \rightarrow \mathrm{coim}(f) \end{align*}

is an epimorphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • map into cokernel as epimorphism
  • image coimage factorization as isomorphism
