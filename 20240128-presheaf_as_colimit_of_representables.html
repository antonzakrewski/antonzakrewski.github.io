presheaf as colimit of representables

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set}\) a set-valued presheaf Then there exists a canonical isomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(-, X) \end{align*}

where \(\mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}\) is the slice category by yoneda

2. Proof

Let \(\mathcal{G} \in \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}}, \mathrm{Set}\) be a functor.

As the contravariant hom-functor dualizes small colimits to limits, it follows that

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C})}(\mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}}(-,X), \mathcal{G}(-)) \simeq& \lim_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}} (\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C})}(-,X)) \mathcal{G}(-))) \end{align*}

Using contravaiant Yoneda, this gives

\begin{align*} \lim_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}} (\mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C})}(-,X)) \mathcal{G}(-))) \simeq& \lim_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}} \mathcal{G}(X) \end{align*}

using the natural isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathrm{lim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}} \mathcal{G}(X) \simeq \mathrm{NatMorph}(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

this gives

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C})}(\mathrm{colim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}}(-,X), \mathcal{G}(-)) \simeq& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{P}(\mathcal{C})}(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

where by limit of yoneda slices and evaluated as set of natural transformations
