Tor Functor commutes with coproducts

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod. Then for a module \(M\) and a filtered diagram \(N_i\), the Tor functor preserves coproducts

\begin{align*} \bigoplus_{i} \mathrm{Tor}_n^R(N_i,M) \rightarrow \mathrm{Tor}_n^R (\bigoplus_{i} N_i, M) \end{align*}
