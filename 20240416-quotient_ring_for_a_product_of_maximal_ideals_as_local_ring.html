quotient ring for a power of maximal ideals as local ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{m} \subseteq A\) a maximal ideal. Then the quotient ring to the power \(\mathfrak{m}^n\)

\begin{align*} A/\mathfrak{m}^n \end{align*}

is a local ring

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • product of maximal ideals and unique maximal ideal
  • correspondence theorem for ideals and quotient rings
