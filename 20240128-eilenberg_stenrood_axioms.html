Eilenberg Stenrood axioms

1. Definition

A sequence of functors

\begin{align*} H_n: \mathrm{TopPairs} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ab} \end{align*}

from category of topological pairs to Category Ab together with natural transformations

\begin{align*} \partial_i: H_i(X,A) \rightarrow H_{i-1}(A, \emptyset) \end{align*}

satisfying

  • Homotopy Axiom of Homology
  • Excision Axiom of Homology
  • Dimension Axiom of Homology
  • Additivity Axiom of Homology
  • Exactness Axiom of Homology
