constant sheaf on an abelian group

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(A\) an abelian group. Then the constant sheaf on \(\mathcal{A}\) on \(X\) is defined, by giving \(A\) the discrete topology and

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}: \mathrm{Op}(X)^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Ab} \\ O \mapsto& \prod_{o \in O} A \\ (f: O \rightarrow O') \mapsto& (\pi: \prod_{o \in O} A \rightarrow \prod_{o \in O'} A) \end{align*}
