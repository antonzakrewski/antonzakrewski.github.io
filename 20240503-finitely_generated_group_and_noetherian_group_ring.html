noetherian group ring and noetherian ring for a finitely generated group

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(G\) a finitely generated group and \(A[G]\) the group ring TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is noetherian
  2. \(A[G]\) is noetherian

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

  • ring epimorphism from the polynomial ring to the group ring
  • image of a noetherian ring as noetherian ring
  • Hilbert's basis theorem

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

corollary of:

  • ring epimorphism from the group ring to the ring
  • image of a noetherian ring as noetherian ring
