tor 1 of finite cyclic groups

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{Z}/n \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/ m \mathbb{Z}\) be cyclic groups Then Tor-1 evaluates as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Tor}_1^{\mathbb{Z}}(\mathbb{Z}/n \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/ m \mathbb{Z}) = \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z}/ \mathrm{gcd}(n,m) \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n,m \neq 0 \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • tor 1 functor for an integral domain and quotient of a pid
  • pi-1 tensor submodule of a quotient ring of the integers and gcd
