kunneth formula

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a hereditary ring, \(C,D\) chain complexes of \(R\)-module, where \(C\) is a chain complex of projectives . Then for \(n \geq 0\) ,there exists a natural split exact sequence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & H(C) \otimes_R H(D) \arrow[]{r}{} & H(C \otimes D) \arrow[]{r}{} & \mathrm{Tor}_1^R(H(C), H(D))[1] \arrow[]{r}{} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

of graded modules (cf. forgetful functor from chain complexes of modules to graded modules)
