weak adjoint inverse

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a functor. Then a weak adjoint inverse is a functor \(\mathcal{G}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{C}\) such that \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}\) are an adjoint equivalence
