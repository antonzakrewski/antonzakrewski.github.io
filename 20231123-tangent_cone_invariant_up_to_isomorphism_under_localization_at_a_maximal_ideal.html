tangent cone invariant up to isomorphism under localization at a maximal ideal

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be an Integral domain, \(\mathfrak{m} \subseteq A\) a maximal ideal. Then for the localization at \(\mathfrak{m}\), \(A_{\mathfrak{m}}\) with maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{n} = \langle \mathfrak{m}\rangle \subseteq A_{\mathfrak{m}}\), the tangent cone is canonically isomorphic

\begin{align*} G_{\mathfrak{m}}(A) \cong G_{\mathfrak{n}}(A_{\mathfrak{m}}) \end{align*}
