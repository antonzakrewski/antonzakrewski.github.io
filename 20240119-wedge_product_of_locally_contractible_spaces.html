wedge product of pointed contractible spaces as contractible space

1. Proposition

Let \(X_i\) be based spaces which are pointed contractible. Then the wedge product

\begin{align*} \bigvee X_i \end{align*}

is also pointed contractible

2. Proof

Choose pointed homotopies

\begin{align*} H_X_i: X_i \times [0,1] \rightarrow& X \\ \end{align*}

Then it follows by product of locally compact spaces preserves colimits

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \coprod X_i \times [0,1] \arrow[twoheadrightarrow]{r}{\pi} \arrow[]{d}{\simeq} &( \bigvee X_i) \times [0,1] \arrow[]{d}{\simeq} & \\ \coprod (X_i \times [0,1]) \arrow[bend right]{rrd}{\coprod H_{x_i}} \arrow[twoheadrightarrow]{r}{\pi} & \mathrm{colim}_{* \times [0,1]} X_i \times [0,1] \arrow[]{r}{\exists!} & \bigvee X_i \\ & & \coprod X_i \arrow[twoheadrightarrow]{u}{\pi} \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Hence

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} &( \bigvee X_i) \times [0,1] \arrow[]{d}{\simeq} & \\ & \mathrm{colim}_{* \times [0,1]} X_i \times [0,1] \arrow[]{r}{\exists!} & \bigvee X_i \\ \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

defines a homotopy
