based compact open bifunctor

1. Definition / Proof

Given category Top*, the based compact open bifunctor is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_{*}(-,-): \mathrm{Top}_*^{\mathrm{op}} \times \mathrm{Top}_*^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top} \\ ((X,x),(Y,y)) \mapsto& \mathrm{Map}_*((X,x),(Y,y)) \\ (f: (X_2,x_2) \rightarrow (X_1,x_1), (g: (Y_1,y_1) \rightarrow (Y_2,y_2)) \mapsto& g^{ \leftarrow} \circ f^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{Map}_*((X_1,x_1), (Y_1,y_1)) \rightarrow \mathrm{Map}_*((X_2,x_2), (Y_2,y_2)) \\ \end{align*}
