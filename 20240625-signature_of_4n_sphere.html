signature of 4n sphere

1. Proposition

The signature of the 4n-sphere is the zero morphism

2. Proof

follows as

\begin{align*} H_{2n}(S^4n) = 0 \end{align*}

showing that

\begin{align*} 0 \simeq H_{2n}(S^4) \otimes H_{2n}(S^4) \otimes \mathbb{R} \rightarrow& \mathbb{Z} \end{align*}
