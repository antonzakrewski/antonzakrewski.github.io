kernel functor coeffacable

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be a pre-abelian category, \(\mathrm{Fun}(\Delta^1, \mathcal{A})\) the arrow category and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{ker}: \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta^1, \mathcal{A}) \rightarrow \mathcal{A} \\ (f: A \rightarrow B) \mapsto& \mathrm{ker}(f) \\ \end{align*}

the kernel functor

Then \(\mathrm{ker}\) is coeffacable.

2. Proof

follows from

  • kernel and monomorphism in pointed category
  • isomorphism as monomorphism
\begin{align*} \mathrm{ker}(\mathrm{id}) = 0 \end{align*}
