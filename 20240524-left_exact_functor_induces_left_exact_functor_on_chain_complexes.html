left exact functor induces left exact functor on chain complexes

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}), \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{B})\) be the categories of chain complexes. Suppose \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) is a functor preserving the zero morphism and \(\mathcal{F}_*\) the induced functor on chain complexes.

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}\) is left exact functor
  2. \(\mathcal{F}_*\) is left exact

2. Proof

follows from exact chain complex, where exactness is determined degreewise.
