join of represented simpilical sets

1. Proposition

Let \(\Delta^n, \Delta^m\) be the represented simplicial sets and \(\star\) the join. Then

\begin{align*} \Delta^n \star \Delta^m \simeq \Delta^{n+m+1} \end{align*}

2. Proof

using nerve commutes with join and represented simplicial set as nerve

\begin{align*} N([n]) \star N([m]) \simeq& N([n] \star [m]) \end{align*}

where the join of categories of \([n], [m]\) is given by \([n + m + 1]\) as it contains \((n + 1) + (m + 1)\) objects and morphisms

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{[n] \star [m]}(a,b) = \begin{cases} 1 & \mbox{if } a,b \in [n], a \leq b \\ 1 & \mbox{if } a,b \in [m], a \leq b \\ 1 & \mbox{if } a \in [n], b \in [m] \\ \emptyset & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}
