finite nonabelian simple group and index of subgroup

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a finite non abelian simple group with a subgroup \(H\) of index \(n > 1\). Then

\begin{align*} n \geq 5 \end{align*}

2. Proof

Consider the group action

\begin{align*} \varphi: G \rightarrow S_n \end{align*}

given by the group action on the cosets

Then by simple group and group homomorphism it follows, that \(\mathrm{ker}(\varphi) = \{1\}\), since \(n > 1\) and hence there exists a coset \(g H \neq H\) where \(g \cdot H = gH\)
