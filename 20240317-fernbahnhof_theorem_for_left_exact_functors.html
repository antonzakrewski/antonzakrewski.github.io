Fernbahnhof theorem for left exact functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories, \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) the category of chain complexes over \(\mathcal{A}\), \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) a left exact functor and \(\mathcal{F}_*\) the induced functor on chain complexes. Then for the homology functor, there exists a natural transformation

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}H^n \leftarrow H^n \mathcal{F}_* \end{align*}

2. Proof

Corollary of, induced morphism between colimits and functor, as the image is a combination of cokernel and kernel, hence we get.

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}H_2(A) =& \mathcal{F}(\mathrm{ker}(\partial_1)/\mathrm{im}(\partial_2)) \\ =& \mathcal{F}(\mathrm{ker}(\partial_1)/\mathrm{ker}(\mathrm{coker}(\partial_2)) \\ \leftarrow& \mathcal{F}(\mathrm{ker}(\partial_1))/ \mathcal{F}(\mathrm{ker}(\mathrm{coker}(\partial_2) \\ \cong& \mathrm{ker}(\mathcal{F}(\partial))/\mathrm{ker}(\mathcal{F}(\mathrm{coker}(\partial_2))) \\ \leftarrow& \mathrm{ker}(\mathcal{F}(\partial))/\mathrm{ker}((\mathrm{coker}(\mathcal{F}(\partial_2)))) \end{align*}
