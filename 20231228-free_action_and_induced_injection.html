free action and induced injection

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(X\) be a set and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a group action. TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is free
  2. for \(x \in X\), the map
\begin{align*} \varphi(-,x): G \rightarrow& X \\ g \mapsto& \varphi(g,x) \end{align*}

is injective
