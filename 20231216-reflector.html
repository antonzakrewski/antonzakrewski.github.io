reflector

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories, where \(\iota: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) is a reflective subcategory.

Then the right adjoint

\begin{align*} T: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \end{align*}

is said to be the reflector
