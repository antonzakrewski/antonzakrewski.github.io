correspondence of abelian groups and Z-Modules

1. Proposition

There is a one to one correspondence of abelian groups and \(\mathbb{Z}\)-modules given by the forgetful functor from RMod to Ab

Note that the module structure uniquely is determined by the abelian group, as distributivity results in
