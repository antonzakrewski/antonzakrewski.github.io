covariant hom functor for a finite product of modules preserves colimits

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring and \(\mathrm{RMod}\) the category RMod, \(R[X]\) a finite product. Then the covariant ab-hom functor / covariant hom functor preserves colimits.

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R[X], \mathrm{colim}(-)) \simeq& \prod \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R,\mathrm{colim}(-)) \\ \simeq& \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. hom functor

Since the covariant hom-functor preserves small limits we get

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R[X], \mathrm{colim}(-)) =& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(\coprod R, \mathrm{colim}(-)) \\ \simeq& \prod \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R, \mathrm{colim}(-)) \end{align*}

using the natural interpretation as set functor (cf. forgetful set functor from RMod as representable functor) we conclude

\begin{align*} \prod \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R, \mathrm{colim}(-)) \simeq \prod \mathrm{Set}(\mathrm{colim}(-)) \\ \end{align*}

as the forgetful functor from RMod to Set preserves filtered colimits we further get

\begin{align*} \prod \mathrm{Set}(\mathrm{colim}(-)) \simeq& \prod \mathrm{colim}(\mathrm{Set}(-)) \end{align*}

and further by filtered colimits commute with finite limits in Set

\begin{align*} \prod \mathrm{colim}(\mathrm{Set}(-)) \simeq& \mathrm{colim} \prod (\mathrm{Set}(-)) \\ \simeq& \mathrm{colim} \prod \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R, -) \\ \simeq& \mathrm{colim} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(\coprod R, -) \\ \simeq& \mathrm{colim} \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{RMod}}(R[X], -) \\ \end{align*}
