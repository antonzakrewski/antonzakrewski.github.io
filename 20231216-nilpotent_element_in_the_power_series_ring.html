Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(A[ [X]]\) the power series ring and \(f = \sum_{i=0}^{\infty} \alpha_i X^i\) a nilpotent element. Then \(\alpha_n\) is nilpotent for arbitrary \(i \in \mathbb{N}\)
