graded group homomorphism

1. Definition

Let \(G \cong \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{Z} } C_n ,G' \cong \bigoplus_{n \in \mathbb{Z}} C_n'\) be graded abelian groups and \(\varphi: G \rightarrow G'\) a homomorphism. Then \(\varphi\) is a graded group homomorphism, if for $n ∈ \mathbb{Z} $ it holds, that

\begin{align*} \varphi[C_n] \subseteq C_n' \end{align*}
