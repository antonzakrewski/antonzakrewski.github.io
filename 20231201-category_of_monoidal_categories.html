category of monoidal categories

1. Definition / Proposition

the category of monoidal categories, \(\mathrm{MonCat}\) is defined as subcategory of Category Cat containing

  1. (small) Monoidal categories as objects
  2. monoidal functors as morphism

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • identity functor as strict monoidal functor
  • composition of monoidal functors as monoidal functor
