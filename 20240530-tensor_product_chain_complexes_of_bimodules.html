tensor product of chain complexes of bimodules functor

1. Proposition

Let \(R,S,T\) be rings and \(M_1,M_2\) be chain complexes of bimodules where

  1. \(M_*\) is a chain complex of \(S,R\)-bimodules
  2. \(N_*\) is a chain complex of \(R,T\)-bimodules

Then

\begin{align*} M_* \otimes_R N_* \end{align*}

is a chain complex of \(S,T\) bimodules

Furthermore the tensor product of chain complexes functor gives rise to a bifunctor

\begin{align*} - \otimes_R -: \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{R-S-Mod}) \times \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{T-R-Mod}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{S-T-Mod}) \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from the

  1. tensor product of bimodules
  2. colimit of bimodules

where each component

\begin{align*} M_i \otimes N_j \end{align*}

carries a canonical structure of a \(S,T\) bimodule and so does the coproduct

\begin{align*} \oplus M_i \otimes N_j \end{align*}
