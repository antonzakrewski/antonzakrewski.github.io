presentation of a group

1. Definition

Let \(S\) be a set, \(F_S\) the free group on \(S\) and \(R \subseteq F_s\) be a set of words. Let \(N_S\) be the normal closure

Then a presentation is the quotient group

\begin{align*} \langle S \vert R\rangle \coloneqq F_S/N \end{align*}
