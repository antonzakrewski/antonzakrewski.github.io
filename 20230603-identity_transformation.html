identity transformation

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(F: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a functor. Then the identity transformation is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{id}_{F}: F \rightarrow& F \\ F(a) \mapsto& F(a) \\ F(g) \mapsto& F(g) \end{align*}
