based mapping space and coproduct as homeomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \((X_i,x_i)\) be a family of based spaces and \((Y,y_0)\) another based space. Then for the Based Mapping space and the categorical coproduct, the wedge product (cf. wedge product as coproduct), the natural map

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_*( \bigwedge_{i \in I} X_i, Y) \rightarrow \prod_{i \in I} \mathrm{Map}_*(X_i, Y) \end{align*}

is a homeomorphism
