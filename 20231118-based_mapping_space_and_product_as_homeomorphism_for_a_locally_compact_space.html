based mapping space and product as homeomorphism for a locally compact space

1. Proposition

Let \((X_i,x_i)\) be a family of based spaces and \((Y,y_0)\) another locally regular compact based space. Then for the Based Mapping space and the categorical product, the canonical map

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_*( Y, \prod_{i \in I} X_i) \rightarrow \prod_{i \in I} \mathrm{Map}_*(Y, X_i) \end{align*}

is a homeomorphism

corollary of:
