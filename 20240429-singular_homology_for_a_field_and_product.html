singular homology for a field and product

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathrm{Top}\) be the category Top, \(K\) a field. Then the map out of the tensor of homology to the homology of the tensor

\begin{align*} H_n(- \times -; K) \simeq& H_n(-;K) \otimes_R H_n(n;K) \end{align*}

is a natural isomorphism

2. Proof

corollary of

  • field as absolutely flat ring
  • singular homologoy of a product for an absoultely flat module
