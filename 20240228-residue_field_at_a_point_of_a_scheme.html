residue field at a point of a scheme

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a scheme. Then for \(x \in X\) the residue field is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathcal{O}_x/ \mathfrak{m}_x \end{align*}

where \(\mathcal{O}_x\) is the stalk and \(\mathfrak{m}_x\) is the maximal ideal of the local ring \(\mathcal{O}_x\)
