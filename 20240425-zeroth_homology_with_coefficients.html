zeroth homology with coefficients

1. Proposition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space, \(H_n(-, M)\) the singular homology then

\begin{align*} H_0(-, M) \cong H_0(-) \otimes M \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of

  • universal coefficient formula for singular homology
  • tor additive in both variables

as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Tor}_1^{\mathbb{Z}}(H_{k-1}(-), M) \cong 0 \end{align*}
