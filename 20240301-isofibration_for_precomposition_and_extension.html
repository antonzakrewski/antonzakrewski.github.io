isofibration for precomposition and extension

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{G}_0,\mathcal{G}_1,\mathcal{G}\) be groupoids and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{G}_0 \rightarrow \mathcal{G}_1\) a functor. Let

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}^{ \rightarrow} : \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{G}_1, \mathcal{G}) \rightarrow \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{G}_0, \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

the precomposition as functor

TFAE:

  1. \(\mathcal{F}^{ \rightarrow}\) is a isofibration
  2. there exists an extension
\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \arrow[]{d}{} \mathcal{G}_0 \times \Delta^1 \coprod_{\mathcal{G}_0 \times \{0\}} \mathcal{G}_1 \times \{0\} \arrow[]{r}{} & \mathcal{G}_0 \\ \mathcal{G}_1 \times \Delta^1 \arrow[]{rd}{} & \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
