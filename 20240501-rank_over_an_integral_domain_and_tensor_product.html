rank over an integral domain and tensor product

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain, \(M\) an \(R\)-module. Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{rank}(M) = \mathrm{dim}(M \otimes_R Q(R)) \end{align*}

where \(Q(R)\) is the quotient field and \(\otimes_R\) the tensor product
