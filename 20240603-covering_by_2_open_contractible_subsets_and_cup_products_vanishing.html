covering by 2 open contractible subsets and cup products vanishing

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(U,V\) an open cover by contractible spaces. Then each cup product of \(\alpha \in H^p(X;R), \beta \in X^q(X;R)\) vanishes for \(p,q > 0\)

2. Proof

Using the

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} H^p(X;R) \otimes_R H^q(X;R) & H^p((X,U);R) \otimes_R H^q((X,V);R) & H^{p + q}((X \times X, X \times U, V \times X)) \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

using the relative cup product empty excisive triad
