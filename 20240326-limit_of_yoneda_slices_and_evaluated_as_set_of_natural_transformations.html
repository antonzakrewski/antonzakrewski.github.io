limit of yoneda slices and evaluated set of natural transformations

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathrm{Set}\) a set-valued presheaf. Then there exists a natural isomorphism

\begin{align*} \mathrm{lim}_{X \in \mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}} \mathcal{G}(X) \simeq \mathrm{NatMorph}(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}) \end{align*}

where \(\mathcal{C}/\mathcal{F}\) is the slice category by yoneda
