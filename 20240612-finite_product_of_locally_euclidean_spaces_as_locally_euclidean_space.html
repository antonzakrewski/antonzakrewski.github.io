finite product of locally euclidean spaces as locally euclidean space

1. Proposition

Let \(M_i\) be locally euclidean topological spaces for \(i \in \{1,...,n\}\). Then the product

\begin{align*} \prod M_i \end{align*}

is also locally euclidean

2. Proof

Let \((m_i) \in \prod M_i\). Then by assumption there exist open neighbourhoods \(U_i \cong \mathbb{R}^{l_i}\) of \(m_i\). Hence \(\prod U_i\) is an open neighbourhood and using the product functor we get an isomorphism

\begin{align*} \prod U_i \cong& \prod \mathbb{R}^{l_i} \\ \cong& \mathbb{R}^{\sum l_i} \end{align*}
