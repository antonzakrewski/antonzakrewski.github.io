free and transitive action and induced bijection

1. Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group, \(X\) a set and \(\varphi: G \times X \rightarrow X\) a group action.

TFAE:

  1. \(\varphi\) is free and transitive
  2. for \(g \in G\), the induced map \(\varphi(-,x): G \rightarrow X\) is a Bijection

2. Proof

corollary of

  • free transitive group action and existence of a unique action path
  • free action and induced injection
  • transitive action and surjection
