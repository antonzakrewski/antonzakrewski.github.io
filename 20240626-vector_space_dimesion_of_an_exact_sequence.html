vector space dimesion of an exact sequence

1. Proposition

Let

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} V \arrow[]{r}{f} & W \arrow[]{r}{g} & U \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

be an exact sequence of vector spaces. Then the dimension of \(W\) is given by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{im}(f) \oplus \mathrm{im}(g) \end{align*}

2. Proof

follows from exact sequence and image and Splitting Lemma
