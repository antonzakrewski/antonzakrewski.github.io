starshaped subset as contractible space

1. Proposition

Let \(S \subseteq \mathbb{R}^n\) be a starshaped set, then \(S\) is contractible

2. Proof

By assumption, there exists a point \(s \in S\), such that \(s + t \cdot (x - s) \in S\) for \(x \in S\). Thus we get a homotopy
