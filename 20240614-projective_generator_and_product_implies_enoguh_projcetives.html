projective generator and coproduct implies enough projcetives

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category, \(P\) a projective generator, \(A \in \mathrm{Ob}(\mathcal{C})\) an object such that

\begin{align*} \coprod_{f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(P,A)} P \end{align*}

exists.

Then there exists a canonical epimorphism

\begin{align*} \coprod_{f \in \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathcal{C}}(P,A)} P \rightarrow A \end{align*}

2. Proof

corollary of:

  • generator and coproduct implies epimorphism
  • coproduct of projective objects as projective object
