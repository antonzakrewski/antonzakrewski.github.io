identification of a functor category of chain complexes as chain complexes of functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a small category, \(\mathcal{A}\) an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) the category of chain complexes. Then there exists a canonical isomorphism of categories

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})) \cong \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{A})) \end{align*}

given by

\begin{align*} \varphi: \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{A})) \\ \mathcal{F}(-) \mapsto& (\mathcal{F}(-)_n) \\ (\eta: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow \mathcal{G}) \mapsto& (\eta(-)_n: \mathcal{F}(-)_n \rightarrow \mathcal{G}(-)_n) \\ \end{align*}
