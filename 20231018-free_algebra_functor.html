free R-algebra functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(R\) be a ring.

The free algebra functor \(\mathrm{freeAlg}_R\) is defined

\begin{align*} \mathrm{freeAlg}_R: \mathrm{Set} \rightarrow& \mathrm{R-Alg} \\ X \mapsto& \mathrm{FreeAlg}_R(X) \\ (f: X \rightarrow Y) \mapsto& (f': \mathrm{FreeAlg}_R(X) \rightarrow \mathrm{FreeAlg}_R(Y)) \end{align*}

where \(f'\) is the algebra-homomorphism induced by

\begin{align*} f'(x) = f(x) \end{align*}

and \(\mathrm{FreeAlg}_R\) is the free algebra
