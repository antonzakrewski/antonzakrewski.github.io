associated and prime element

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(p_1,p_2 \in A\) elements such that

\begin{align*} p_1 \sim p_2 \end{align*}

are associated.

TFAE:

  1. \(p_1\) is prime
  2. \(p_2\) is prime

2. Proof

corollary of

  • associated as equivalence relation, providing symmetry of the argument
  • prime multiplied with unit as prime
