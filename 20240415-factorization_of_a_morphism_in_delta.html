factorization of a morphism in a simplex category

1. Proposition

Let \(\Delta\) be the simplex category and \(f: [k] \rightarrow [k']\) a morphism. Then \(f\) is the factorization of

\begin{align*} f = g \circ h \end{align*}

where \(g\) is an injection and \(h\) a surjection

see:

  • factorization of a surjection as degeneracy maps
  • factorization of an injective morphism as face maps

2. Proof

idea: collapse to the "image" (linear order, cardinality of the image)d then embedd into \([k']\)
