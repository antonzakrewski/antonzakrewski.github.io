composition of monoidal natural transformations as monoidal natural transformation

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be monoidal categories, \(\mathcal{F}, \mathcal{G}, \mathcal{H}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{H}\) be monoidal functors and

\begin{align*} \alpha: \mathcal{F} \rightarrow& \mathcal{G} \\ \beta: \mathcal{G} \rightarrow& \mathcal{H} \end{align*}

be monoidal natural transformations

Then the composition is also a monoidal natural transformation

2. Proof
