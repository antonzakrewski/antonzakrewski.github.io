co-extension of scalars

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be rings, \(f: A \rightarrow B\) a ring-homomorphism Then the co-extension of scalars functor is defined as functor

\begin{align*} \mathrm{coext}: \mathrm{A-Mod} \rightarrow& \mathrm{B-Mod} \\ M \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{A}(B,M) \\ (f: M \rightarrow N) \mapsto& \mathrm{Hom}_{A}(B,M) \rightarrow \mathrm{Hom}_{A}(B,N) \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. objects welldefined

  • hom-set out of a bimodule with module structure

2.2. morphisms welldefined

-postcomposition with a module homomorphism as module homomorphism for a bimodule
