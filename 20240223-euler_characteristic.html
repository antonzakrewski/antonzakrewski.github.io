homological euler characteristic

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a homologically finite topological space. Then the euler characteristic is defined as

\begin{align*} \chi_{\mathrm{hom}}(X) = \sum_{n \geq 0} (-1)^n \mathrm{rank}(H_n(X)) \end{align*}
