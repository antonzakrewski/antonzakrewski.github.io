concrete category and element chase

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a concrete category and

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} A \arrow[]{rd}{} \arrow[]{r}{} & B \arrow[]{d}{}\\ & C \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

a diagram (which may not commute).

Then one can element chase in \(\mathrm{Set}\)

2. Proof

corollary of

  • faithful functor and preimage as diagram
