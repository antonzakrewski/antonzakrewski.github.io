coordinate ring of an quasi-affine variety

1. Definition

Let \(K\) be a field, \(A \subseteq \mathbb{A}_K^n\) a simple quasi-affine variety. Then the coordinate ring of \(A\) is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathcal{O}(A) \coloneqq K[X_1,...,X_n]/I(A) \end{align*}

where \(I(A)\) is the vanishing ideal
