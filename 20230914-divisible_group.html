divisible group

1. Definition

Let \((G, \cdot)\) be a group. Then \(G\) is said to be divisible, if for every positive integer \(n \in \mathbb{N}^+\) and group element \(x \in G\) there exists a \(y \in G\) such that

\begin{align*} x =& y^n \end{align*}
