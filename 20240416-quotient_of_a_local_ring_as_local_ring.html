quotient of a local ring as local ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a local ring and \(\mathfrak{a} \subsetneq A\) a strict ideal. Then \(A/\mathfrak{a}\) is also a local ring.

2. Proof

Corollary of correspondence theorem for ideals and quotient rings, as the correspondence is order preserving. Therefore a maximal ideal \(\mathfrak{m} \subseteq A/\mathfrak{a}\) is also a maximal ideal \(\pi^{-1}[\mathfrak{m}]\) with \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{m}\). Thus it is unique
