semi locally simply connected space and weakly connected space

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a weakly locally connected topological space.

TFAE:

  1. each connected component is semi-locally simply connected space
  2. \(X\) is semi locally simply connected

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corollary of:

  • semi locally simply connected space and open covering of semi locally simply connected spaces
  • open connected components and weakly locally connected space

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

Let \(x \in X\), then by assumption, there exists an open neighbourhood \(V \subseteq X\), such that each loop inside \(X\) is nullhomotopic Then \(V \cap [x]\) is open (cf. open connected components and weakly locally connected space).

Furthermore, let \(\gamma\) be a path inside \(V \cap [x]\). Then \(\gamma\) is nullhomotopic inside \(X\), i.e. there exists a homotopy

\begin{align*} H: [0,1] \times [0,1] \rightarrow X \end{align*}

since \([0,1] \times [0,1]\) is connected, it follows, that \(H\) restricts to \([x]\) (image of a connected set is connected) Thus \(\gamma\) is nullhomotopic inside \([x]\)
