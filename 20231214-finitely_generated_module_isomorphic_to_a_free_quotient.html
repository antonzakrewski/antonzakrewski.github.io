finitely generated module isomorphic to a free quotient

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(M\) a module.

TFAE:

  1. \(M\) is finitely generated module.
  2. there exists an module-isomorphism \(A^n/\mathfrak{q} \simeq M\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
