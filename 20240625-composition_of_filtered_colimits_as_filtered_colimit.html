composition of filtered colimits as filtered colimit

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}\) be a category and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{colim}_I \mathrm{colim}_J A_{i,j} \end{align*}

be a colimit

Then

\begin{align*} \mathrm{colim}_I \mathrm{colim}_J A_{i,j} \end{align*}

is a filtered colimit
