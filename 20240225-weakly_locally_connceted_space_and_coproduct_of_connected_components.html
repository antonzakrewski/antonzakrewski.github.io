weakly locally connceted space and sum of connected components

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space.

TFAE:

  1. \(X\) is weakly locally connected space
  2. \(X\) is the sum of the topological sum of the connected components \(X \cong \coprod [x]\)

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

corolarry of:

  • open connected components and weakly locally connected space
  • topological space as colimit of an open covering

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)

by assumption, each connected component is open and hence suffices as connected open neighbourhood
