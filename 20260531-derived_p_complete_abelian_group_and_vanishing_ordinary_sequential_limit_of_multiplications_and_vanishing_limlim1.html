derived p-complete abelian group and vanishing ordinary sequential limit of multiplications and vanishing limlim1

Proposition

Let \(A\) be an abelian group
TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is derived p-complete or equivalently \(p\) complete as Eilenberg-MacLane spectrum.
  2. the sequential limit of $… \xrightarrow{\cdot p} X \xrightarrow{\cdot p} X$ in Category Ab and its lim-lim1 vanishes.

Proof

Recall that \(A\) is p-complete if and only if \(\lim_{n \in \mathbb{N}} (... \xrightarrow{\cdot p} A \xrightarrow{\cdot p} A )\) vanishes in the category of spectra (cf. p-complete spectrum and vinishing sequential limit of multiplication).
This is equivalent to the statement, that \(\pi_k(\lim_{n \in \mathbb{N}} (... \xrightarrow{\cdot p} A \xrightarrow{\cdot p} A )) \cong 0\) for all \(k \in \mathbb{Z}\).
But the lim-lim1 sequence of homotopy objects in a stable category shows

\begin{align*} 0 \rightarrow \mathrm{lim} \mathrm{lim}^1_{n \geq 0} \pi_{k+1}(X_n) \rightarrow \pi_k(\mathrm{lim}_{n \geq 0} X_n) \rightarrow \mathrm{lim}_{n \geq 0} \pi_k(X_n) \rightarrow 0 \end{align*}

shows that \(\pi_k(\lim_{n \in \mathbb{N}} (... \xrightarrow{\cdot p} A \xrightarrow{\cdot p} A ))\) vanishes if and only if \(\mathrm{lim}_{n \geq 0} \pi_k(X_n), \mathrm{lim} \mathrm{lim}^1_{n \geq 0} \pi_{k+1}(X_n)\) vanishes.
