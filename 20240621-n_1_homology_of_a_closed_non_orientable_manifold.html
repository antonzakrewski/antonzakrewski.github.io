n-1 homology of a closed non orientable manifold

1. Proposition

Let \(M\) be a closed, connected \(n\)-dimensional, non-orientable manifold. Then the torsion submodule of the \(n-1\)-th homology is isomorphic to

\begin{align*} T(H_{n-1}(M)) = \mathbb{Z}/2\mathbb{Z} \end{align*}
