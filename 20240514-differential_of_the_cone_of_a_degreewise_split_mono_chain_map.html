differential of the cone of a degreewise split mono chain map

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}\) be an abelian category and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A})\) be the category of chain complexes and

\begin{align*} \iota: B_* \rightarrow C_* \end{align*}

be a degreewise split mono chain map

then the chain cone is given by

\begin{align*} \mathrm{cone}(\iota)_n =& (B_n \oplus D_n \oplus B_{n-1}) \end{align*}

with differential

\begin{align*} \begin{pNiceMatrix} d & \varphi & 1 \\ 0 & d & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & -d \\ \end{pNiceMatrix}: B_n \oplus D_n \oplus B_{n-1} \rightarrow& B_{n-1} \oplus D_{n-1} \oplus B_{n-2} \end{align*}

2. Proof

\begin{align*} \begin{pNiceMatrix} d & \varphi & \mathrm{id} \\ 0 & d & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & - d \\ \end{pNiceMatrix} \end{align*}
