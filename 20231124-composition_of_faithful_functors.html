composition of faithful functors

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{E}\) be categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}, \mathcal{G}: \mathcal{D} \rightarrow \mathcal{E}\) be faithful functors Then the composition

\begin{align*} \mathcal{G} \circ \mathcal{F} \end{align*}

is also faithfull
