zariski spectrum of an integral domain as connected space

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain, \(\mathrm{Spec}(R)\) the zariski spectrum with the Zariski topology Then \(\mathrm{Spec}(R)\) is a connected space

2. Proof

clearly

\begin{align*} \{0\} \end{align*}

is a connected space.

By closure of a connected set as connected set it follows that

\begin{align*} \overline{\{0\}} =& V(0) \\ =& \mathrm{Spec}(R) \end{align*}

is connected. (cf. closure of a point in the zariski topology)
