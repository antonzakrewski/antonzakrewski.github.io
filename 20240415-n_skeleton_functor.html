n skeleton functor

1. Definition

Let \(\Delta\) be the simplex category and \(\Delta_{\leq n} \hookrightarrow \Delta\) the n-reduced simplex category Then the n-skeleton funtcor is defined as

\begin{align*} \mathrm{sk}_n \coloneqq \mathrm{Lan}(i_n), i_n^{ \rightarrow}: \mathrm{sSet} \rightarrow \mathrm{sSet} \end{align*}

where \(\mathrm{Lan}\) is the left kan extension or

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta, \mathrm{Set}) \arrow[]{r}{\mathrm{Lan}(i_n)} & \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta_{\leq}, \mathrm{Set}) \arrow[]{r}{} & \mathrm{Fun}(\Delta, \mathrm{Set}) \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

where \(\mathrm{Lan}_{i_n}\) is the left Kan extension as left adjoint on functor categories
