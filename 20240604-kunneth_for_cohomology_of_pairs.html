kunneth for cohomology of pairs

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a principal ideal domain, \((X,A), (Y,B)\) be pairs of spaces excisive pair of spaces category of excisive pairs of spaces Suppose \(H^*(X;A;R)\) is degreewise finitely generated, then there exists a natural <pairs of > short exact sequence

\begin{displaymath} H^* \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & H^*(X,A;R) \otimes_R H^*(Y,B;M) \arrow[]{r}{}& H^*((X,A) \times (Y,B); M) \arrow[]{r}{}& \mathrm{Tor}_1^R((X,A;R), H^*(Y,B,M))[-1] \arrow[]{r}{}& 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

Moreover this splits, albeit not naturally
