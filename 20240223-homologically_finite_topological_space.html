homologically finite topological space

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space. Then \(H_n(X)\) is said to be homologically finite if

\begin{align*} H_k(X) \end{align*}

is a finitely generated group for any \(k\) and zero for almost all \(k \in \mathbb{N}_0\)
