isomorphism of ringed spaces and isomorphism of sheaves with a homeomorphism

1. Proposition

Let \((X,\mathcal{T}_X)\) and \((Y,\mathcal{T}_Y)\) be topological spaces and \((X,\mathcal{O})\) and \((Y, \mathcal{O}')\) be ringed spaces. Suppose \((f,f^#)\) is a morphism of ringed spaces

TFAE:

  1. \((f,f^#)\) is an isomorphism
  2. \(f\) is a homeomorphism and \(f^#\) is an isomorphism of sheaves

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 1)
