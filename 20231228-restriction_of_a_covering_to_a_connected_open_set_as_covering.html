restriction of a covering to a connected component as covering

1. Proposition

Let \(B\) be a locally connected topological space, \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map Let \(E_0\) be a connected component, then the restriction

\begin{align*} p_0: E_0 \rightarrow B \end{align*}

is also a covering map

2. Proof

Suppose \(b \in B\) such that w.l.o.g. \(p^{-1}[b] \cap U \neq \emptyset\).

Then take a trivializing open cover \(U_i\) with \(b \in U_i\) and choose a connected open subset \(V \subseteq U_i\) of \(U_i\). Then we get

\begin{align*} p^{-1}[V] \cong \prod_{j \in J} V_j \subseteq E \end{align*}

restricting to \(U\) gives

\begin{align*} p_{\vert U}^{-1}[V] = \prod_{j' \in J} V_{j'} \subseteq U \end{align*}

as \(V_j\) is either disjoint from \(U\) or a subset by Vereinigung von überlappenden zusammenhängenden Mengen
