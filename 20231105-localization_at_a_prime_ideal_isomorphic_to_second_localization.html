localization at a prime ideal isomorphic to second localization

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(S \subseteq A\) a multiplicatively closed subset and \(\mathfrak{p}\) a prime ideal such that

\begin{align*} S \cap \mathfrak{p} = \emptyset \end{align*}

Then for the localization, it holds that

\begin{align*} A_{\mathfrak{p}} \cong (S^{-1}A)_{S^{-1} \mathfrak{p}} \end{align*}
