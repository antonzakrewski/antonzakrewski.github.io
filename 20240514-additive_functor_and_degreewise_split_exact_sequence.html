additive functor and degreewise split exact sequence

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{A}, \mathcal{B}\) be abelian categories and \(\mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{A}), \mathrm{Ch_*}(\mathcal{B})\) be the categories of chain complexes Suppose \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{A} \rightarrow \mathcal{B}\) is an additive functor, then the induced functor on chain complexes preserves degreewise split exact sequences
