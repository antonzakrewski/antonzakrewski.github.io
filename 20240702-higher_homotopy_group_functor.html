higher homotopy group functor

1. Proposition

Let \(n \geq 2\), then the homotopy group functor is defined as

\begin{align*} \pi_n: \mathrm{Top}_* \rightarrow& \mathrm{Ab} \\ (X,x) \mapsto& \pi_n(X,x) \\ (f: (X,x) \rightarrow (Y,y)) \mapsto& (f': \pi_n(X,x) \rightarrow \pi_n(Y,y)) \end{align*}

where \(\pi_n(X,x)\) is the homotopy group and

\begin{align*} f': \pi_n(X,x) \rightarrow& \pi_n(Y,y) \\ [g] \mapsto& [f^{ \leftarrow}g] \end{align*}

is the path component functor after the covariant compact open functor.
