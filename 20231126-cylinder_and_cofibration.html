inclusion of coproduct to product with interval as cofibration

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space and

\begin{align*} \iota: X \times \{0,1\} \rightarrow X \times [0,1] \end{align*}

be the inclusion. Then \(\iota\) is a cofibration

2. Proof

using cofibration and retraction

\begin{align*} X \times [0,1] \times [0,1] \rightarrow& (X \times \{0,1\} \times [0,1]) \cup (X \times [0,1] \times \{0\}) \end{align*}

This follows from

  • product and cofibration
  • inclusion from a coproduct to the product with the interval

construct cofibration:

  • map as retraction
