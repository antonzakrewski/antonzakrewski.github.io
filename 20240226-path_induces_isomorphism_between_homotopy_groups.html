path induces isomorphism between homotopy groups

1. Proposition

Let \(X\) be a topological space, \(x,x' \in X\) points and \(\gamma: [0,1] \rightarrow X\) a path from \(x\) to \(x'\). Then \(\gamma\) induces an isomorphism between the homotopy group

\begin{align*} \gamma: H_n(X,x) \rightarrow H_n(X,x') \end{align*}
