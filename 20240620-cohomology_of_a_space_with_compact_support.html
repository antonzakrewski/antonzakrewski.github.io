cohomology of a space with compact support

1. Definition

Let \(X\) be a topological space and \(M\) an abelian group then the cohomology of \(X\) with compact support is defined as colimit

\begin{align*} H^n_c(X;M) \coloneqq \mathrm{colim}_{K \in \mathcal{K}_x}(X, X \setminus K;M) \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let \(K \subseteq K'\). Then there exists a map of pairs of spaces

\begin{align*} \iota: (X,K) \hookrightarrow (X,K') \end{align*}

Applying the cohomology as contravariant functor gives a map

\begin{align*} H^*(X,K') \rightarrow H^*(X,K) \end{align*}
