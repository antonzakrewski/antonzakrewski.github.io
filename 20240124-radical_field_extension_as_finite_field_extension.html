radical field extension as finite field extension

1. Proposition

Let \(L/K\) be a radical extension, then \(L/K\) is a finite field extension.

2. Proof

corollary of Gradlemma applied to the tower of fields, where each

\begin{align*} [K_{i+1} : K_i] < \infty \end{align*}

by finitely generated algebraic field extension as finite field extension
