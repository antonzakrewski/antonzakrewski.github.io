smash product as left adjoint

1. Proposition

Let \((X, x)\) be a locally regular compact based space Then the unary smash product functor \((X,x) \wedge -\) is a left adjoint to the covariant based compact open functor \(\mathrm{Map}_*((X,x), -)\)

2. Proof

Consider

\begin{align*} \mathrm{comp}: \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Top}_*}((X \wedge - ),-) \rightarrow& \mathrm{Hom}_{\mathrm{Top}_*}(-, \mathrm{Map}_*(X, -)) \\ \end{align*}

Then it is a bijection of maps (cf. smash-currying of based mapping spaces bijective for a locally compact space) in category set and hence an isomorphism on objects. Therefore, it is a natural isomorphism (cf. natural isomorphism and isomorphism on the objects)
