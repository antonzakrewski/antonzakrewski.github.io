fully faithful functor between infinity categories as conservative functor

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be infinity categories and \(f: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) an infinity functor Suppose \(f\) is fully faithful, then \(f\) is conservative functor

2. Proof

the induced functor

\begin{align*} hf: h \mathcal{C} \rightarrow h \mathcal{D} \end{align*}

is by assumption fully faithful. Therefore \(hf\) is conservative (cf. fully faithful functor as conservative functor)
