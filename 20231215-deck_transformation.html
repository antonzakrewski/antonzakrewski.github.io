deck transformation

1. Definition

Let \(B,E\) be a topological space and \(p: E \rightarrow B\) a covering map.

A deck transformation is an automorphism in the category of covering maps, i.e. a homeomorphism

\begin{align*} \varphi: E \rightarrow E \end{align*}

compatible with \(p: E \rightarrow B\)
