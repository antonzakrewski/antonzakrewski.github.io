preimage of a maximal ideal for a finite type algebra over a jacobson ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a Jacobson ring, \(B\) a finite type A-Algebra for \(\iota: A \rightarrow B\). Then the contraction of a maximal ideal

\begin{align*} \iota^{-1}[\mathfrak{m}] \end{align*}

is also maximal
