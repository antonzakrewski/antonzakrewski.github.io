hereditary ring and ring as hereditary module

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring. TFAE:

  1. \(A\) is a hereditary ring
  2. \(A\) is a hereditary module as \(A\)-module
  3. each ideal of \(A\) is a projective \(A\)-module

2. Proof

2.1. 1) \(\implies\) 2)

special case

2.2. 2) \(\implies\) 3)

follows from submodule as ideal

2.3. 3) \(\implies\) 1)

Let \(P\) be a projective module. Then by projective module and sum as free module there exists a free module \(A^{\kappa}\) and an inclusion

\begin{align*} P \hookrightarrow A^{\kappa} \end{align*}
