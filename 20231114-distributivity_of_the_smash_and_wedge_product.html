distributivity of the smash and wedge product

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0), (Y,y_0), (Z,z_0)\) be based spaces. then

\begin{align*} (X \vee Y) \wedge Z \simeq& (X \wedge Z) \vee (Y \wedge Z) \end{align*}

2. Proof

\begin{align*} (x \wedge z) \mapsto& (\iota_l(x \wedge z)) \\ (y \wedge z) \mapsto& (\iota_l(x \wedge z)) \end{align*}
