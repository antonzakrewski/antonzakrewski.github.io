distributivity of the smash and wedge product

1. Proposition

Let \((X,x_0), (Y,y_0), (Z,z_0)\) be based spaces. then for the smash product and the wedge product, the following are canonically iso

\begin{align*} (X \vee Y) \wedge Z \cong& (X \wedge Z) \vee (Y \wedge Z) \end{align*}
