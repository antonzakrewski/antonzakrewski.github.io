commutator subgroup

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(G\) be a group. Then the commutator supgroup is defined as

\begin{align*} [G,G] \coloneqq& \langle\{[h,g] \vert g,h \in G\} \rangle \\ =& \langle\{g \cdot h \cdot g^{-1} \cdot h^{-1} \vert g,h \in G\} \rangle \end{align*}
