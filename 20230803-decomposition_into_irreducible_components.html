decomposition into irreducible components

1. Definition

Let \((X, \mathcal{T})\) be a topological space and \(Y \subseteq X\) a closed subset. A decomposition into irreducible components is a cover

\begin{align*} Y = \bigcup_{i=1}^n Y_i \end{align*}

where \(Y_i\) are irreducible components (or irreducible subspaces)
