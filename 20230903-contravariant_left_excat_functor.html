contravariant left exact functor

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be finitely complete categories, \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a contravariant functor.

Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is said to be left exact, if the dualized functor is a covariant left exact functor
