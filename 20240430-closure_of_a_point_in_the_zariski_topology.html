closure of a point in the zariski topology

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring, \(X = \mathrm{Spec}(A)\) the zariski spectrum with the Zariski topology and \(\mathfrak{p} \in \mathrm{Spec}\) a prime ideal. Then the closure of the point is given by the Vanishing set

\begin{align*} \overline{\{\mathfrak{p}\}} \subseteq V(\mathfrak{p}) \end{align*}

2. Proof

2.1. \(\subseteq\)

follows from closure as minimal closed set

2.2. \(\supseteq\)

Let \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\), then \(V(\mathfrak{p}) \subseteq V(\mathfrak{a})\). In particular

\begin{align*} V(\mathfrak{p}) \subseteq \bigcup \{V(\mathfrak{a}) \vert \mathfrak{a} \subseteq \mathfrak{p}\} = \overline{ \{\mathfrak{p}\}} \end{align*}
