infinity functor category

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be infinity categories. Then the functor category is defined as exponential object

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Fun}(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}) = \mathcal{D}^{\mathcal{C}} \end{align*}
