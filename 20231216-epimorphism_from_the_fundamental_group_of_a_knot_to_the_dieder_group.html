epimorphism from the fundamental group of a knot to the dihedral group

1. Proposition

Let \(p \in \mathbb{P}\) be a prime number, \(K\) a \(p\)-colorable Knot. Then for the fundamental group of the complement of \(K\), there exists a algebraic group-epimorphism

\begin{align*} q: \pi_1( \mathbb{R}^3 \setminus K) \rightarrow D_p \end{align*}
