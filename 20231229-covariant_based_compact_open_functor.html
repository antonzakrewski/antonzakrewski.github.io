covariant based compact open functor

1. Definition / Proposition

Let \((X,x)\) be a based space, then the covariant based compact open functor is defined

\begin{align*} \mathrm{Map}_*((X,x),-): \mathrm{Top}_* \rightarrow& \mathrm{Top} \\ (Y,y) \mapsto& \mathrm{Map}_*((X,x), (Y,y)) \\ (f: (Y_1,y_1) \rightarrow (Y_2,y_2)) \mapsto& f^{ \leftarrow}: \mathrm{Map}_*((X,x), (Y_1,y_1)) \rightarrow \mathrm{Map}_*((X,x), (Y_2,y_2)) \end{align*}
