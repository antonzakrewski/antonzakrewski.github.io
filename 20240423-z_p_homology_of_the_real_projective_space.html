Z/p homology of the real projective space

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n\) be the real projective space and \(p\) an odd prime. Then for singular homology with coefficients \(\mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}\) is given by

\begin{align*} H_k( \mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}) \simeq \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n = k, n \text{ is odd} \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

2. Proof

using universal coefficient formula for singular homology

2.1. \(k = 0\)

follows from zeroth homology with coefficients

2.2. \(k > n + 1\)

follows from singular homology with coefficients and two zeros

2.3. \(k = n +1\)

Then \(H_k(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n) = 0\) and

\begin{align*} H_{k-1}(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n) = \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } n \text{odd} \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}

hecne flat and by tor functor for a flat module it follows that

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & 0 \arrow[]{r}{}0& 0 H_k(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{}& 0 \arrow[]{r}{}& 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2.4. \(k = n\)

2.4.1. \(n\) even

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & 0 \otimes \mathbb{Z} \arrow[]{r}{} & H_k(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{}& \mathrm{Tor}_1(\mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2.4.2. \(n\) odd

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z} \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z} \otimes \mathbb{Z} \arrow[]{r}{} & H_k(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{}& \mathrm{Tor}_1(0, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2.5. \(0 < k < n\)

2.5.1. \(k\) even

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z} \otimes 0 \arrow[]{r}{} & H_k(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{}& \mathrm{Tor}_1(\mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}

2.5.2. \(k\) odd

\begin{displaymath} \begin{tikzcd} 0 \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z} \otimes \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z} \arrow[]{r}{} & H_k(\mathbb{R}\mathbb{P}^n, \mathbb{Z}/p \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{}& \mathrm{Tor}_1(0, \mathbb{Z}/2 \mathbb{Z}) \arrow[]{r}{\simeq} & 0 \end{tikzcd} \end{displaymath}
