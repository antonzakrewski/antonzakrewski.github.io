discriminant and multiple roots

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be an Integral domain, \(f \in R[X]\) a polynomial and

\begin{align*} \mathrm{disc}(f) \end{align*}

the discriminant

TFAE:

  1. \(f = 0\)
  2. there exists an \(\alpha \in L\) for some algebraic closure \(L\) of the Quotient ring \(Q(R)\), such that \(\alpha\) is a multiple root
