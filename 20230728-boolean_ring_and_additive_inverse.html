boolean ring and additive inverse

1. Proposition

Let \(R\) be a Boolean ring and \(r \in R\). Then

\begin{align*} r = -r \end{align*}

2. Proof

\begin{align*} (r + r)^2 - (r + r) = 0 \\ r^2 + r^2 + 2r - (r + r) =& 0 \\ 2r =& 0 && \vert -r \\ r = -r \end{align*}
