singular homology of the quaternionic projective space

1. Proposition

Let \(\mathbb{H}\mathbb{P}^n\) be the quaternionic projective space Then the singular homology is given by

\begin{align*} H_k(\mathbb{H}\mathbb{P}^n) = \begin{cases} \mathbb{Z} & \mbox{if } k \equiv \mod{4}, 0 \leq \frac{k}{4} \leq n \\ 0 & \mbox{else } \\ \end{cases} \end{align*}
