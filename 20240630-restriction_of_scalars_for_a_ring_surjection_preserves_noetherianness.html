restriction of scalars for a ring surjection preserves noetherian modules

1. Proposition

Let \(A,B\) be rings, \(\varphi: A \twoheadrightarrow B\) a surjective ring-homomorphism Then the restriction of scalars functor preserves Noetherian modules

2. Proof

follows from:

  • bijection between submodules for restriction of scalars for a surjective ring homomorphism
