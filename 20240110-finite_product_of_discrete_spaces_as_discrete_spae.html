finite product of discrete spaces as discrete space

1. Proposition

Let \(X^{\delta}, Y^{\delta}\) be discrete topological spaces. Then the product topology

\begin{align*} X^{\delta} \times Y^{\delta} = (X \times Y)^{\delta} \end{align*}

2. Proof

Let \((x,y) \in X \times Y\), then \(\{x\} \times \{y\} \subseteq X \times Y\) is open by construction as product of open sets.
