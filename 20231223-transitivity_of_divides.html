transitivity of divides

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a ring Then the relation

\begin{align*} aRb :\Leftrightarrow a \mid b \end{align*}

is transitive

2. Proof

Suppose there exists \(a,b,c \in A\) such that

\begin{align*} a \mid& b \\ b \mid& c \end{align*}

then there exists \(r_1,r_2 \in A\) such that

\begin{align*} a \cdot r_1 =& b \\ b \cdot r_2 =& c \end{align*}

Hence

\begin{align*} a \cdot r_1 \cdot r_2 =& b \cdot r_2 \\ =& c \end{align*}

and therefore

\begin{align*} a \mid c \end{align*}
