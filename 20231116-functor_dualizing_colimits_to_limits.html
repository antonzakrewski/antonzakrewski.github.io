functor dualizing colimits to limits

1. Definition

Let \(\mathcal{C}, \mathcal{D}\) be categories and \(\mathcal{F}: \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{D}\) be a contravariant functor. Then \(\mathcal{F}\) is said to be dualizing colimits to limits, if the covariant version

\begin{align*} \mathcal{F}': \mathcal{C}^{\mathrm{op}} \rightarrow \mathcal{D} \end{align*}

preserves colimits
