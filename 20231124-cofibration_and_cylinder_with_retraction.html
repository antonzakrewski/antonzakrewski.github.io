cofibration and cylinder with retraction

1. Proposition

Let \(A,X\) be topological spaces and \(\iota: A \rightarrow X\) a continuous map.

TFAE:

  1. there exists a retraction \(X \times [0,1] \rightarrow \mathrm{Cyl}(i)\)
  2. \(\iota\) is a cofibration

2. Proof

follows from the definition of the mapping cylinder as

\begin{align*} A \times [0,1] \coprod_{A \times \{0\}} X \times \{0\} \end{align*}
