correspondence theorem for maximal ideals and quotient ring

1. Proposition

Let \(A\) be a commutative ring, \(\mathfrak{a} \subseteq A\) an ideal and \(A/\mathfrak{a}\) the quotient ring. Then the map

\begin{align*} \pi_{\mathfrak{a}}^{-1}: \{\overline{\mathfrak{m}} \subseteq A \vert \overline{\mathfrak{m}} \text{ maximal ideal such that } \mathfrak{a} \subseteq \overline{\mathfrak{m}}\} \rightarrow \{\mathfrak{m} \subseteq A/\mathfrak{a} \vert \mathfrak{m} \text{ maximal ideal}\} \end{align*}

is an isomorphism between posets (see: Category Pos)

2. Proof

corollary of correspondence theorem for ideals and quotient rings since the correspondence is order preserving
